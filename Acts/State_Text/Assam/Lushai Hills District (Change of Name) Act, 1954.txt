Lushai Hills District (Change of Name) Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> State(s): Assam An Act to change the name of the Lushai Hills District. BE it enacted by Parliament as follows:--
Lushai Hills District (Change of Name) Act, 1954_Section 1--> State(s): Assam (1) This Act may be called the Lushai Hills District (Change of Name) Act, 1954. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Lushai Hills District (Change of Name) Act, 1954_Section 2--> State(s): Assam The tribal area in Assam now known as the Lushai Hills District shall, as from the commencement of this Act, be known as the Mizo District.
Lushai Hills District (Change of Name) Act, 1954_Section 3--> State(s): Assam In the Sixth Schedule to the Constitution , in paragraph 20,- (a) after sub-paragraph (2), the following sub-paragraph shall be inserted namely:- "(2A) The Mizo District shall comprise the area which at the commencement of this Constitution was known as the Lushai Hills District."; (b) in sub-paragraph (3), after the words "United Khasi Jaintia Hills District" the words "and the Mizo District" shall be inserted; and (c) in Part A of the table, for the words "The Lushai Hills District", the words "The Mizo District" shall be substituted.
Lushai Hills District (Change of Name) Act, 1954_Section 4--> State(s): Assam - Any reference to the Lushai Hills District in any law, instrument or other document shall, unless the context otherwise requires, be construed as a reference to the Mizo District.
