Assam Criminal Law Amendment (Supplementary) Act, 1934_Section Preamble--> State(s): Assam 
Assam Criminal Law Amendment (Supplementary) Act, 1934_Section 1--> State(s): Assam This Act may be called the Assam Criminal Law Amendment (Supplementary) Act, 1934.
Assam Criminal Law Amendment (Supplementary) Act, 1934_Section 2--> State(s): Assam Any person convicted on a trial held by Commissioner under the Assam Criminal Law Amendment Act, 1934 ( Assam 3 of 1934), may appeal to the High Court of Judicature at Fort William in Bengal,and such appeal shall be disposed of by the said High Court in the manner provided in Chapter XXXI of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898 (5 of 1898). (2) When the said Commissioners pass a sentence of death,the record of the proceedings before them shall be submitted to the said High Court, and the sentence shall not be executed unless it is con- firmed by the High Court which shall exercise in respect of such proceedings all the powers conferred on the High Court by Chapter XXVII of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898 (5 of 1898).
Assam Criminal Law Amendment (Supplementary) Act, 1934_Section 3--> State(s): Assam The powers conferred by section 491 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898 (5 of 1898), shall not be exercised in respect of any person arrested, committed to or detained in custody under the Assam Criminal Law Amendment Act, 1934 (Assam 3 of 1934).
Assam Criminal Law Amendment (Supplementary) Act, 1934_Section 4--> State(s): Assam section 2 and section 3 shall have effect from the commencement of the Assam Criminal Law Amendment Act, 1934 (Assam 3 of 1934).
