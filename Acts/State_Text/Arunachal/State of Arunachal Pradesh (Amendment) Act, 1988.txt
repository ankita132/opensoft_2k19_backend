State of Arunachal Pradesh (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> State(s): Arunachal Pradesh An Act further to amend the State of Arunachal Pradesh Act, 1986 BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows--
State of Arunachal Pradesh (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 1--> State(s): Arunachal Pradesh This Act may be called the State of Arunachal Pradesh (Amendment) Act, 1988.
State of Arunachal Pradesh (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 2--> State(s): Arunachal Pradesh In the State of Arunachal Pradesh Act, 1986 (69 of 1986.), for section 10, the following section shall be substituted, namely :-- "10.Provisions as to Legislative Assembly.- The total number of seats in the Legislative Assembly of the State of Arunachal Pradesh, to be filled by persons chosen by direct election from assembly constituencies shall be sixty, out of which fifty-nine seats shall be reserved for the Scheduled Tribes; and the provisions of the Representation of the People Act, 1950 (43 of 1950.) shall be deemed to be amended accordingly.".
