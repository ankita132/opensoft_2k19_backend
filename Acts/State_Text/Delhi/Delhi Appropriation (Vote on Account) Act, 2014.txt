Delhi Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act to provide for the withdrawal of certain sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi for the services for a period of six months of the financial year 2014-15. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Delhi Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi This Act may be called the Delhi Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014.
Delhi Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi there may be withdrawn sums not exceeding those specified in column 3 of the Schedule amounting in the aggregate to the sum of eighteen thousand and thirty-three crores of rupees towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year 2014-15 in respect of the services specified in column 2 of the Schedule.
Delhi Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section 3--> State(s): Delhi The sums authorised to be withdrawn from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in (he Schedule in relation to the said year.
