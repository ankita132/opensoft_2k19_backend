Delhi Appropriation Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act to authorise payment and appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi for the services in respect of the financial year 2013-14. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Delhi Appropriation Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi This Act may be called the Delhi Appropriation Act, 2014.
Delhi Appropriation Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi, there may be paid and applied further sums not exceeding those specified in column 3 of the Schedule, amounting in the aggregate to the sum of three hundred sixty-three crores five lakh and sixty-four thousand rupees towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year 2013-14, in respect of the services specified in column 2 of the Schedule.
Delhi Appropriation Act, 2014_Section 3--> State(s): Delhi The sums authorised to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
