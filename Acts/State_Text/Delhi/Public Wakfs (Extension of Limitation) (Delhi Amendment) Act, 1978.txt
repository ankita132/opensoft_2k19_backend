Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act further to amend the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 as in force in the Union territory of Delhi. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi (1) This Act may be called the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1978. (2) It extends to the whole of the Union territory of Delhi. (3) It shall be deemed to have effect from the 1st day of January, 1976.
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi In section 3 of the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 , as in force in the Union territory of Delhi, for the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1975", the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1980" shall be substituted.
