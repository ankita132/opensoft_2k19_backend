Delhi High Court (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act further to amend the Delhi High Court Act, 1966. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Delhi High Court (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi (1) This Act may be called the Delhi High Court (Amendment) Act, 2015.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the President on the 11th August, 2015
Delhi High Court (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi In sub-section (2) of section 5 of the Delhi High Court Act, 1966, for the words ‘‘rupees twenty lakhs’’, the words ‘‘rupees two crore’’ shall be substituted.
Delhi High Court (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Delhi In the Punjab Courts Act, 1918, as in force in the National Capital Territory of Delhi, in section 25, for the words ‘‘rupees twenty lakhs’’, the words ‘‘rupees two crore’’ shall be substituted.
Delhi High Court (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 4--> State(s): Delhi The Chief Justice of the High Court of Delhi may transfer any suit or other proceedings which is or are pending in the High Court immediately before the commencement of this Act to such subordinate court in the National Capital Territory of Delhi as would have jurisdiction to entertain such suit or proceedings had such suit or proceedings been instituted or filed for the first time after such commencement. (Dr. Sanjay Singh), Secretary to the Govt. of India.
