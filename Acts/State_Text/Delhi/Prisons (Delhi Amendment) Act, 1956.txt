Prisons (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act to amend the Prisons Act, 1894, in its application to the State of Delhi. BE it enacted by the Delhi State Legislative Assembly in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Prisons (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi (1) This Act may be called the Prisons (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1956. (2) It shall come into force on such 1 date as the Chief Commissioner may, by notification in the official Gazette, appoint. 15th August, 1956, vide Notification No. F. 4(96)/55-GA & R, dated July, 1956, see Delhi State Gazette, Part V, dated 19th July, 1956.
Prisons (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi In section 46 of the Prisons Act, 1894 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act)- (i) sub-section (12) shall be omitted; (ii) in the proviso the words "or to whipping" shall be omitted.
Prisons (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 3--> State(s): Delhi In sub-section (1) of section 47 of the principal Act, clause (4) shall be omitted.
Prisons (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 4--> State(s): Delhi In sub-section (1) of section 50 of the principal Act, the words "or of whipping" shall be omitted.
Prisons (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 5--> State(s): Delhi In sub-section (2) of section 51 of the principal Act, the words "and, in the case of offences for which whipping is awarded, the Superintendent shall record the substance of the evidence of the witnesses, the defence of the prisoner, and the finding with the reasons therefor" shall be omitted.
Prisons (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 6--> State(s): Delhi Section 53 of the principal Act is hereby repealed.
