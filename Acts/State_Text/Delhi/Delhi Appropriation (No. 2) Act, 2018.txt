Delhi Appropriation (No. 2) Act, 2018_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act to authorize payment and appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of National Capital Territory of Delhi for the services in respect of the Financial Year 2018-19 Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the National Capital Territory of Delhi in the Sixty Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Delhi Appropriation (No. 2) Act, 2018_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi This Act may be called the Delhi Appropriation (No.2) Act, 2018.
Delhi Appropriation (No. 2) Act, 2018_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi, there may be paid and applied further sums not exceeding those specified in column (5) of the Schedule, amounting in the aggregate to the sum of Rupees Fifty Three Thousand crore only towards defraying the several charges which will come in the course of payment during the financial year 2018-2019 in respect of the services specified in column (2) of the Schedule.
Delhi Appropriation (No. 2) Act, 2018_Section 3--> State(s): Delhi The sums authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi by this Act, shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
