Punjab Pre-Emption (Chandigarh and Delhi Repeal) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi, Chandigarh An Act to repeal the Punjab Pre-emption Act, 1913, as in force in the Union territories of Chandigarh and Delhi. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab Pre-Emption (Chandigarh and Delhi Repeal) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi, Chandigarh This Act may be called the Punjab Pre-emption (Chandigarh and Delhi Repeal) Act, 1989.
Punjab Pre-Emption (Chandigarh and Delhi Repeal) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi, Chandigarh The Punjab Pre-emption Act, 1913 , as in force in the Union territories of Chandigarh and Delhi, is hereby repealed.
Punjab Pre-Emption (Chandigarh and Delhi Repeal) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Delhi, Chandigarh No court in the Union territories of Chandigarh and Delhi shall, after the commencement of this Act, pass a decree in any suit for pre-emption.
