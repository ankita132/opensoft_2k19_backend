Delhi Appropriation (No.1) Bill, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi A Bill to authorise payment and appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi for the services in respect of the financial year 2016-17. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the National Capital Territory of Delhi in the Sixty seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Delhi Appropriation (No.1) Bill, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi This Act may be called the Delhi Appropriation (No.1) Act, 2017.
Delhi Appropriation (No.1) Bill, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi, there may be paid and applied further sums not exceeding those specified in column (5) of the Schedule, amounting in the aggregate to the sum of rupees Six hundred Eighty Eight crore Seventy Three Lakh Twenty Five Thousand only towards defraying the several charges which will come in the course of payment during the financial year 2016-2017 in respect of the services specified in column (2) of the Schedule.
Delhi Appropriation (No.1) Bill, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Delhi The sums authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the National Capital Territory of Delhi by this Act, shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
