Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act further to amend the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty third Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi (1) This Act may be called the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1972. (2) It extends to the whole of the Union territory of Delhi. (3) It shall be deemed to have effect from the 1st day of January, 1971.
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi In section 3 of the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 (29 of 1959) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), as in force in the Union territory of Delhi, for the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1970", the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1972" shall be substituted.
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 3--> State(s): Delhi (1) The Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Delhi Amendment) Ordinance, 1972, (3 of 1972) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the Principal Act is amended by this Act.
