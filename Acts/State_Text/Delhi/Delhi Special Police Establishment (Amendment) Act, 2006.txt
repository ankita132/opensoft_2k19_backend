Delhi Special Police Establishment (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act further to amend the Delhi Special Police Establishment Act, 1946. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-seventh year of the Republic of India as follows -
Delhi Special Police Establishment (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi This Act may be called the Delhi Special Police Establishment (Amendment) Act, 2006.
Delhi Special Police Establishment (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi In Section 4-A of the Delhi Special Police Establishment Act, 1946 (25 of 1946), in sub-section (1), for clause (d), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(d) the Secretary to the Government of India in charge of the Member". Ministry or Department of the Central Government having Administrative Control of the Delhi Special Police Establishment.
