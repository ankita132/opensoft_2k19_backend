Delhi (Control of Building Operations) Continuance Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> State(s): Delhi An Act to continue the Delhi (Control of Building Operations) Act, 1955, for a further period. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Delhi (Control of Building Operations) Continuance Act, 1956_Section 1--> State(s): Delhi This Act may be called the Delhi (Control of Building Operations) Continuance Act, 1956.
Delhi (Control of Building Operations) Continuance Act, 1956_Section 2--> State(s): Delhi In sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Delhi (Control of Building Operations) Act, 1955 (53 OF 1955), for the figures, letters and words '1st day of January, 1957', the figures, letters and words '1st day of January, 1958' shall be substituted.
