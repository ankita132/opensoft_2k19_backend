Bihar Legislature (Removal of Disqualifications) Act, 1950_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to provide that holders of certain offices shall not be disqualified for being chosen as, or for being, members of the Bihar Legislative Assembly or the Bihar Legislative Council. WHEREAS it is expedient to provide that holders of certain offices shall not be disqualified for being chosen as, or for being, members of the Bihar Legislative Assembly or the Bihar Legislative Council. It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Bihar Legislature (Removal of Disqualifications) Act, 1950_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar This Act may be called the Bihar Legislature (Removal of Disqualifications) Act, 1950.
Bihar Legislature (Removal of Disqualifications) Act, 1950_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar A person shall not be [Fn. 1] [and shall be deemed never to have been] disqualified for being chosen as, or for being, a member of the Bihar Legislative Assembly or the Bihar Legislative Council by reason only of the fact that he holds any of the offices, being offices of profit, mentioned in the Schedule. FOOTNOTES : 1. Inserted by the Bihar Legislature (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Act, 1956 (Bihar Act 33 of 1956), Section 2.
Bihar Legislature (Removal of Disqualifications) Act, 1950_Section 3--> State(s): Bihar The Bihar Legislature (Removal of Disqualifications) Ordinance, 1950 (Bihar Ordinance No. 1 of 1950) is hereby repealed.

(4) [Fn. 3] [Any office in the National Cadet Corps constituted under the National Cadet Corps Act, 1948 (31 of 1948).] (5) [Fn. 4] [The office of the Chairman or member of any Committee or body appointed by the Central or a State Government or by a servant of the Government: Provided that the Chairman or any member of such Committee or body does not receive any remuneration other than compensatory allowance.] Explanation - [Fn. 5] [For the purpose of this item "Compensatory allowance" shall mean (i) the travelling allowance, daily allowance, or any allowance in the shape of honorarium, which may be paid to the holder of the office for the purpose of reimbursing the personal expenditure incurred by him in attending the meetings of the committee or body or performing any other functions as the holder of the said office; and (ii) the allotment of quarter and the provision for conveyance, and such other facilities, privileges and amenities to the holder of the office or cash payment in lieu thereof as the State Government, the Board or the managing authority of the committee or the body, as the case may be, may, by order made in this behalf, on such terms and conditions as may be mentioned therein, from time to time, determine.] (6) [Fn. 6] [Any office under an insurer the management of whose controlled business has vested in the Central Government. (7) The office of an agent or other like office under the Central Government or the State Government for the purpose of effecting sales of, or collecting subscriptions towards, National Plan Certificates for such Commission as the Central Government may have fixed in that behalf or without such commission.
