Bihar Land Reforms (Validation) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to validate certain actions taken under the Bihar Land Reforms Act, 1950. In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Bihar State Legislature (Delegation of Powers) Act, 1968 (39 of 1968), the President is pleased to enact as follows:-
Bihar Land Reforms (Validation) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar This Act may be called the Bihar Land Reforms (Validation) Act, 1969.
Bihar Land Reforms (Validation) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar Notwithstanding anything contained in Sections 3, 3A and 3B of the Bihar Land Reforms Act, 1950 (Bihar Act XXX of 1950), or notifications issued, all actions taken or all things done in respect of the vesting of the estates or tenures in the State during the period commencing from the 25th day of September, 1950 and ending with the commencement of the Bihar Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1953 (Bihar Act XX of 1954), shall always be deemed to have been validly issued, taken or done and shall not be called in question merely on the ground of non-compliance or irregular compliance of the provisions of the said Section except the provision of sub-section (1) of Section 3 and sub-section (1) of Section 3A of the said Act.
