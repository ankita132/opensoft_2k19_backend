Bihar Delegated Legislation Provisions Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act To amend certain Acts to provide for laying of rules and other delegated legislations, before the Houses of the State Legislature. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Bihar in the Sixty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Bihar Delegated Legislation Provisions Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar (1) This Act may be called the Bihar Delegated Legislation Provisions Act, 2013. (2) It shall extend to the whole of the State of Bihar. (3) It shall come into force at once.
Bihar Delegated Legislation Provisions Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar The enactments specified in the schedule of this Act are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner mentioned in the third column thereof.
Bihar Delegated Legislation Provisions Act, 2013_Section Sch.--> State(s): Bihar (See Section 2) 1. Bihar Co-operative Society Act 1935 (Bihar & Orissa Act 6, 1935) -The following new sub-section (5) shall be added after sub section(4) of section 66 of the said act 1935- "(5) Every Rule made by the State Government under this Act shall be laid, as soon as may be after it is made, before each House of the State Legislature, while it is in session, for a total period of fourteen days which may be comprised in one session or in two or more successive sessions, and if, before the expiry of the session immediately following the session or the successive sessions aforesaid, both Houses agree in making any modification in the rule or both Houses agree that the rule should not be made, the rule shall thereafter have effect only in such modified form or be of no effect, as the case may be; however, that any such modification or annulment shall be without prejudice to the validity of anything previously done under that rule." 2.
