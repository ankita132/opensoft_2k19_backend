Bihar College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to Amend the Bihar College Service Commission Act, 1976. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Bihar in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Bihar College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar (1) The Act may be called the Bihar College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1996. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Bihar College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar In the College Service Commission Act, 1976 (Bihar Act 26 of 1976) hrereinafter referred to as the said Act,- Sub-section (11) of Section 2 shall be deleted.
Bihar College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Bihar (1) The Bihar College Service Commission (Amendment) Second Ordinance, 1996 (Bihar Ordinance No. 3, 1996) is hereby repealed. (2) Nothing in this Ordinance or the amendment effected thereby shall affect or be deemed to affect anything done or any action taken under the provision of the said Act, prior to the commencement of the Act.
