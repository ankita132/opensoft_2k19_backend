Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to amend the Prisons Act, 1894, in its application to the State of Bihar. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Bihar in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar This Act may be called the Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 19561 . For Statement of Object and Reasons see the Bihar Gazette, Extraordinary, of the 4th May, 1956.
Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar In Section 46 of the Prisons Act, 1894 (IX of 1894) (hereinafter referred to as the said Act), - (i) clause (12) shall be omitted: and (ii) in the proviso, the words "or to whipping" shall be omitted.
Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 3--> State(s): Bihar In Section 47 of the said Act' clause (4) shall be omitted.
Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 4--> State(s): Bihar In sub-section (1) of Section 50 of the said Act, words "or of whipping" shall be omitted.
Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 5--> State(s): Bihar In sub-section (2) of Section 51 of the said Act, the words "and, in the case of the offence for which whipping is awarded, the Superintendent shall record the substance of the evidence of the witnesses, the defence of the prisoner, and the finding with the reasons there for" shall be omitted.
Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 6--> State(s): Bihar Section 53 of the said Act shall be omitted.
Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 7--> State(s): Bihar In sub-section (1) of Section 57 of the said Act, for the word "transportation", the word "imprisonment for life" shall be substituted.
Prisons (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 8--> State(s): Bihar In Section 50 of the said Act, - (i) in clause (16), for the word "transportation", the words "imprisonment for life" shall be substituted; and (ii) in clause (22), the words "transportation or" shall be omitted.
