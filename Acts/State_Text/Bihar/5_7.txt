Bihar Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2008_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to make amendment and validation in Bihar Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 1993 (Bihar Act, 16 of 1993). Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Bihar in the Fifty-ninth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bihar Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2008_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar (1) This Act may be called the Bihar Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment and Validation) Act, 20081 . (2) It shall extend to the whole of the State of Bihar. (3) It shall come into force with effect from 29th August, 2006. Published in Bihar Gazette (Extra ordinary) dated 15.4.2008.
Bihar Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2008_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar Incorporated in the text of the Act.
Bihar Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2008_Section 3--> State(s): Bihar It is hereby declared that no act, or omission on the part of any person shall be publishable as an offence, which would not have been so publishable if this amendment had not come into force.
