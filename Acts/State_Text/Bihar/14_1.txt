Bihar (Non-Trading) Companies Act, 1959_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act relating companies which are non-trading corporations Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Bihar in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bihar (Non-Trading) Companies Act, 1959_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar (1) This Act may be called the Bihar (Non-Trading) Companies Act, 1959. (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Bihar. (3) This Act shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April, 1956.
Bihar (Non-Trading) Companies Act, 1959_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar The powers conferred by the Companies Act, 1956 (1 of 1956), on the Central Government shall, in relation to companies with objects confined to the State of Bihar which are not trading corporations within the meaning of entries 43 and 44 in List I in the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India, be exercised by the State Government and the provisions of the said Act shall, as far as may be, apply to such companies.
