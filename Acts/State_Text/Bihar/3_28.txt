Court-Fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to Amend the Court-Fees Act, 1870 in its Application to the State of Bihar. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Bihar in the sixty one year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Court-Fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar (1) This Act may be called the Court-Fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 20101 . (2) It shall extend to the whole of the State of Bihar. (3) It shall come into force at once. Published in Bihar Gazette (Ex. ord.) No. 269, dated 16 April, 2010.
Court-Fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar Any fee payable under Schedule-I and Schedule-II of the Court-Fees Act, 1870 calculated in paise shall be rounded off in rupee.
