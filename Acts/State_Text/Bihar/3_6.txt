Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to amend the Bihar Co-operative Societies Act, 1935 Whereas, it is priority of the Government to conduct the election of the Managing Committee of co-operative societies registered under this Act, by Bihar State Election Authority, constituted under Bihar State Election Authority Act, 2008, so that it may be made more democratically well governed and the conduct of the election process, be more transparent; And Whereas, it is necessary to bring out certain amendment to the Bihar Co-operative Societies Act, 1935; Be it enacted by the legislature of the State of Bihar in the sixty second year of Republic of India as follows: -
Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar (1) This Act may be called the Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 20111 . (2) It shall extend to the whole of the State of Bihar. (3) This Amendment shall be deemed to have come into effect on the date of Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2008 (Bihar Act 18, of 2008) came into effect. Published in Bihar Gazette (Extra ordinary) No. 57, dated 10th February, 2012.
Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar The sub-section (1) of Section 14A of the said Act shall be substituted by the following: - "(1) Notwithstanding, anything contained in any provision of this Act, Rules made thereunder and bye-laws of a registered society, the election to the Managing Committee of co-operative societies registered under this Act shall be conducted by Bihar State Election Authority constituted under Bihar State Election Authority Act, 2008 in the manner prescribed for the conduct of election under Bihar State Election Authority Act, 2008 and Rules made thereunder read with Bihar Co-operative Societies Act, 1935 and Rules made thereunder: Provided that all the previous elections of registered co-operative societies conducted by the Competent Authority under the existing provisions would be saved: Provided, further that any action, order, decision to hold the election of Managing Committee of co-operative societies shall be deemed to have been made under this Amendment and any election so held shall be valid, notwithstanding any judgment, order or decree of any court."
Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Bihar The sub-section (2) of Section 14A of the said Act shall be substituted by the following: - "(2) Notwithstanding, anything contained in any provisions of this Act, Rules made thereunder and bye-laws of a registered society, the election to the Managing Committee of co-operative societies registered under this Act shall be held in terms of the provision of this Section, even if the process of election has commenced but the result of such election has not been declared."
Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 4--> State(s): Bihar The sub-section (3) of Section 14A of the said Act shall be substituted by the following: - "(3) Notwithstanding, anything contained in any provision of this Act, Rules made thereunder and bye-laws of a registered society, the election to the Managing Committee of co-operative societies registered under this Act shall be held before the expiry of the term of Managing Committee as prescribed in sub-section (9) of Section 14 of this Act or within period of nine months from the order of supersession of Managing Committee passed under the provisions of this Act: Provided that in the case of supersession of Managing Committee of any society as on the date of notification of this Amendment, the election to the Managing Committee shall be held within nine months."
Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 5--> State(s): Bihar The sub-section (4) of Section 14A of the said Act is hereby deleted.
Bihar Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 6--> State(s): Bihar The sub-section (5) of Section 14A of the said Act is hereby deleted.
