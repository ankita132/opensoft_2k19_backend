Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment (Extension to Bihar) Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to extend the Provisions of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960, to the State of Bihar. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Bihar in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment (Extension to Bihar) Act, 1960_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar (1) This Act may be called the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment (Extension to Bihar) Act, 19601 . (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Bihar. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th day of October, 1960. [Note. - This Act, has been repealed by Bihar Repeal of Laws (which are no longer needed or relevant) Act, 2006 (Bihar Act, 14 of 2006), being published here only for reference and record. Ed.]
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment (Extension to Bihar) Act, 1960_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar The amendments made to the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1951 by the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960 shall, in so far as they relate to any matter enumerated in List II of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution, be as valid in the State of Bihar as if the provisions contained therein had been enacted by the Legislature of the State.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment (Extension to Bihar) Act, 1960_Section 3--> State(s): Bihar The Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment (Extension to Bihar) Ordinance, 1960 is hereby repealed.
