Court-fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Bihar An Act to amend the Court-fees Act, 1870 in its application to the State of Bihar. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Bihar in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Court-fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Bihar (1) This Act may be called the Court-fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 19581 . (2) It shall come into force on the 1st day of April, 1958. Published in the Bihar Gazette, Extraordinary, of the 31st March, 1958.
Court-fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Bihar For the 'proper fee' set forth in Schedule I to the Court-fees Act, 1870 (VII of 1870), as amended from time to time in its application to the State of Bihar (hereinafter referred to as the said Act), for Articles 1 and 6 and shown in the second column of the Schedule A to this Act, the 'proper fees' shown against them in the third column of the said Schedule A shall be substituted.
Court-fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Bihar For the 'proper fees' set forth in the table of rates annexed to Schedule to the said Act and shown in the first column of Schedule B to this Act, the 'proper fees' shown in the second column of the Schedule B shall be substituted.
Court-fees (Bihar Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 4--> State(s): Bihar For the 'proper fee' set forth in Schedule II to the said Act for Article 1 and shown in the second column of Schedule C to this Act, the 'proper fee, shown in the third column of the said Schedule C shall be substituted.
