Chandigarh Disturbed Areas (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chandigarh An Act to amend the Chandigarh Disturbed Areas Act, 1983. BE enacted by Parliament in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Chandigarh Disturbed Areas (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Chandigarh (1) This Act may be called the Chandigarh Disturbed Areas (Amendment) Act, 1989. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Chandigarh Disturbed Areas (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Chandigarh In section 6 of the Chandigarh Disturbed Areas Act, 1983 (33 of 1983), for the words 'the Administrator", the words "the Central Government" shall be substituted.
