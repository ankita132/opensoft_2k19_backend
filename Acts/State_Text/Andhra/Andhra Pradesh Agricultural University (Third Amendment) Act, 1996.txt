Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Third Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University Act, 1963. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Third Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Third Amendment) Act, 19961 . (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 19th October, 1996
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Third Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University Act, 1963 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in section 2, in clause (n) for the words 'includes a Professor", the words "includes a Professor, an Associate Professor, an Assistant professor" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Third Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh For sub-section (1) of section 32 of the principal Act, the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) The Board shall constitute a Selection Committee in regard to the appointment of teachers for each Faculty which shall consist of the following persons, namely:- (i) the Vice-Chancellor who shall be Ex-Officio Chairman of the Committee; (ii) the Dean of the Faculty concerned; and (iii) three experts in the case of selection of Professors, two experts in the case of selection of Associate Professors and one expert in the case of selection of Assistant Professors, the experts being from outside the University, having special knowledge of the subject of the Department in the Faculty. The experts are to be nominated by the Board.". (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
