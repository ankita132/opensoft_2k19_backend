Andhra Pradesh Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot Leggers, Dacoits, Drug-Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Land Grabbers (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to amend the Andhra Pradesh Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot Leggers, Dacoits, Drug Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Land Grabbers Act,1986. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot Leggers, Dacoits, Drug-Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Land Grabbers (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot Leggers, Dacoits, Drug-Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Land Grabbers (Amendment) Act, 1986.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 6th August, 1986
Andhra Pradesh Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot Leggers, Dacoits, Drug-Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Land Grabbers (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot Leggers, Dacoits, Drug Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Land Grabbers' Act, 1986, in section 2, in clause (f)- (i) for the expression "the Dangerous Drugs Act, 1930", the expression "the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985" shall be substituted ; (ii) in the marginal heading for the expression "Central Act II of 1930" the expression "Central Act 61 of 1985" shall be substituted.
