Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) Act, 1975. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) (Second Amendment) Act, 1996.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 3rd February 1996. The above Act of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly received the assent of the Governor on the 26-8-1996 and the said assent is hereby first pub. in the A P. Gaz., for general information in A.P. Gaz., Part IV-B (Ext.), dated 26-8-1996.
Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) Act, 1975, in Section 28, in sub-section (2), for the first proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely:- "Provided that the development charges may be levied at different rates for different institutions of use as may be prescribed from time to time to which the land, or as the case may be the building, is used."
Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1996 is hereby repealed.
