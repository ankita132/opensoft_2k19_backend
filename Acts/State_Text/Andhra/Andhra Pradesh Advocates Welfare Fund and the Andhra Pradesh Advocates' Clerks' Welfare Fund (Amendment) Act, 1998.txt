Andhra Pradesh Advocates Welfare Fund and the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Clerks' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Welfare Fund Act, 1987 and Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Clerks Welfare Fund Act, 1992. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-ninth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Advocates Welfare Fund and the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Clerks' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Advocates Welfare Fund and the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Clerks' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 14th May, 1998
Andhra Pradesh Advocates Welfare Fund and the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Clerks' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Welfare Fund Act, 1987,- (1) in section 4, in sub-section (3) to clause (h), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that the Secretary shall be paid such sum as honorarium from the Advocates' Welfare Fund as the Committee may determine from time to time, for the services rendered by him" (2) in section 12-A for the expression "value of rupees eight out of which a sum of rupees six shall be credited to the Andhra Pradesh Advocates, "Welfare Fund and rupees two", the expression "value of rupees twelve out of which a sum of rupees nine shall be credited to the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Welfare Fund and rupees three" shall be substituted (3) in sub-section (1) of section 15, for the words "every advocate, the words "Every Advocate below the age of 65 years shall be substituted. (4) after section 15, the following section shall be inserted namely;- "15A. Retired person is not eligible for membership:- A person retired from the service of Central Government, State Government or from any Corporation incorporated under the provisions of Indian Companies Act, 1956 and drawing pension and practicing as an advocate shall not be admitted to the membership of the Fund.". (5) in section 16,- (i) in sub-section (1) for the words "on cessation of practice", the words "on cessation of practice or suspension of practice." shall be substituted; (ii) in the marginal title to the said section, after the word "cessation" the words "or suspension" shall be inserted; (iii) sub-section (5) shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Advocates Welfare Fund and the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Clerks' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Advocates Clerks' Welfare Fund Act, 1992, in section 12-A for the expression "value of rupees-eight out of which a sum of rupees two shall be credited to the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Clerks' Welfare Fund and rupees six shall be credited to the Andhra Pradesh Advocates, Welfare Fund", the expression "value of rupees twelve of which a sum of rupees three shall be credited to the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Clerks, Welfare Fund and rupees nine shall be credited to the Andhra Pradesh Advocates' Welfare Fund" shall be substituted. (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
