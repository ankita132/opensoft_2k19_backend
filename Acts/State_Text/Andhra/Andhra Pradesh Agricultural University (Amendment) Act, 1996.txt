Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University Act, 1963. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 19961 . (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Government may, by notification, in the Andhra-Pradesh Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 19th October, 1996
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University Act, 1963, in section 19,- (i) In sub-section (2),- (a) after item (d), the following item shall be inserted namely;- "(dd) the Secretary to Government Finance Department."; (b) in item (i), for the words "two persons "four persons" shall be substituted; (c) after item (i), the following item shall be inserted, namely:- "(k) two persons nominated by the chancellor from among the Agro Industrialists and other entrepreneurs including self employed graduate;" (ii) in sub-section (a), after the proviso the following proviso shall added namely:- "Provided further" this even if some of the members are nominated in different times to the Board under item (a) to (k) of sub-section (2), of three years in their case shall be reckoned from the done on which the notification constituting the Board be first issued.".
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh On and from the commencement of the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1996, all the members nominated by the Chancellor in the year, 1993 under items (h), (i) and (1) of sub-section (2) of section 19 shall cease to hold such office; and the chancellor shall, thereafter fill up all such vacancies by nomination in accordance with the provisions of the Principal Act as amended by this Act. (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
