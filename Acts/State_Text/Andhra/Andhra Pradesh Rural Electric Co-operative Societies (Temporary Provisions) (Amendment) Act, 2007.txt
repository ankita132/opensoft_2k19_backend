Andhra Pradesh Rural Electric Co-operative Societies (Temporary Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Rural Electric Co-operative Societies (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1998. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Rural Electric Co-operative Societies (Temporary Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Rural Electric Co-operative Societies (Temporary Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 1st November, 1976.
Andhra Pradesh Rural Electric Co-operative Societies (Temporary Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Rural Electric Co-operative Societies (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1998, in Section 2, for the expression "31st March 2007", the expression "the 31st March, 2008", shall be substituted.
