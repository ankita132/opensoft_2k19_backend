Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1956. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 20011 . (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 22nd April, 2001
Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1956, in Schedule II, in article 10, against item (g), in column (3), for the existing rate of proper fee, the following shall be substituted, namely:- (K.G. Shankar), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice (FAC) Law Department.
