Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission. Act, 1985 Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 19951 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 8th September, 1994. Received the assent of the Governor on the 14th Feb, 1995
Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission Act, 1985 (here in after referred to as the principal Act in section 1, in sub-section (2), in clause (b) after the words Government Colleges", the words including Government Polytechnics" shall be inserted
Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 2 of the principal Act, in clause (b) for the words includes a Junior College", the words includes a Government Polytechnic and a Junior College" shall be substituted
Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 10 of the principal Act, to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that the selection to the posts of teachers/faculty positions in Government Polytechnics shall be made in accordance with the norms and standards laid down by the All India Council of Technical Education."
Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh Notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act, as amended by the Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1995, it shall be competent for the Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission to continue the process of of selection of teachers to Government Polytechnics and complete the same in respect of the vacancies already notified for recruitment prior to the commencement of this Act.
Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh College Service Commission (Amendment) Ordinance, 1994 is hereby repealed.
