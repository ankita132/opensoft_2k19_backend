Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty second Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 19911 . (2) Sections 2 and 3 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 6th December, 1990 and sections 4 and 5 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th March, 1991. Received the assent of the Governor on the 09-04-1991
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964, (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 2, classes (j-i), (j-ii), (k-i) and clause (u) shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 21-A of the principal Act, sub-section (1A) shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh Section 21C of the principal Act, shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 31 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1), in clause (a), after the second proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided also that two women members shall be nominated by the Registrar to the Committee of such class of societies and in such manner as may be prescribed from among the women members of the general body of such societies. Such nominated Women members shall, notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, have the right to vote and otherwise to take part in the proceedings of the meetings of the Committee." (ii) sub-section (5) shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1990 and the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative societies (Amendment) Ordinance, 1991 are hereby repealed.
