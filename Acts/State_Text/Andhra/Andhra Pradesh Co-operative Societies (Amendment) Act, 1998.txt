Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-Operative Societies Act, 1964. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-eighth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 19981 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 27th September, 1997. Received the assent of the Governor on the 10-01-1998
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964 in section 12A, in Sub-section (3), for the words 'for a period of one year", the words "for a period of two years" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Ordinance, 1997 is hereby repealed. (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
