Andhra Pradesh (Issuance of Integrated Registration and Furnishing of Combined Returns under Various Labour Laws by Certain Establishments) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to amend the Andhra Prdesh (Issuance of Integrated Registration and Furnishing of Combined Returns Under Various Labour Laws by Certain Establishments) Act, 2015 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:
Andhra Pradesh (Issuance of Integrated Registration and Furnishing of Combined Returns under Various Labour Laws by Certain Establishments) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh (Issuance of Integrated Registration and Furnishing of Combined Returns under Various Labour Laws by Certain Establishments) (Amendment) Act, 2015; (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 23rd April, 2015.

S. S. V. Durga Prasad) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
