Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 19931 . Received the assent of the Governor on 1st Feb 1993
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 21A of the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964 Act 7 of 1964 (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), in the proviso to sub-section (1), before clause (iii), the following clauses shall be inserted namely,- "(i) his delegation is withdrawn ; (ii) the Committee which elected him as the delegate, has been superseded;"
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh 
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 25 of the Principal Act, sub-section (4) shall be omitted
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 30 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (2),- (a) after item (viii) the following item shall be inserted, namely:- "(ix) election of delegates to the financing Bank or other societies"; (b) in the proviso, for the expression "item (viii)", the expression items (viii) and (ix)" shall be substituted.
