Andhra Pradesh Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas Act, 2001. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas (Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Andhra Pradesh. (3) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Andhra Pradesh Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas Act, 2001, in section 3, in the end of proviso of clause (c) of sub-section (1), the words "or Central Sales Tax Act, 1956" shall be inserted.
