Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970 in its Application to the State of Andhra Pradesh. Be it enacted by the Andhra Pradesh State Legislature in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2015. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970, in section 1, in sub-section (4), in clauses (a), (b) and the provisio thereunder, for the word "twenty" the word "fifty" shall be substituted. (C. S. S. V. Durga Prasad) Secretary to Government, Law Department
