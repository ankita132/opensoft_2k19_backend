Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend The Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty Fourth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2013. (2) (a) clauses (i) and (iii) of section 2 and clause (i) of section 3 shall come into force from the date of publication of the Act. (b) clause (ii) of section 2 and clauses (ii), (iii), (iv), (vi), (vii) and (viii) of section 3, shall be deemed to have come into force on the 31st January, 2013; (c) clause (iv) of section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 17th February, 2013; (d) clause (v) of section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 23rd March, 2013; (e) clause (ix) of section 3, shall be deemed to have come into force on the 28th February, 2013; (f) item 1-E and 1-F of the table under clause (i) of section 4 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th January, 2013, and the remaining provisions in section 4 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 11th January, 2013.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005 (herein after referred to as Principal Act), in Schedule-I,- (i) in entry 27, after the words "rupee note" the words "and coins" shall be inserted; (ii) in entry 34, after the words "unbranded bread", the words "and unbranded rusk" shall be added; (iii) entries 59 and 59A shall be deleted; (iv) after entry 64, the following shall be added, namely,- "65. Transfer of right to use feature films (in digital or physical form) by film producers to distributors or exhibitors".
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Principal Act, in Schedule-IV,- (i) in entry 14, after the word "Bitumen" the words "of all varieties" shall be added; (ii) in entry 28, after the word 'Vermicelli", the word "Macaroni", shall be inserted; (iii) for entry 43, the following shall be substituted, namely,- "43. All kinds of Ores and all kinds of minerals excluding Marble and Granite"; (iv) in entry 44, after the words "paper of all kinds", the expression "(excluding Wall paper)" shall be inserted; (v) in entry 101, after the words "Liquefied Petroleum Gas" the words "and Piped Natural Gas" shall be inserted; (vi) in entry 107, in sub-entry (b), after the words "fruit squash", the expression "fruit pulp", shall be inserted; (vii) in entry 124, for the words "Diesel Power Generators", the words "All kinds of Portable Power Generator Sets" shall be substituted; (viii) after entry 128, the following entry shall be added, namely,- "129. Bodies built on chassis of Motor Vehicles"; (ix) after entry 129, the following entry shall be added, namely,- "130. Plastic Moulded Furniture, purely made of Plastic and not containing any other material like Steel, Aluminium, Wood and Glass, etc.".
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Principal Act, in Schedule-VI,- (i) in the table, for Item No. 1 and the corresponding entries thereto, the following shall be substituted, namely,- "1-A Indian Made Foreign Liquor (other than Beer, Wine and Ready to drink varieties) bottled and/or packed in India as per the provisions of Andhra Pradesh Excise Act, 1968, but excluding Toddy and Arrack At the point of first sale in the State  Basic price (per case):  (a) Upto Rs. 2,500/- 190% (b) Above Rs. 2,500/- upto Rs. 3,500/- 160% (c) Above Rs. 3,500/- upto Rs. 5,000/- 140% (d) Above Rs. 5,000/- 130% 1-B Beer bottled and/or packed in India as per the provisions of the Andhra Pradesh Excise Act, 1968 At the point of first sale in the State 175% 1-C Wine and Ready to drink varieties bottled and/or packed in India as per the provisions of the Andhra Pradesh Excise Act, 1968 At the point of first sale in the State 150% 1-D Foreign Liquor bottled and/or packed outside India At the point of first sale in the State 70% 1-E Liquor sold by Military Canteens holding licences in Form CS-1 and CS-2 under the provisions of The Andhra Pradesh Excise (Grant of licence of selling by in-house and conditions of licence) Rules, 2005. At the point of first sale in the State 70% 1-F Privilege fee on all liquors, bottled and packed as per the provisions of the Andhra Pradesh Excise Act, 1968 (including imported liquor) whether bottled/packed in India or outside India but excluding toddy and arrack under the provisions of the following rules;- At the point of first sale in the State 70% (i) Rule 16(9) of the Andhra Pradesh Excise (Grant of license of selling by shop and conditions of license) Rules, 2012.  (ii) Rule 10-A of the Andhra Pradesh Excise (Grant of license of selling by bar and conditions of license) Rules, 2005.  (iii) Rule 12 of the Andhra Pradesh Excise (Grant of license of selling by in-house and conditions of license) Rules, 2005.  (ii) in Explanation-1 and Explanation-11, for the expression "Item (1)", the expression "items 1-A to 1-F" shall be substituted; (iii) for Explanation-III, the following shall be substituted, namely,- "Explanation III: For the purpose of item 1-A: (a) Basic price means: Ex-factory price + Cost of Bottles + Cost of packing material + Freight + Insurance + Handling charges and import fee, if any. (b) Case means: (i) Indian Made Foreign Liquor (other than Beer, Wine and Ready to Drink varieties): 9 numbers of 1000 ml. or 12 numbers of 750 ml. or 24 numbers of 375 ml. or 48 numbers of 180 ml. or 96 numbers of 90 ml. or 150 numbers of 60 ml. bottles of Indian Made Foreign Liquor; (i) Beer: 12 numbers of 650 ml. or 24 numbers of 330 ml. bottles or 24 numbers of 500 ml. in cans; (ii) Wine : 9 numbers of 1000 ml. or 12 numbers of 750 ml. or 24 numbers of 375 ml. or 48 numbers of 180 ml. or 96 numbers of 90 ml. or 150 numbers of 60 ml. bottles; (iii) Ready to Drink Varieties: 24 numbers of 250 ml. bottles or 24 numbers of 275 ml. bottles or 24 numbers of 330 ml. bottles".
