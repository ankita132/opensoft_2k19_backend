Andhra Pradesh Records of Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Record of rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books Act, 1971. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Records of Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Record of Rgihts in land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989.1 (2) It shall come into force at once Received the assent of the Governor on the 16th November 1989.
Andhra Pradesh Records of Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Record of Rights In Land and Pattadar Pass Books Act, 1971 (herein after referred to as the principal Act), in section 2, (i) clause (6-a) shall be re numbered as clause (6-b) and before the clause as so renumbered, the following clause shall be Inserted, namely:- (6-a) "owner" means a person who has permanent And heritable rights of possession on the land which can be alienated and Includes the bolder of a patta Issued to him as a landless poor person.; (ii) In clause (7), the words or as occupant or khatadar" shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Records of Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 5 of the principal Act, for sub-section (6), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- “(6) The Mandal Revenue Officer shall have the power to correct clerical errors, If any, in the Pass Books".
Andhra Pradesh Records of Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 5A of the principal Act, In sub-section (1) for the words "Where the name of any person is recorded as an occupant In the Record of Rights”, the words "Where a person is an occupant", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Records of Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 6A of the principal Act, in sub-section (1),- (a) the word "occupant" shall be emitted; (b) in the proviso, for the words “Provided that" the words "Provided further that" shall be substituted and before the proviso, the following person shall be Inserted, namely "Provided that an occupant of an inam land is also eligible to apply for the issue of a pass book as an occupant."
Andhra Pradesh Records of Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In sub section (1) of section 6C of the principal Act,- (i) for the words on the security of land," the words "on the security of land or crop" shall be substituted; (ii) for the, proviso the following proviso shall be substituted, namely "Provided that after three months from the date of publication of the notification under sub-section (2) of section 3, is the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, In Loan shall be granted by any credit agency unless the pattadar pass book is produced".
Andhra Pradesh Records of Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 7--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In sub-section (1) of section 6D of the principal Act, for the proviso, the following proviso Shall be substituted, namely:- "Provided that in the case of a landless per- son including a tenant or a mortgagee who becomes owner of land for the first time by purchase of land through a registered sale deed, the registering authority shall obtain a declaration from, him in the form prescribed and send the same to Mandal Revenue Officer to enable him to issue a pattadar pass book in the owner category to such purchaser in the manner prescribed".
