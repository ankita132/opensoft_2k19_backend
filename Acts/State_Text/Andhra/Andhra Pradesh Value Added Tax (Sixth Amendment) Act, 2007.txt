Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Sixth Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Sixth Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called as the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Sixth Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Andhra Pradesh. (3) (a) Sub-clause (i) of clause 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April, 2005 ; (b) Sub-clause (ii) of clause 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April, 2007 ; and (c) Sub-clause (iii) of clause 2 shall come into force on such date as the Government may, by notification, appoint.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Sixth Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005 (i) in Schedule I, for entry at Serial No. 24, the following shall be substituted, namely :- "24. Meat, Flesh of Poultry, Fish including dry fish, prawns, prawn seed, lobsters, crabs, shrimps and other sea food except when they are sold in frozen state or in a sealed container, eggs, Livestock and animal hair."; (ii) In Schedule IV, for entry at Serial No. 51, the following shall be substituted, namely :- "51. Rail Coaches, Engines, Wagons and Parts thereof."; (iii) in Schedule VI, after item 5, the following item shall be added, namely :- "6. Tobacco products including Gutkha, Khara At the point of first 25%" Masala, Kimam, Dokta, Zarda, Sukha or sale in the State. Surthi except those specified in Schedule I and Cigarettes.
