Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Spinning Mills (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-Operative Spinning Mills (Regulation) Act, 1983. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Spinning Mills (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Spinning Mills (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 20011 . (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 7th April, 2001
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Spinning Mills (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Spinning Mills (Regulation) Act, 1983 in the Schedule, after item 18, the following item shall be added, namely:- "19. The Chilakaluripeta Cotton Growers Co-operative Spinning Mills Limited, Chilakaluripeta." (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
