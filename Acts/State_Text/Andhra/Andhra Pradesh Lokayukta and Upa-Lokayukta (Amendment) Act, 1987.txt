Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to amend the Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta Act, 1983. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Thirty- eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (i) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April. 1986. Received the assent of the Governor on the 23rd August, 1987.
Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokyukta Act, 1983 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act section 5. for sub-section (4), the following Sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(4) The salary payable to the Lokayukta or Upa-Lokayukta in respect of time spent ON actual service shall respectively be the same as that of the Chief justice or a Judge of t he High Court of Andhra Pradesh."
Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the principal Act, Second Schedule shall be omitted.
