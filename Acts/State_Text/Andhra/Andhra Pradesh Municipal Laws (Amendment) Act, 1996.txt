Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1965 and the Hyderabad Municipal Corporations Act, 1955. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in 'the Forty-sixth Year' of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 19961 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force -with effect from 27th September 1995. Received the assent of the Governor on the 23rd January, 1996
Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1965, in section 62-A, in sub- section (1), in the proviso, for the words "in the 'aggregate exceed three and half years", the words "in the aggregate exceed" four years", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Hyderabad Municipal Corporations Act, 1955, in section 70G, in sub-section (1), in the proviso, for the words "in the aggregate exceed four and half years", "the words "in the aggregate exceed five years", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1994, in section 5, for the words "not exceeding one year and six months", the words "not exceeding two years", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Third, Amendment) Ordinance, 1995 is hereby repealed. (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs, Law Department.
