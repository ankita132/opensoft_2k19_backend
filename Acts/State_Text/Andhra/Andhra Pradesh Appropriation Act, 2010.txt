Andhra Pradesh Appropriation Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to Authorise Payments and Appropriation of Moneys from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Andhra Pradesh for the Services of the Financial Year Commencing on the 1st April, 2010. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Appropriation Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Appropriation Act, 2010.
Andhra Pradesh Appropriation Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The State Government may appropriate from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Andhra Pradesh, for the financial year commencing on the 1st April, 2010, a sum not exceeding one lakh fourteen thousand three hundred and seven crores, eighty five lakhs and ninety three thousand rupees, being moneys required to meet,- (a) the grants made by the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly for that year, as set-forth in column (3) of the Schedule; and (b) the expenditure charged on the Consolidated Fund of the State of Andhra Pradesh for the year, as set forth in column (4) of the Schedule.
Andhra Pradesh Appropriation Act, 2010_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The sums authorized to be appropriated from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by Andhra Pradesh by this Act, shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
