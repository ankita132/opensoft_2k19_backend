University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to Amend the University of Health Sciences Act, 1986. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India, as follows: -
University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 1990.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th February, 1990. Received the assent of the Governor on the 2nd April, 1990
University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the University of Health Sciences Act, 1986, in section 11, in sub-section (1), for the words "Chief Minister", the word "Governor" shall be substituted.
University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Ordinance, 1990 is hereby repealed. (P.V. Vidya Sagar), Secretary to Government, Law and Legislative Affairs, Law Department.
