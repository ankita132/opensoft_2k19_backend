Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries (Validation) Act, 1924_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to remove all doubts as to the validity of the Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries Act, 1923. Whereas it appears that the Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries Act, 1923, as assented to by the Governor on the 21st day of February, 1923, and by the Governor-General on the 12th day of March, 1923 and as published in the St. For George Gazette on the 29th day of May, 1923 contained the words "order regarding" in sub-section (2) of Section 11 of the said Act instead of the words "map recording" which occurred in the said sub-section as passed by the Local Legislative Council. And Whereas doubts have arisen as to the validity of the said Act and of things done under the same; And Whereas it is expedient to remove all such doubts; it is hereby enacted as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries (Validation) Act, 1924_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries (Validation) Act, 1924.
Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries (Validation) Act, 1924_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In this Act, "The Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries Act. 1923", shall mean what was published under that title in Part IV of the St. Fort George Gazette, dated the 29th day of May, 1923.
Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries (Validation) Act, 1924_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The provisions contained in the Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries Act, 1923, are hereby enacted with the substitution of the words, "A copy of the order and a copy of the map recording the boundaries as determined under Section 9, 10 or 11(1) shall be furnished to any person interested in such order or map" for the words "a copy of the order under Section 10 or 11(1) and a copy of the order regarding the boundaries as determined under Section 9, 10 or 11(1) shall be furnished to any person interested in such orders" occurring in sub-section (2) of Section 11 of the Andhra Pradesh Survey and Boundaries Act, 1923. And the said provisions with the said substitution shall be deemed to have been in force with effect from the 29th day of May, 1923.
