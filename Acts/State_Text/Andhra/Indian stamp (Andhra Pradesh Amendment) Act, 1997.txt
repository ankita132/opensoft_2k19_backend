Indian stamp (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 in its application to the State of Andhra Pradesh. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Indian stamp (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This act may be called the Indian stamp (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1997.1 (2) it extends to the whole of the State of Andhra Pradesh. (3) It shall come into force on such date as the state Government may, by notification in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 19th August	, 1997
Indian stamp (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 as an force in the State of Andhra Pradesh, at section 16-A,- (i) in clause (a), the word "and" shall be omitted; (ii) in clause (b), the word "and" shall be added at the end; (iii) after clause (b), the following shall be inserted; namely:- All or any of the powers conferred on it by section 9(1)(b) to the Commissioner and Inspector General of Registration and Stamps." (K.M. Nagabushan Rao) Secretary to Government, Legislative affairs & justice, I/c Law Department.
