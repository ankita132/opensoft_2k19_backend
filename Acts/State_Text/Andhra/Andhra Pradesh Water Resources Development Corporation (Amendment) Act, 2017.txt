Andhra Pradesh Water Resources Development Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Water Resources Development Corporation Act, 1997. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Water Resources Development Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Water Resources Development Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall come into force on such date, as the Government may, be notification appoint.
Andhra Pradesh Water Resources Development Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Water Resources Development Corporation Act, 1997, in section 34,- (i) in sub-section (1), the expression, "(1)" shall be omitted; (ii) sub-section (2) shall be omitted. (Duppala Venkataramana) Secretary to Government, Legal and Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
