Andhra Pradesh University Acts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra University Act, 1925, Sri Venkateswara University Act, 1954, Osmania University Act, 1999, Kakatiya University Act, 1976 and Sri Krishnadevaraya University Act, 1981. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh University Acts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh University Acts (Amendment) Act, 1989.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 16th November 1989.
Andhra Pradesh University Acts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra University Act, 1925,- (i) in Section 13-A, for sub-section (1) the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) The Finance officer shall be a whole time officer of the University appointed by the State Government on such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the rules made by the State Government in this behalf. He shall be the employee of the State Government and the salary, allowances, pension and other remuneration shall be paid in the first instance out of the consolidated Fund of the State and later recovered from the University."; (ii) in section 18, in sub-section (1) under Class-I Ex-Officio Members, for item (3), the following shall be substituted, namely;- "(3) the Secretary to Government in the Education Department or his nominee; (4) the Secretary to Government in the Finance and Planning (Finance Wing) Department or his nominee; and (5) the Director of Higher Education.".
Andhra Pradesh University Acts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Sri Venkateswara University Act, 1954,- (i) in section 13-A, for sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) The Finance Officer shall be a whole time officer of the University appointed by the State Government on such terms and conditions as may be prescribed by the rules made by the State Government in this behalf. He shall be the employee of the State Government and the salary, allowances, pension and other remuneration shall be paid in the first instance out of the Consolidated Fund of the State and later recovered from the University."; (ii) in section 18, in sub-section (1), under Class-I Ex-Officio Members, for items (3) and. (4), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(3) the Secretary to Government in the Education Department or his nominee; (4) the Secretary to Government in the Finance and Planning (Finance Wing) Department or his nominee; (5) the Director of Higher Education; and (6) the Executive Officer, Tirumala Tirupathi Devasthanams.".
