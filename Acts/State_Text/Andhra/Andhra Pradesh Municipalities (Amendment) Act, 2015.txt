Andhra Pradesh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1965. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2015. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Government may, by notification, in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint.
Andhra Pradesh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1965, in section 43, in sub-section (1),- (1) after the words "any contract", the words "including administrative sanction for the works" shall be inserted. (2) for the Table thereunder, the following Table shall be substituted, namely:- "TABLE Municipality Monetary Limits Authority (1) (2) (3) Third Grade Municipality including Nagar Panchayat (a) not exceeding Rs. 20,000/- Commissioner (b) exceeding Rs. 20,000/- but not exceeding Rs. 50,000/- Committee consisting of the Chairperson, Commissioner and not less than three but not more than seven members, chosen in this behalf by the Council. Second Grade Municipality (a) not exceeding Rs. 50,000/- Commissioner (b) exceeding Rs. 50,000/- but not exceeding Rs. 1,00,000/- Committee consisting of the Chairperson, Commissioner and not less than three but not more than seven members, chosen in this behalf by the Council. First Grade Municipality (a) not exceeding Rs. 1,00.000/- Commissioner (b) exceeding Rs. 1,00,000/-but not exceeding Rs. 2,00,000/- Committee consisting of the Chairperson, Commissioner and not less than three but not more than seven members, chosen in this behalf by the Council. Special Grade Municipality (a) not exceeding Rs. 2,00,000/- Commissioner (b) exceeding Rs. 2,00,000/-but not exceeding Rs. 4,00,000/- Committee consisting of the Chairperson, Commissioner and not less than three but not more than seven members, chosen in this behalf by the Council. Selection Grade Municipality (a) not exceeding Rs. 5,00,000/- Commissioner (b) exceeding Rs. 5,00,000/-but not exceeding Rs. 10,00,000/- Committee consisting of the Chairperson, Commissioner and not less than three but not more than seven members, chosen in this behalf by the Council.
