Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control (Amendment) Act, 19971 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 18th August, 1994. Received the assent of the Governor on 14th March, 1997
Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960 (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) in section 1 in sub-section (2),- (i) in clause(a) for the words "and to all Municipalities in the State of Andhra Pradesh" the words "and to all Municipal Corporations and Municipalities in the State of Andhra Pradesh" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause(b) for the words "to any Municipality in the State of Andhra Pradesh" the words "to any Municipal Corporation or Municipality in the State of Andhra Pradesh" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 3 of the Principal Act in sub-section (2) for the words "in any Municipality" the words "In any Municipal Corporation or in any Municipality" shall be substituted. (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs and Justice, Law Department.
