Andhra Pradesh Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Excise Act, 1968. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Andhra Pradesh Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Excise Act, 1968, (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 34, in item (1), in sub-item (ii), for the words "one year", the words "three years", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 37 of the principal Act,- (1) in item (i), for the words "a term which shall not be less than one year but which may extend upto three years", the words "a term which shall not be less than three years but which may extend upto five years", shall be substituted; (2) in item (ii), for the words "a term which shall not be less than two years but which may extend upto three years", the words "a term which shall not be less than three years but which may extend upto six years", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 37-A of the principal Act,- (1) in sub-section (1), in clause (i), for the words "two years", the words "three years", shall be substituted; (2) in sub-section (2), in clause (i), for the words "two years", the words "three years", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 60 of the principal Act, for sub-section (5), the following shall be substituted, namely,- "(5) Notwithstanding anything contained in the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, no court shall grant any bail to any person accused of an offence punishable under sub-item (i) of item (1) of section 34 and to any person accused of an offence under clause (h) of section 34 or section 40-A or section 50 or section 50-A of this Act, unless the prosecuting officer is given an opportunity to oppose the application and the Court record reasons while granting the bail."
Andhra Pradesh Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh After section 60 of the principal Act, the following new section shall be inserted namely,- 60-A. Non-bailable Offences - The offences punishable under sub-item (ii) of item (1) of section 34 or section 37 or section 37-A shall be non-bailable and the provisions of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 with respect to non bailable offences shall apply to those offences." R. Rama Chandra Reddy, Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
