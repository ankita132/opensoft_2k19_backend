Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts Act, 1972. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 19931 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 3rd September, 1993
Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts Act, 1972, in Section 16, in sub-section (2) for the words "rupees twenty five thousand", the words "rupees fifty thousand" shall be substituted.
