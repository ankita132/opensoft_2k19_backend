Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Small Causes Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Small Causes Courts Act, 1330F. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Small Causes Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Small Causes Courts (Amendment) Act, 19891 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 4th October, 1989
Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Small Causes Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh (Telanagana Area) shall Causes Courts Act, 1330F (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in section 4, in sub-section (4) for the words "rupees one thousand", the words "rupees four thousand" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Small Causes Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 6 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in the second paragraph, for the words "rupees two thousand and five hundred", the words "rupees ten thousand", shall be substituted.
