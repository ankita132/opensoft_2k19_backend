Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation Act, 1979. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1986.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 13th April, 1986.
Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation Act, 1979, in section 8,- (1) in sub-section (3), for the words "All taxes, fees and duties", the words "Save as otherwise provided in sub-section (3-A), all taxes, fees and duties" shall be substituted ; (2) after sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(3-A) In the case of lands and buildings vested in the trustees of the Visakhapatnam Port Trust, it shall be lawful for the Corporation to levy by resolution in any year, a property tax at the rate of four per centum of the annual gross earnings of the said Trust, in the year immediately preceding such levy".
