Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh 1 An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications Act, 1953. Be, it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty third year of the Republic of India, as follows:- Received the assent of the Governor on the 17th April, 2012 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 20th April, 2012 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette for general information.
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint and different dates may be appointed for different provisions of the Act.
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications Act, 1953, (herein after referred to as the principal Act),- (1) in Section 3,- for sub-section (1) the following shall be substituted namely; - "(1) (i) There shall be paid to the Chief Minister, every month, a salary of rupees sixteen thousand, a special allowance of rupees eight thousand, a sumptuary allowance of rupees seven thousand, a security car allowance of rupees twenty five thousand and a conveyance allowance of rupees thirty thousand in case a bullet proof motor car is used or rupees fifteen thousand in case any other motor car is used: Provided that if the Chief Minister desires that the propulsion charges incurred in respect of the motor car used by him be borne by the State Government in lieu of the conveyance allowance payable to him, such propulsion charges shall be borne by the State Government: (ii) There shall be paid to the Deputy Chief Minister, if any, and such member of the Council of Minister, whether a Cabinet Minister or a State Minister or a Deputy Minister, every month, a salary of rupees fourteen thousand, a special allowance of rupees eight thousand, a sumptuary allowance of rupees seven thousand, a security car allowance of rupees twenty five thousand and a conveyance allowance of rupees thirty thousand in case a bullet proof motor car is used or rupees fifteen thousand in case any other motor car is used." (iii) in sub-section (2),- (a) in the first, second and third provisos, for the words "rupees thirty thousand" wherever they occur, the words "rupees fifty thousand" shall be substituted; (b) in the fourth proviso, for the words "rupees five thousand" the words "rupees ten thousand" shall be substituted.".
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the principal Act, in Section 3-A, in sub-section (1),- (a) for clause (a), the following shall be substituted, namely,- "(a) There shall be paid to the Chief Whip and the Whip in the Assembly, and the Chief Whip and the Whip in the Council, every month, a salary of rupees fourteen thousand, a special allowance of rupees eight thousand, a sumptuary allowance of rupees seven thousand, a security car allowance of rupees twenty five thousand and a conveyance allowance of rupees thirty thousand in case a bullet proof motor car is used or rupees fifteen thousand in case any other motor car is used; (b) in clause (b), in the first, second and third proviso, for the words "thirty thousand" wherever they occur, the words "rupees fifty thousand" shall be substituted; (c) in the fourth proviso, for the words "rupees five thousand" the words "rupees ten thousand" shall be substituted.".
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the principal Act, in Section 4,- (1) for sub-section (1), the following shall be substituted, namely,- "(1) There shall be paid to the Speaker and the Deputy Speaker, the Chairman and the Deputy Chairman, every month, a salary of rupees fourteen thousand, a special allowance of rupees eight thousand, a sumptuary allowance of rupees seven thousand, a security car allowance of rupees twenty five thousand and a conveyance allowance of rupees thirty thousand in case a bullet proof motor car is used or rupees fifteen thousand in case any other motor car is used; (2) in sub-section (2), in the first, second and third provisos, for the words "thirty thousand" wherever they occur, the words "rupees fifty thousand" shall be substituted; (3) in the fourth proviso, for the words "rupees five thousand" the words "rupees ten thousand" shall be substituted.".
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the principal Act, in Section 6, in the first and second proviso, for the words "ten thousand rupees" wherever they occur, the words "thirty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.".
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the principal Act, in Section 11, in sub-section (1) clause (b) shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 7--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the principal Act, after Section 11, the following new section shall be inserted, namely,- "11-F. Every member of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly or the Legislative Council including those holding any of the offices referred to in Sections 3, 3-A, 4, 12-A and 12-B shall be entitled to receive a constituency allowance of Rs. 83,000/- (Rupees eighty three thousand only) per mensum.".
