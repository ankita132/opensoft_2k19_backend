Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1985. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-first year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 19911 . (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the 19-01-1991
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1985, section 36 shall be omitted.
