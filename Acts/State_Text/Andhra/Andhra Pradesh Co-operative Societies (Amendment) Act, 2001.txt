Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-Operative Societies Act, 1964. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 20011 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 27th September, 2000. Received the assent of the Governor on the 24th March, 2001
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964, in section 12A,- (i) in sub-section (1), in clauses (a) and (b), for the words "to transfer its assets and liabilities", the words "to transfer its assets or its assets and liabilities, in whole or part", shall be substituted; and (ii) Sub-section (3) shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) ordinance, 2000 is hereby repealed. (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
