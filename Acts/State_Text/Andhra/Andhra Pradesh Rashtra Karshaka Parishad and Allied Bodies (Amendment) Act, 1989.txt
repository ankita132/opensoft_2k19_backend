Andhra Pradesh Rashtra Karshaka Parishad and Allied Bodies (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to amend the Andhra Pradesh Rashtra Karshaka Parishad and Allied Bodies Act, 1988. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of the Andhra Pradesh in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Rashtra Karshaka Parishad and Allied Bodies (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Rashtra Karshaka Parishad and Allied Bodies (Amendment) Act, 19891 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 16th November 1989
Andhra Pradesh Rashtra Karshaka Parishad and Allied Bodies (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Section 4 of the Andhra Pradesh Rashtra Karshaka Parishad and Allied Bodies Act, 1988 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), in clause (i),- (i) sub-clause (a) shall be omitted; (ii) for sub-clause (b), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(b) to review and to monitor progress periodically against the programme targets;"
Andhra Pradesh Rashtra Karshaka Parishad and Allied Bodies (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 5 of the -principal Act, in sub-section (2), clause (v) shall be omitted.
