Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second-Amendment) Act, 19991 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 27th September, 1998. Received the assent of the Governor on the 1st January, 1999.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative societies Act, 1964, in section 12 A, in sub-section (3) for the words "for a period of two years", the words "for a period of four years", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Ordinance, 1998 is hereby repealed. (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice Law Department.
