Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University Act, 1963. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 19921 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th February, 1992. Received the assent of the Governor on the 14th April, 1992
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University Act, 1963. in section 43, after subsection (6-B), the following sub-section, shall be inserted, namely:- "(6C) Notwithstanding anything in sub-section (6-A), every such teacher or other employee who had exercised his revised option under sub-section (6-B) to revert to the service under the Government or the Osmania University, as the case may be, to which he belongs, but has not seen reverted in pursuance of such option, shall be given an opportunity to revise the said revised option within such period not exceeding two year as may be specified by the Government from the date of the commencement of the Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, Act 9 of 1992."
Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University (Amendment) Ordinance, 1992 is hereby repealed.
