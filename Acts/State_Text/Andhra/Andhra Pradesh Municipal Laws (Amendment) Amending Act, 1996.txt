Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Amending Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1994. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Amending Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Amending Act, 19961 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 31st May, 1996. Received the assent of the Governor on the 4th October, 1996
Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Amending Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1994, in section 5, for the words "not exceeding two years", the words "not exceeding three years" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Amending Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Municipal Laws (Amendment) Amending Ordinance, 1996 is hereby repealed. (G. Bhavani Prasad), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice, Law Department.
