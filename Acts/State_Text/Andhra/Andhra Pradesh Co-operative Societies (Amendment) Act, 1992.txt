Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-third Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 19921 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 14th April 1992.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964. in section 85, after item (iv) and before the explanation, the following shall be inserted, namely:- "(v) the acquisition construction, rebuilding or repairing of rural dwelling houses."
