Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act, 1987. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of die State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows:
Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2005.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 30th October, 2004. Received the assent of the Governor on the 12th January, 2005 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 17th January, 2005 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette Part-IV-B (Ext.), dated 17th January 2005 for general information.
Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh Incorporated in the Main Act.
Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments (Amendment) Ordinance, 2004 is hereby repealed.
