Andhra Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act, 1961. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) (Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Andhra Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act, 1961 in Section 2,- (i) in sub-section (2), for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(a) The Ministers in charge of Sugar, Agriculture and Industries". (ii) in sub-section (5) for the word "Agriculture" wherever it occurs, the word "Sugar" shall be substituted.
