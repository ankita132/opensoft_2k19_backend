Andhra Pradesh Appropriation (No.2) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to Authorise the Appropriation of certain further Moneys from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Andhra Pradesh for the Services of the Financial year which Commenced on the 1st April, 2010. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-second year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Appropriation (No.2) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Appropriation (No.2) Act, 2011.
Andhra Pradesh Appropriation (No.2) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The State Government may appropriate from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Andhra Pradesh, for the financial year which commenced on the 1st April, 2010, a further sum not exceeding Nine thousand four hundred and sixty eight crores, one lakh and forty six thousand rupees, being moneys required to meet,- (a) the supplementary grants made by the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly for that year, as set-forth in column (3)of the Schedule; and (b) the supplementary expenditure charged on the Consolidated Fund of the State of Andhra Pradesh for that year, as set forth in column (4) of the Schedule.
Andhra Pradesh Appropriation (No.2) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The sums authorized to be appropriated from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act, shall b e appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
