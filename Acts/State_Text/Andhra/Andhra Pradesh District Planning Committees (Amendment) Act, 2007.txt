Andhra Pradesh District Planning Committees (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to Amend the Andhra Pradesh District Planning Committees Act, 2005. Be, it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh District Planning Committees (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh District Planning Committees (Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint.
Andhra Pradesh District Planning Committees (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh District Planning Committees Act, 2005, in Section 5, in subsection (1), after clause (b) the following clause shall be added, namely:- "(c) Members of the Legislative Council of the State shall also be the permanent special invitees to the meetings of the Committee of the District where he/ she is a registered voter.".
