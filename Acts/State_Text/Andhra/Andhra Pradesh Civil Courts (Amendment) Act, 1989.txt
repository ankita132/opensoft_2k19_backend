Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts Act, 1972. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fortieth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 19891 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 30th November 1989
Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts Act, 1972 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 5, in subsection (2) for the words "rupees-twenty thousand", the words "rupees fifty thousand" shall be substituted twenty thousand", the words "rupees fifty thousand" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 9 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in sub-clause (a) of clause (ii), for the words "rupees thirty thousand"; the words "rupees one lakh" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 16 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "rupees twenty thousand", the words "rupees twenty-five thousand" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 17 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in sub-clause (a) of clause (ii) for the words "rupees thirty thousand", the words "rupees one lakh" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 24 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1),- (a) in clause (i) for the words "two thousand and five hundred rupees", the words "ten thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in clause (ii) for the words "one thousand rupees", the words "four thousand rupees" shall be substituted."
