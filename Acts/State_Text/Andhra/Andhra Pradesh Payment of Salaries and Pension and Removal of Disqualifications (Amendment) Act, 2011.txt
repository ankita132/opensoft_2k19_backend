Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications Act, 1953. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-second year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Amendment) Act, 2011. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the First January, 2011.
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications Act, 1953 (herein after referred to as the Principal Act,) in Section 11,- (i) in sub-section (1), for clauses (a) and (b) the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(a) a salary of RS 12,000/- (Rupees Twelve thousand only) per mensum;" (b) a constituency allowance of RS 83,000/- (Rupees Eighty Three thousand only) per mensum; and (ii) in sub-section (1A),- (a) for the expression "of 30,000 Kms. (Thirty thousand Kilometers)" the expression "of 70,000 Kms, (Seventy thousand Kilometers)" shall be substituted. (b) for the expression "up to 30,000 Kms." The expression "up to 70,000 Kms." Shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Section 11-D of the Principal Act, in sub-section (1) for the paragraph after clause (iii) the following shall be, substituted, namely;- "A pension of fifteen thousand rupees per mensum for the first term or a part of it and a pension of one thousand rupees per mensum for each year of his service in subsequent term as such member, so however, that in no case the pension payable to such person shall exceed twenty five thousand rupees per mensum."
