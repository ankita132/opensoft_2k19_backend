Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1956. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 19921 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 1st May, 1992. Received the assent of the Governor on the 27th September 1992
Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh Act VII of 1956. In the Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1956, in section 67, after clause (XV), the following clause shall be added, namely:- (xvi) Plaints, memorandum of appeal, all types of petitions and applications, memorandum of appearance and vakalatnamas filed by the State Government or its Lawyers before any court, tribunal or other authority.
Andhra Pradesh Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Court fees and suits valuations (Amendment) ordinance, 1992 is hereby repeated.
