Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh 1 An Act further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-third year of the Republic of India as follows:- Received the assent of the Governor on the 17th April, 2012 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 20th April, 2012 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette for general information.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) Sub-section (3) and sub-section (5) of Section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 15th September, 2011 and the remaining provisions shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 14th September, 2011.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005 (hereinafter referred to as Principal Act) in Section 4,- (1) in sub-section (4), for the words and expression "at the rate of four percent (4%)" the words and expression "at the rate of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted; (2) in sub-section (7),- (i) in clause (b), for the words and expression "at the rate of 4%" the words and expression "at the rate of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (d), for the words and expression "at the rate of 4%' the words and expression "at the rate of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted; (3) in sub-section (8-B), for the words and expression "at the rate of 4%", the words and expression "at the rate of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted; (4) in sub-section (9), (i) in clause (b), for the words and expression "at the rate of four percent (4%): the words and expression "at the rate of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (d), for the words and expression "at the rate of four percent (4%)" the words and expression "at rate of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted. (5) in sub-section (11), for the words and expression "at the rate of 4%" the words and expression "at the rate of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the principal Act, in Section 13, in sub-section (6), for the words and expression "in excess of 4%" the words and expression "in excess of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the principal Act, in Section 22, in sub-section (3-A), for the words and expression "at the rate of 4%" the words and expression "as the rate of five percent (5%)" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2012 is hereby repealed.
