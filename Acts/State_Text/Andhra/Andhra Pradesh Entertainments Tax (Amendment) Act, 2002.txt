Andhra Pradesh Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Entertainments Tax Act, 1939. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty third Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Andhra Pradesh Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 2002.1 (2) It shall come into force on such dale as the State- Government may, by notification in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 21st December. 2002 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 23rd December, 2002 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazelle.
