Hyderabad Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further To Amend the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualification Act, 1953 Be it enacted by the Legislative assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty third year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Hyderabad Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Hyderabad Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act,2002 1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the state Government may by notification appoint. Received the Governor on the 21st December, 2002 and hereby first published on the 23rd December 2000 Andhra Pradesh Gazette
Hyderabad Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications Act, 1953 thereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 11, in sub-section(1) clause (1) shall be omitted.
Hyderabad Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 11A of the principal Act in Sub-section (1), in clause(a), for the words rupees one thousand per mensum the words, rupees three thousand per mensum Shall be substituted. (K.G. Shankar) Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice (FAC) Law Department
