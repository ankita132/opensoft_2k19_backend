Andhra Pradesh Tax on Luxuries (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Tax on Luxuries Act, 1987. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-first Year of the-Republic of India as follows :
Andhra Pradesh Tax on Luxuries (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Tax on Luxuries (Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Andhra Pradesh. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 18th December, 1998.
Andhra Pradesh Tax on Luxuries (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 3 of the Andhra Pradesh Tax on Luxuries Act, 1987, in sub-section (1), for the words and figures "is Rs. 60 or more per day per person, a tax at the rate of ten per centum of such rate", the words "is Rs. 300 or more per day per person, a tax at the rate of five per centum of such rate", shall be substituted.
