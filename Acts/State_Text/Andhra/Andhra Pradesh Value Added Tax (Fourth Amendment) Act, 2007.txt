Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-eighth year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Andhra Pradesh. (3) (a) clause (i) in sub-section (1) of section 2 shall come into force on the 1st September, 2007. (b) entries 54, 55 and 56 in clause (ii) in sub-section (1) of section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st June, 2007 and entry 57 in clause (ii) of sub-section (1) of section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 18th August, 2005. (c) clauses (i), (iii) and (iv) in sub-section (2) of section 2 shall be deemed to have been come into force on the 13th June, 2007. Clause (ii) in sub-section (2) of section 2 shall be deemed to have been come into force on the 1st June, 2007 and clause (v) in sub-section (2) of section 2 shall be deemed to have been come into force on the 4th June, 2007.
