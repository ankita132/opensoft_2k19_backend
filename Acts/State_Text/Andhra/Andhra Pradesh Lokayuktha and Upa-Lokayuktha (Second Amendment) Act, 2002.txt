Andhra Pradesh Lokayuktha and Upa-Lokayuktha (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta Act, 1983. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Lokayuktha and Upa-Lokayuktha (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Lokayuktha and Upa-Lokayuktha (Second Amendment) Act, 2002.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 17th August, 2002 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 20th August, 2002 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette
Andhra Pradesh Lokayuktha and Upa-Lokayuktha (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta Act, 1983, in section 2, in clause (k), in sub-clause (iv) for item (1), the following shall be substituted "(1) Act No. 13 of 1994:- Every Chairperson and every Vice-Chairperson of a Zilla Parishad and every President of a Mandal Parishad constituted by or under the Andhra Pradesh Panchayat Raj Act, 1994." (K.G Shankar), Secretary to Government Legislative Affairs and Justice (FAC) Law Department
