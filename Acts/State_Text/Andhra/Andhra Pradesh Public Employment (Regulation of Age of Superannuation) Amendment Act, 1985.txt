Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to amend the Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Act, 1984. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1985_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1985. (2) Sections 2 and 4 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 23rd August, 1984 and Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th April, 1983.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 5th April, 1985 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 6th April, 1985 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette
Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1985_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Act, 1984 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in Section 3, (i) in sub-section (1), for the words “fifty five years” the words “fifty-eight years” shall be substituted; (ii) in Explanation II, in clause (a), for the words “fifty-five” the words “fifty-eight” shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1985_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh Printed in the main Act, i.e., Act No. 23 of 1984, which is published in the previous page.
Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1985_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) The provisions of Section 2 of this Act shall not apply to persons who attained the age of superannuation in pursuance of the notifications issued in G.O. Ms. No. 36, Finance and Planning (Finance Wing-FRI) Department, Dt. 8-2-1983, or in pursuance of the provisions of the Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Act, 1984, as in force prior to the commencement of this Act(Act 23 of 1984). (2) Nothing in Section 2 of this Act, shall apply to or affect any proceedings pending before any Court or the Andhra Pradesh Administrative Tribunal or any other Tribunal questioning the validity of the notifications or the law referred to in sub-section (i).
Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1985_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Third Amendment Ordinance, 1984 is hereby repealed.
