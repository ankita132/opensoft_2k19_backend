Andhra Pradesh University of Law (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh 1 An Act to Amend the Andhra Pradesh University of Law Act, 2008 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:- Received the assent of the Governor on the 10th May, 2012 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 14th May, 2012 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette for general information.
Andhra Pradesh University of Law (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh University of Law (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Andhra Pradesh University of Law (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh University of Law Act, 2008 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in Section 1, in sub-section (1), for the expression "the Andhra Pradesh University of Law Act, 2008", the expression "Damodaram Sanjivayya National Law University Act, 2008" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh University of Law (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Section 2 of the principal Act,, in clause (vii), for the words "the Andhra Pradesh University of Law" the words "Damodaram Sanjivayya National Law University" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh University of Law (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Section 3 of the principal Act,- (1) In sub-section (1), for the words "the Andhra Pradesh University of Law" the words "Damodaram Sanjivayya National Law University" shall be substituted; (2) In the marginal heading, for the words "Establishment of University of Law", the words "Establishment of Damodaram Sanjivayya National Law University" shall be substituted.
