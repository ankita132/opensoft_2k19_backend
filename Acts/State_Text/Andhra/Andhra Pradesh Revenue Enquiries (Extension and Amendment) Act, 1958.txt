Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries (Extension and Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to extend [the Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries Act, 1893], to certain areas in the State of Andhra Pradesh and further to amend it in its application to that State. (Substituted for the original short title by the First Schedule to the A.P. Laws (Amendment of Short Titles) Act, 1961 (Andhra Pradesh Act IX of 1961)). Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries (Extension and Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called [the Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries (Extension and Amendment) Act, 1958.]1 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Andhra Pradesh. (3) This section shall come into force at once and the remaining provisions shall come into force on such date as the Government may, by notification in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint.2 Substituted for the original short title by the First Schedule to the A.P. Laws (Amendment of Short Titles) Act, 1961 (Andhra Pradesh Act IX of 1961)). Came into force on the 2-7-1959
Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries (Extension and Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In this Act- (a) ‘Andhra area’ means the territories of the State of Andhra Pradesh which immediately before the 1st November, 1956 were comprised in the State of Andhra ; (b) ‘Government means the State Government; (c) ‘transferred territories’ means the territories specified in sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the States Re-organisation Act, 1956 (Central Act 37 of 1956).
Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries (Extension and Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh [The Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries Act, 1893 (Act V of 1893)] as in force at the commencement of this Act in the Andhra area (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), as amended by Sections 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8 is hereby extended to, and shall be in force in the Transferred Act, 1893].1 Substituted for the original short title by the First Schedule to the A.P. Laws (Amendment of Short Titles) Act, 1961 (Andhra Pradesh Act IX of 1961)

Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries (Extension and Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 9--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh If any difficulty arises in giving effect to the provisions of this Act or of the principal Act as extended by this Act, in their application to the transferred territories, the Government may, by order, make such provisions or give such directions, not inconsistent with the purposes of this Act or of the principal Act as extended by this Act, as appear to them to be necessary or expedient for removing the difficulty.
Andhra Pradesh Revenue Enquiries (Extension and Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 10--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) The Conferring of Powers for Summoning Witnesses Act (Hyderabad Act I of 1314 F.) is hereby repealed. (2) Upon such repeal, the provisions of the Andhra Pradesh General Clauses Act, 1891 (Act I of 1891), shall apply; and for the removal of doubts it is hereby declared that any proceeding commenced under the said Act and pending at the commencement of this Act before any officer or as if Court, shall be disposed of in accordance with the provisions of the said Act had continued in force and this Act had not been passed.
