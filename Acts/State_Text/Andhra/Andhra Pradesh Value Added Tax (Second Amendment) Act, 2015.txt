Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2015. (2) It shall be deemed to have come in to force on the and from the 20th September, 2014.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh value Added Tax Act, 2005 [herein after referred to as the Principal Act], in Schedule-VI, in the table, against item No. 4 "Aviation Turbine Fuel", in column No. 4, Rate of Tax I, for the figure and percentage "16%", the figure and percentage "1%" shall be substituted. T. Narayana Reddy Secretary to Government (F.A.C), Law Department.
