Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries Act, 1960. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 19901 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 26th October, 1990.
Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries Act, 1960 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 7, for sub-section (2), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(2) The Chairman and every member of the Parishad, other than the ex-officio Member, shall hold office during the pleasure of the Governor".
Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 7B of the principal Act, the words "before the expiration of the term" and the words "and the persons so nominated, shall hold office for the residue of the term of his predecessor" shall be omitted.
Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 11 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1), for the words "for a period of three years from the date of his nomination", the words "during the pleasure of the Governor" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (3), for the words "the term of Office of the Chairman of a Zilla Grandhalaya Samstha shall be three years from the date of his nomination as Chairman", the words "the Chairman of a Zilla Grandhalaya Samstha shall hold office during the pleasure of the Governor", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 11A of the principal Act in the proviso to sub-section (2), for the words "during his term of office", the words "during the period he holds office", shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In section 12A of the principal Act, for the word "Committee", wherever it occurs the word "Parishad" shall be substituted.
