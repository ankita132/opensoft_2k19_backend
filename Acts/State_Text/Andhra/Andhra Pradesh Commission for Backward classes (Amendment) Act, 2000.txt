Andhra Pradesh Commission for Backward classes (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Commission For Backward Classes Act, 1993. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of "the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Commission for Backward classes (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Commission for Backward classes (Amendment) Act, 2000.
Andhra Pradesh Commission for Backward classes (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1993, in section 4, to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely- "Provided that the Government, may by notification, and for reasons specified therein extend the term of office of the member for a period not exceeding six months at a time."
