Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications Act, 1953. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 10th July, 2007.
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Section 3-A, in sub-section (1) in clause (b) of the principal Act,-(i) in the first proviso, for the words "rupees fifteen thousand", the words "rupees thirty thousand", shall be substituted; (ii) in the second proviso, for the words "rupees fifteen thousand", the words "rupees thirty thousand", shall be substituted: (iii) in the third proviso, for the words "rupees fifteen thousand", the words "rupees thirty thousand", shall be substituted;
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (i) in the first proviso, for the words "rupees ten thousand", the words "rupees thirty thousand", shall be substituted; (ii) in the second proviso, for the words "rupees ten thousand", the words "rupees thirty thousand", shall be substituted; (iii) in the third proviso, for the words "rupees ten thousand", the words "rupees thirty thousand", shall be substituted;
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh After Section 4 of the principal Act, the following new section shall be inserted namely:- 4-A. "Validation of enhancement of House Rent Allowance to the Speaker:-Notwithstanding any thing contained in Section 4, the enhancement of House Rent Allowance from rupees ten thousand to rupees fifteen thousand per month in respect of the building owned by the Speaker with effect from 1-6-2004 through executive orders issued in G.O.Rt.No. 4750, General Administration (Accommodation) Department, dated 17-8-2004 shall be deemed to have been validly made.".
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Section 12-A of the principal Act, after item (3) the following item shall be added namely:- "(4) Chairman of the North Coastal Andhra Planning and Development Committee.".
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Principal Act, in the Schedule, in entry 1 -B the following shall be added at the end namely:- "or the Chairman of the North Coastal Andhra Planning and Development Committee.".
Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of Disqualifications (Second Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 7--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Payment of Salaries and Pension and Removal of disqualifications (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 2007 is hereby repealed.
