Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India, as follows :
Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2002.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 20th April, 2002 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 22nd May, 2002 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette
Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, in section 14, in Sub-section (1), the second proviso shall be omitted. (K.G. Shankar), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice (FAC), Law Department. Law: Sales Tax; Indirect Tax
