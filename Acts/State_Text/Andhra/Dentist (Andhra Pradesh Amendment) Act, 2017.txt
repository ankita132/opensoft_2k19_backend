Dentist (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Dentist Act, 1948 in its Application to the State of Andhra Pradesh. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Dentist (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Dentist (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Dentist (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Dentist Act, 1948, in section 27, for sub-section (1), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) Subject to the provisions of this section, an elected or nominated member shall hold office for a term of five (5) years from the date of his election or nomination.". (Duppala Venkata Ramana) Secretary to Government Legal and Legislative Affairs and Justice, Law Department.
