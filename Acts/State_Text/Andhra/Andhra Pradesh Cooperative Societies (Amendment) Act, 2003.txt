Andhra Pradesh Cooperative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-Operative Societies Act, 1964. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Andhra Pradesh Cooperative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Cooperative Societies (Amendment) Act, 20031 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 5th August, 2003. Received the assent of the Governor on the 22nd October, 2003
Andhra Pradesh Cooperative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964, in section 32, in sub-section (7),- (1) clause (a), shall be renumbered as sub-clause (a) (i) thereof; (2) after sub-clause (a) (i) as so renumbered, the following shall be added, namely: - "(ii) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, in the special circumstances and for the reasons to be recorded, if in the opinion of the Government, it is not possible to hold the elections to the societies or class of societies, the Government may by order extend the term of the person or the persons appointed to manage the affairs of the society or class of societies beyond three years but not exceeding five years in aggregate.".
Andhra Pradesh Cooperative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 2003 is hereby repealed. (K.G. Shankar), Secretary to Government, Legislative Affairs & Justice (FAC), Law Department
