Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra University, Sri Venkateswara University, The Osmania University, The Nagarjuna University, The Kakatiya University, Sri Krishna Devarayan University, The Andhra Pradesh Open University, Sri Padmavathi Mahila Viswavidyalayam and the Telugu University Acts. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh University Acts (Amendment) Act, 1986.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 8th August, 1986.
Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra University Act, 1925, in section 12 in sub-section (1), to clause (a), the following Proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that a person who has completed the age of sixty years shall not be eligible for appointment as a Vice-Chancellor."
Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Sri Venkateswara University Act, 1954, in section 12, in sub-section (1), to clause (a), the following proviso shall be added, namely: "Provided that a person who has completed the age of sixty years shall not be eligible for appointment as a Vice-Chancellor."
Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 4--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Osmania University Act, 1959, in section 12, to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that a person who has completed the age of sixty years shall not be eligible for appointment as a Vice-Chancellor.".
Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 5--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Nagarjuna University Act, 1976, in section 10, to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that a person who has completed the age of sixty years shall not be eligible for appointment as a Vice-Chancellor.".
Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 6--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Kakatiya University Act, 1976, in section 10, to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided that a person who has completed the age of sixty years shall not be eligible for appointment as a Vice-Chancellor".
Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 7--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Sri Krishnadevaraya University Act, 1981 in section 10, to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided that a person who has completed the age of sixty years shall not be eligible for appointment as a Vice-Chancellor.".
Andhra Pradesh University Acts, (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 8--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Open University Act, 1982, in section 10, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), after the proviso, the following further proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided further that a person who has completed the age of sixty years shall not be eligible for appointment as a Vice-Chancellor.".
