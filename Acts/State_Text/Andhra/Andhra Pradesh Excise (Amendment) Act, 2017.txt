Andhra Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Excise Act, 1968. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Andhra Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Excise Act, 1968, in section 47, in sub-section (1), for the words 'one lakh rupees and subject to such minima, as may be prescribed', the words 'a sum of money subject to such minima, as may be prescribed and notified by the Government from time to time', shall be substituted. (T. Narayana Reddy) Secretary to Government (FAC), Law Department.
