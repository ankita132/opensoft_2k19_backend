Hyderabad Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act Further to Amend the Hyderabad Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1989. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Hyderabad Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Hyderabad Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Hyderabad Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Hyderabad Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1989 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in Section 3, in sub-section (2), after clause (j), the following clause shall be added, namely:- "(ja) Directors":-Four persons to be nominated by the Government from the category of Mayors/Chairpersons/ elected members of the Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation and Municipalities falling within the areas of the Board.
Hyderabad Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In Section 7 of the principal Act, after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely: - "(3) the Board shall involve Grater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation and all the Municipalities falling within the area of the Board in the preparation and implementation of major water supply and sewerage projects.".
