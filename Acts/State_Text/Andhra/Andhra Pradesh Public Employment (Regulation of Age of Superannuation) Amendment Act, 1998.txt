Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Act, 1984. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in Forty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) Amendment Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Age of Superannuation) (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 16th August, 1998 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 19th August, 1998 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette.
