Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta Act, 1983. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-second Year of Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 2002.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 22nd January, 2002 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 24th January, 2002 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette
Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Lokayukta and Upa-Lokayukta Act, 1983, in section 2, in clause (i), for the words, "rupees one thousand one hundred and fifty and below", the words, "rupees seven thousand four hundred and below of the Revised Scales of Pay, 1999 or the corresponding revision of scale of pay as and when such revision takes place from time to time" shall be substituted. (K.G Shankar), Secretary to Government Legislative Affairs and Justice (FAC) Law Department
