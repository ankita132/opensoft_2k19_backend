Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh 1 An Act further to Amend the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Sixty-Third year of the Republic of India as follows:- Received the assent of the Governor on the 17th April, 2012 and the said assent is hereby first published on the 20th April, 2012 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette for general information.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) (a) Section 2 and clause (v) of Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from 11th, July, 2011; (b) clause (i) of Section 3 and Section 4 shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from 14th September, 2011; (c) clause (ii) of Section 3, shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from 24th August, 2011; (d) clause (iii) of Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from 22nd November, 2011; (e) clause (iv) of Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from 17th May, 2011;
