Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 1991 (16 of 1991)_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to amend the Andhra Pradesh Co-Operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1990. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Forty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 1991 (16 of 1991)_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 19911 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th June, 1991. Received the assent of the Governor on the 29-09-1991
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 1991 (16 of 1991)_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1990, in section 4, in sub-section (2), in the proviso for the words "one year", the words "one year and nine months" shall be substituted.
Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Act, 1991 (16 of 1991)_Section 3--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh The Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Amending Ordinance, 1991 is hereby repealed.
