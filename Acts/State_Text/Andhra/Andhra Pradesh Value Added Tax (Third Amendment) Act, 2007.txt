Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act further to amend the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh in the Fifty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1) This Act may be called as the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Andhra Pradesh. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from 1st April, 2007.
Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh In the Andhra Pradesh Value Added Tax Act, 2005 in Schedule I,- (i) for serial No.47, the following shall be substituted, namely:- "47. Tobacco and manufactured Tobacco but excluding cigarettes containing Tobacco with Excise Tariff Code 2402.20 and also excluding cigarettes with Tobacco substitutes with Excise Tariff code 2402.90." (ii) in the Explanation, for the figures "46 & ; 47", the figures " & ; 46" shall be substituted.
