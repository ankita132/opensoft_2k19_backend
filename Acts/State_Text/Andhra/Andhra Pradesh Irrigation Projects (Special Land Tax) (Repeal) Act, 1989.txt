Andhra Pradesh Irrigation Projects (Special Land Tax) (Repeal) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh An Act to repeal the Andhra Pradesh Irrigation Projects (Special Land Tax) Act, 1976. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the state of Andhra Pradesh in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Andhra Pradesh Irrigation Projects (Special Land Tax) (Repeal) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh This Act may be called the Andhra Pradesh Irrigation Projects (Special Land Tax) (Repeal) Act, 1989.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 30th November, 1989 and published on the 2nd December, 1989 in the Andhra Pradesh Gazette, Part IV-B (Ext.)
Andhra Pradesh Irrigation Projects (Special Land Tax) (Repeal) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Andhra Pradesh (1)The Andhra Pradesh Irrigation Projects (Special Land Tax) Act, 1976 is hereby repealed. (2) Upon such repeal, the provisions of Section 8 of the Andhra Pradesh General Clauses Act, 1891 shall apply.
