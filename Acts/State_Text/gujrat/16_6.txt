Gujarat Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat Sales Tax Act, 1969. It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :
Gujarat Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat 1) This Act may be called the Gujarat Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 1st January, 2000.
