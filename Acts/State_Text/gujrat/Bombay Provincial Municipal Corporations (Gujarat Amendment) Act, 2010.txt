Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations Act, 1949. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force from the 1st January, 2009.
Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations Act, 1949, in section 458, in clause (36), after the words "proper behavior of persons in them", the words "and the levy of fees therein" shall be added at the end.
