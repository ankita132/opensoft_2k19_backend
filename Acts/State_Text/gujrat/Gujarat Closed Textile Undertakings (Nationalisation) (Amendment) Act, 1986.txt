Gujarat Closed Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to amend the Gujarat Closed Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) Act, 1980. It is hereby enacted In the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Gujarat Closed Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat Closed Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) (Amendment) Act, 1986.
Gujarat Closed Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat Closed Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) Act, 1986 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in section 2, in sub-section (1), in clause (b), after sub-clause (iii), the following sub-clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(iii-a) a corresponding new bank constituted under section 3 of the Banking Companies (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1980;".
Gujarat Closed Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in the First Schedule, in entry 10, in column 3, for the words and letters "Shri Ambica Mills Ltd., Ahmedabad", the words and letters "The Sarangpur Cotton Mfg. Co, Ltd, Ahmedabad" shall be substituted.
Gujarat Closed Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 4--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in the Second Schedule- (1) under the heading 'Category I' for the portion beginning with the words "all dues" and ending with the word "undertaking", the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(i) All dues including gratuity of employees in the specified textile undertaking; (ii) Arrears relating to contributions towards Provident Fund and contributions under the Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 payable by the owner" (2) under the heading 'Category II', for the portion beginning with the words "Arrears' relating to" and ending with the words "local authority", the following shall be substituted, namely:- "Arrears of Excise Duty, Sates Tax, dues relating to electricity and dues of a local authority.".
