Gujarat Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat Entertainments Tax Act, 1977. It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Gujarat Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st April, 2007.
Gujarat Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat Entertainment Tax Act, 1977 (Guj. 16 of 1977), in section 10, in sub-section (2) for the words "at the rate of twenty-four per cent per annum", the words "at such rate not exceeding eighteen per cent per annum, as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, specify," shall be substituted.
