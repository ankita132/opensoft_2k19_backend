Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2002. (2) It shall come into force cm the 1st April, 2002.
Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958, in the First Schedule, in Part-I,- (1) after clause IV, the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "IVAA. Sleeper designated omnibuses- (a) Sleeper designated omnibuses licensed to carry not more than twenty passengers Rs. 13.000 per passenger which the vehicle is licensed to carry. (b) Sleeper designated omnibuses licensed to carry more than twenty passengers. Rs. 18.000 per passenger which the vehicle is licensed to carry". (2) before Explanation I, the following Explanation shall be inserted, namely:- "Explanation 1A.-For the purposes of clause IVAA "sleeper designated omnibus" means a contract carriage constructed or adapted to provide berths for passengers to sleep thereon.".
