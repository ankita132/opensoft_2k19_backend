Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to amend the Bombay Khadi and Village Industries Act, 1960. It is hereby enacted in the Sixteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1965.
Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Khadi and Village Industries Act, 1960 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in section 4. in sub-sections (3) and (4), for the word "allowances" the word "emoluments" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In section 29 of the principal Act, in clause (a) of sub-section (2), for the word "allowances" the word "emoluments" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
