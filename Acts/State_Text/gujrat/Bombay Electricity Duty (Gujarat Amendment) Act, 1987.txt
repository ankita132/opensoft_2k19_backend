Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Electricity Duty Act, 1958 for the purpose hereinafter appearing. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1987.
Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Electricity Duty Act, 1958, in section 8, for the figures and words "12 per cent per annum", the figures and words "24 per cent per annum" shall be substituted.
