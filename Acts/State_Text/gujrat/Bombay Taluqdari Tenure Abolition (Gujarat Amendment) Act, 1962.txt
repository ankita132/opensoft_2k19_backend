Bombay Taluqdari Tenure Abolition (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to amend the Bombay Taluqdari Tenure Abolition Act, 1949. It is hereby enacted in the Thirteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Taluqdari Tenure Abolition (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Taluqdari Tenure Abolition (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Bombay Taluqdari Tenure Abolition (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 5A of the Bombay Taluqdari Tenure Abolition Act, 1949, in sub-section (2) for the words "seven years" the words "eight years" shall be substituted.
Bombay Taluqdari Tenure Abolition (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat The Bombay Taluqdari Tenure Abolition (Gujarat Amendment) Ordinance, 1962 is hereby repealed and the provisions of sections 7 and 25 of the Bombay General Clauses Act, 1904 shall apply to such repeal as if that Ordinance were an enactment.
