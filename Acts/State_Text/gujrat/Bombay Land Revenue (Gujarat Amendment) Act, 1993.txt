Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879. It is hereby enacted in the forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1993. (2) It shall come into force on such date, as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
