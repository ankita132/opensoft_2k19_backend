Gujarat Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat Value Added Tax Act, 2003. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follow :-
Gujarat Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Gujarat Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2011. (2) It shall come into force from the 1st April, 2011.
Gujarat Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat Value Added Tax Act, 2003 (Guj. 1 of 2005) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in Sec. 7, in sub-sec. (1A), in the proviso, Item (a), shall be deleted.
Gujarat Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in Sec. 9, in sub-sec. (6), in the proviso, item (a), shall be deleted.
Gujarat Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 4--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat Value Added Tax Act, 2003 (Guj. 1 of 2005), in Schedule II, in the entry at serial No. 76A in column 3, for the words "Seventeen and a half paise in the rupee", the words "Twenty-two and a half paise in the rupee" shall be substituted.
