Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An act further to amend the Gujarat Carriage of Goods, Taxation Act, 1962, It is hereby funded in the Twenty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1977. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 12 of the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation Act, 1962, after sub- section (1A), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(1B) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1A) (i) every operator of goods vehicle who has not exercised the option of lump sum payment of tax under that sub-section immediately before the date of commencement of the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment ) Act, 1977, and (ii) any person who becomes such an operator on a clay after that date, shall have an option of Jump sum payment of tax to be exercised as provided in sub-section (2) at any time after that date.".
