Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to, amend the Bombay Stamp Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1987.
Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Stamp Act, 1958, in Section 7, after sub-section (2), the following new sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(3) The provisions of this Act and the rules made thereunder in so far as they relate to the recovery of duties chargeable on instruments under Section 3 shall, so far as may be apply to the recovery of duties chargeable on a counterpart, duplicate or copy of an instrument under sub-section (1)".
