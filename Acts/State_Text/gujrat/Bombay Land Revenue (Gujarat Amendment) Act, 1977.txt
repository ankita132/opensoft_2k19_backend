Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879. It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1977.
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879, in section 132, for the words "provided that the said fee shall in no case exceed twenty rupees for each building site or any portion thereof held separately" the words "provided that the said fee shall in no case exceed such amount for each building site or any portion thereof held separately as the State Government may, having regard to the cost ordinarily incurred in such survey operations, specify in those rules" shall be substituted.
