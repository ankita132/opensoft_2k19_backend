Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to amend the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879 with a view to discontinuing exemption from payment of land revenue in respect of lands, other than lands ordinarily used for the purposes of agriculture only, situate within the sites of certain towns and cities in the State of Gujarat. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), section 128 shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) thereof and after sub-section (1) as so renumbered, the following sub-section shall be added, namely:- "(2) The existing exemption from payment of land revenue continued under sub-section (1) in respect of lands specified in that sub-section, shall with effect on and from 1st August 1981 stand discontinued.".
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in section 129, in sub-section (1), for the words "under the last preceding section" the words, brackets and figures "under sub-section (1) of 128" shall be substituted.
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in section 130, for the word and figures "section 128", the words, brackets and figures "sub-section (1) of section 128" shall be substituted.
