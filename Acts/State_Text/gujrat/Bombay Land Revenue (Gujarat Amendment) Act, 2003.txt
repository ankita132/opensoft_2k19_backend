Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2003_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat Ant Act further to amend the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879. It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2003. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st April, 2003.
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in section 67A,- (1) in sub-sections (1) and (2), for the words, brackets and figures "in column (3), (4), (5), (6) or (7)", the words, brackets and figures "in column (3) or (4)" shall be substituted; (2) sub-section (2A),- (i) for the word, brackets and figure "column (6)", the word, brackets and figure "column (4)" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words, brackets and figures "columns (3), (4) and (5)", the word, brackets and figure "column (3)" shall be substituted; (3) for the existing Table and Explanation thereunder, the following Table shall be substituted, namely:- "TABLE Sr. No. Area in which land is situated Rate of conversion tax per square metre of land  when land is to be used for industrial purpose or for any other purpose. when land is to be used for temporary non-agricultural purpose or for residential purpose or for charitable purpose. 1 2 3 4 1.
