Bombay Non-Trading Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Non-trading Corporations Act, 1959. It is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Non-Trading Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Non-trading Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963.
Bombay Non-Trading Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In Chapter V of the Bombay Non-trading Corporations Act, 1959, after section 66 the following new section shall be and shall be deemed always to have been added, namely:- "66A. Fees in respect of certain matters. - The prescribed fees shall be paid to the Registrar in respect of- (a) the filing of any document with him, (b) the registration or recording of any document by him, (c) his making a record of or registering any fact, in pursuance of this Act.".
