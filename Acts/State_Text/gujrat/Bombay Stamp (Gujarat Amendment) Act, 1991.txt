Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Stamp Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Forty-second Tear of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1991. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st May, 1984.
Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Stamp Act, 1958 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in section 32A, in sub-section (3), for the words "which shall not be less than such difference and not more than twice the amount of such difference", the words "of two hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in section 32B, in sub-section (1), for the word "seventy five per cent.", the words "twenty-five per cent" shall be substituted.
