Gujarat National Law University (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend to the Gujarat National Law University Act, 2003. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat National Law University (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat National Law University (Amendment) Act, 2011.
Gujarat National Law University (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat National Law University Act, 2003 (Guj. 9 of 2003) in Section 9, for sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(I) The Chief Justice of India, or a sitting Judge of the Supreme Court to be nominated by the Chief Justice of India, shall be the Visitor of the University.".
