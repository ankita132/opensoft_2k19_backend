Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat 1 An Act further to amend the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :- First published after having received the assent of the Governor on 3-5-2011 and published in the Gujarat Government Gazette, Extraordinary Part IV, No. 20, dated 4-5-2011.
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This may be called the Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011.
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Land Revenue Code, 1879 (Bom. V of 1879) (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in Sec. 61, in Para (3), for the words "five rupees, or a sum equel to ten times the amount of assessment payable by him for one year, if such sum be in excess of five rupees,", the words and brackets "one per cent of the prevalent annual statement of rate (Jantri) as may be notified by the State Government from time to time," shall be substituted.
Bombay Land Revenue (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in Sec. 63, for Para (2), the following shall be substituted, namely :- "The price of the land so offered shall be such as may be determined by the State Government.".
