Bombay Medical (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Medical Act, 1912. It is hereby enacted in the Eighteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Medical (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Medical (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1967.
Bombay Medical (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 2B of the Bombay Medical Act, 1912, in sub-section (4), for the words "six years" the words "seven years" shall be substituted.
