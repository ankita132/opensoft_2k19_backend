Bombay District Municipal (Saurashtra Area) and Bombay Municipal Boroughs (Saurashtra and Kutch Areas) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to amend the Bombay District Municipal Act, 1901 as applied to the Saurashtra area and the Bombay Municipal Boroughs Act, 1925 as applied to the Saurashtra and Kutch areas of the State of Gujarat. It is hereby enacted in the Twelfth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay District Municipal (Saurashtra Area) and Bombay Municipal Boroughs (Saurashtra and Kutch Areas) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may he called the Bombay District Municipal (Saurashtra Area) and Bombay Municipal Boroughs (Saurashtra and Kutch Areas) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1961.
Bombay District Municipal (Saurashtra Area) and Bombay Municipal Boroughs (Saurashtra and Kutch Areas) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay District Municipal Act, 1901 as adapted and applied to the Saurashtra area of the Gujarat State in sub-section (1) of section 179, the following words and figures shall be added at the end, namely:- "Such period may be longer than the term for which the councillors of the municipality would have held office under section 17, if the municipality had not been superseded under this section.
Bombay District Municipal (Saurashtra Area) and Bombay Municipal Boroughs (Saurashtra and Kutch Areas) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Municipal Boroughs Act, 1925 as adapted and applied to the Saurashtra area of the Gujarat State and that Act as extended to the Kutch area of the Gujarat State in sub-section (1) of section 219, the following words and figures shall be added at the end, namely:- "Such period may be longer than the term for which the councillors of the municipality would have held office under section 24 or 25, if the municipality had not been superseded under this section.".
