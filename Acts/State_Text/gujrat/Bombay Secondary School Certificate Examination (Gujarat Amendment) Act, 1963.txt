Bombay Secondary School Certificate Examination (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to amend the Bombay Secondary School Certificate Examination Act, 1948. It is hereby enacted in the Fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Secondary School Certificate Examination (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Secondary School Certificate Examination (Gurajat Amendment) Act, 1963. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Bombay Secondary School Certificate Examination (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the preamble to the Bombay Secondary School Certificate Examination Act, 1948 (hereinafter referred to as "the said Act"), after the words "high school education stage" the words "and the post basic education stage" shall be inserted.
Bombay Secondary School Certificate Examination (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In section 2 of the said Act,- (i) in clause (h) after the words "established by law in the State" the words "and includes a recognised post basic school" shall be inserted; (ii) after clause (h), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely:- "(hh) "recognised post basic school" means a post basic school recognised by the Director of Education, Gujarat State or an officer authorised by him in this behalf; (hhh) "secondary teachers" include teachers imparting instruction in recognised post basic schools;".
