Gujarat Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat Sales Tax Act, 1969. It is hereby enacted in the fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :
Gujarat Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Gujarat Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st April, 2001.
Gujarat Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat Sales Tax Act, 1969, in section 4A, in sub-section (1), for the words "one year", the words "two years", shall be substituted.
