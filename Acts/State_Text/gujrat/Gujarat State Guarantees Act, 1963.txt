Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to fix the limit upto which the executive power of the State shall extend to the giving of guarantees. It is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963.
Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat (1) The limit upto which the executive power of the State Government shall extend to the giving of guarantees (including the guarantees given befor the commencement of this Act) as provided in clause (1) of article 293 of the constitution shall be the sum of Rs. 60.00,00,000. (2) The State Government shall lay beforc the State Legislature. (a) a statement of any guarantee given as soon as may be after it is given. and (b) within three months after the end of any financial year in Which any guarantees so given are, in force; an account of the total sums, if any, which during that year have been either issued out of the Consolidated Fund of the State or paid in or towards repaymept of any sum so issued.
