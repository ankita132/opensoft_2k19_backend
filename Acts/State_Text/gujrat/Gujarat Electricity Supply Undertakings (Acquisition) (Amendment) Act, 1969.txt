Gujarat Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to amend the Gujarat Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1969. It is hereby enacted in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Gujarat Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1969.,
Gujarat Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1969 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in section 3, sub-section (2) and the Explanation shall be deleted.
Gujarat Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In section 4 of the principal Act, after sub-section (2), the following subsection (5) shall be added, namely: "(3) Where an order under sub-section (1) is made in respect of an undertaking of a licensee to whom notice has been served under section 6 of the Electricity Act prior to the making of such order, such notice shall lapse and be of no effect upon the making of such order.".
