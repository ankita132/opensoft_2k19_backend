Bombay Primary Education (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act, further to amend the Bombay Primary Education Act, 1947. It is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Primary Education (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Primary Education (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1963.
Bombay Primary Education (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 59A of the Bombay Primary Education Act, 1947, in sub-section (3), for the figures, letters and words "31st day of December 1963" the figures, letters and words "31st day of December 1964" shall be substituted.
