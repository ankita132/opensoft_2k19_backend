Saurashtra Rent Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Saurashtra Rent Control Act, 1951. It is her by enacted in the Thirteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Saurashtra Rent Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Saurashtra Rent Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962.
Saurashtra Rent Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 3 of the Saurashtra Rent Control Act, 1951, in sub-section (1) for the figures "1961" the figures "1964" shall be substituted.
