Bombay National Parks (Gujarat Repeal) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to repeal the Bombay National Parks Act, 1950 in its application to the State of Gujarat. It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay National Parks (Gujarat Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay National Parks (Gujarat Repeal) Act, 2004.
Bombay National Parks (Gujarat Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat The Bombay National Parks Act, 1950 in its application to the State of Gujarat is hereby repealed.
