Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1986.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In die Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in section 43, in sub-section (1), for the figures, letters and word "32U, or 43-1D" the figures, letters and word "32U, 43-1D or 88E" shall be substituted.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Second Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, section 64A shall be deleted.
