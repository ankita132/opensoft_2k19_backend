Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation Act, 1962 . It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981. (2) It shall come into force on such date, as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation Act, 1962, in section 12, in sub-section (2), for the figures "22"the figures "30"shall be substituted.
