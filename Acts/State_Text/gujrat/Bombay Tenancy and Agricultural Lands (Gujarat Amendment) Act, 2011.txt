Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend to the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948 (Bom. LXVII of 1948) (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in section 32M, in subsection (5),- (1) in clause (b), for the words and figures "before the end of December, 1986", the words "before the date specified from time to time by notification in the Official Gazette, by the State Government in this regard" shall be substituted; (2) in the Explanation, for the figures, letters and word "32PP and 32PPP", the figures, letters and word "32PP, 32PPP and 32QQ" shall be substituted.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in section 32PP, in sub-section (1 C), in clause (b) and in sub-section (1 D ), in clause (b), for the words and figures "before the end of December, 1986", the words, brackets, letters and figures, "before the date specified under clause (b) of sub-section (5) of section 32M" shall be substituted.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 4--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in section 32PPP, in sub-section (I), in clause (ii), in sub-clause (b) for the words and figures "before the end of December, 1986", the words, brackets, letters and figures "before the date specified under clause (b) of sub-section (5) of section 32M" shall be substituted.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 5--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in section 32QQ, in sub-section (1), in clauses (a) and (b) for the words and figures "before the end of December, 1986".wherever they occur, the words, brackets, letters and figures "before the date specified under clause (b) of sub-section (5) of section 32M" shall be substituted.
