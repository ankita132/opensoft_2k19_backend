Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Repeal) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat AN Act to repeal the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation Act, 1962 . It is hereby enacted in the Forty-eighty Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Repeal) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Repeal) Act, 1997.
Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation (Repeal) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat On and from the 1st April, 1997, the Gujarat Carriage of Goods Taxation Act, 1962 shall stand repealed.
