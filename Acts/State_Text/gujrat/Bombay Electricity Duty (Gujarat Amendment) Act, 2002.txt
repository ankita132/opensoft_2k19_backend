Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Electricity Duty Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2002. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st April, 2002.
Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Electricity Duty Act, 1958, in Schedule I, in Part I, in item (7), in column 2, for the figure and word "60 per cent.", the figure and word "45 per cent." shall be substituted.
