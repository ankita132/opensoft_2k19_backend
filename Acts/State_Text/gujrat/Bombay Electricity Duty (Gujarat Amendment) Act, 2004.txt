Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Electricity Duty Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called for Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2004. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st July, 2004.
Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Electricity Duty Act, 1958 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in section 3, in sub-section (2), after clause (iii), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(iii-a) in respect of pumping water for agricultural irrigation purposes including energy consumed for lighting the premises used as a pump house;".
Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in Schedule I, in Part I, item (6) and the Explanation thereunder shall be deleted.
Bombay Electricity Duty (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 4--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in Schedule II, in Part I, in item (6), in column 2, for the figures and words "20 paise per unit", the figures and words "40 paise per unit" shall be substituted.
