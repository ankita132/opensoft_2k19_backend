Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st day of April, 1981.
Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Sales of Motor spirit Taxation Act, 1958, in section 5A, in sub-section (1), for the words "at the rate of six paise in the rupee" the words "at the rate of ten paise in the rupee" shall be substituted.
