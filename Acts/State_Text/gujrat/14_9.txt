Gujarat Public Trusts (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat 1 An Act to repeal The Gujarat Public Trusts Act, 2011. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:- The following Act of the Gujarat Legislature received the assent of the Governor on 11th April, 2012 and was published in the Gujarat Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part IV, No. 10, dated 11th April, 2012.
Gujarat Public Trusts (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat Public Trusts (Repeal) Act, 2012.
Gujarat Public Trusts (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat The Gujarat Public Trusts Act, 2011 (Guj. 23 of 2011) is hereby repealed.
