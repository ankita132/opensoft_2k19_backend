Gujarat Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat Co-operative Societies Act, 1961. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2013.1 Received the assent of the President on 19th September, 2013
Gujarat Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat Co-operative Societies Act, 1961 (Guj. X of 1962), in section 17, in sub-section (1), after clause (a), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(aa) to amalgamate with another society registered under the Multi-State Co-operative Societies Act, 2002 (39 of 2002);".
