Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Stamp Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act 1996. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st April, 1996.
Bombay Stamp (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Stamp Act, 1958, in Schedule-I, in Article 6, in clause (2) in sub-clause (a), in item (ii), in column 2, for the words "One rupee for every hundred rupees", the words "Subject to a maximum of one lakh rupees, one rupee for every hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
