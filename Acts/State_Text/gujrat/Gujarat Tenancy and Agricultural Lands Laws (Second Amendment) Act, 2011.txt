Gujarat Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948 and the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region and Kutch Area) Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 2011.
Gujarat Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, in section 70, in clause (o), after the word "referred", the words "by the Slate Government" shall be inserted.
Gujarat Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region and Kutch Area) Act, 1958, in section 100, in clause (19), after the word "referred", the words "by the State Government" shall be inserted.
