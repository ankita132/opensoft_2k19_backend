Bombay Medical (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Medical Act, 1912. It is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Medical (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Medical (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963.
Bombay Medical (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 2B of the Bombay Medical Act, 1912, in sub-section (4), for the words beginning with the words "for a term" and ending with the words "by an order, appoint" the following shall be, and shall be deemed always to have been, substituted, namely:- "for such term not exceeding five years in the aggregate from the date of their nomination as the State Government may from time to time by order determine.".
