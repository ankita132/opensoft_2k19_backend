Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1964_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act, to amend the Bombay Khadi and Village Industries Act, 1960. It is hereby enacted in the Fifteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1964.
Bombay Khadi and Village Industries (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Khadi and Village Industries Act, 1960, after section 26, the following shall be inserted, namely:- "26A. Recovery of arrears. - If any amount due to the Board in accordance with the terms of a contract or otherwise or any sum payable in connection therewith, has not been paid, the amount or, as the case may be, the sum shall, without prejudice to any other remedy provided by law, be recoverable as an arrear of land revenue on a certificate in the prescribed form being issued by the Board to the Collector.".
