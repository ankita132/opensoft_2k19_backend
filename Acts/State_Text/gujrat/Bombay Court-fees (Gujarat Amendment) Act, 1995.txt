Bombay Court-fees (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Court-fees Act, 1959. It is hereby enacted in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Court-fees (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Court-fees (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1995. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
