Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963. It is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1963.
Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963, for the letters and figures "Rs. 60,00,00,000" the letters and figures "Rs. 1,00,00,00,000" shall be substituted.
