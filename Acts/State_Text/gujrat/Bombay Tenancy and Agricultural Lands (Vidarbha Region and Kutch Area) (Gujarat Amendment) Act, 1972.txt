Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region and Kutch Area) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region and Kutch Area) Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region and Kutch Area) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region and Kutch Area) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1972.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region and Kutch Area) (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 89 of the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region and Kutch Area) Act, 1958, in sub-section (1), after the existing proviso, the following further proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided further that no such permission shall be granted, where land is being sold to a person who is not an agriculturist for agricultural purpose, if the annual income of such person from other sources exceeds five thousand rupees".
