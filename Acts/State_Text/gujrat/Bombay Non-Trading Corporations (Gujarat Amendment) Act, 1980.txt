Bombay Non-Trading Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Non-Trading Corporations Act, 1959. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Non-Trading Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Non-Trading Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1980.
Bombay Non-Trading Corporations (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Non-Trading Corporations Act, 1959, in section 66, in sub-section (2), for the words "six annas" the words "one rupee" shall be substituted.
