Bombay Irrigation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Irrigation Act, 1879. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Irrigation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Irrigation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1982.
Bombay Irrigation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Irrigation Act, 1879 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in Part VI, the sub-heading "Irrigation Cess" and sections 56C, 56D, 56E and 56F thereunder shall be deleted.
Bombay Irrigation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in section 57, in sub-section (2), the words, figures and letter "and the amount of the irrigation cess if not paid on the date specified in section 56F" shall be deleted.
Bombay Irrigation (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 4--> State(s): Gujarat In the principal Act, in section 75, in sub-section (1), for the words, figures and letter "sections 49 to 56F" the words, figures and letter "sections 49 to 56B" shall be substituted.
