Gujarat Short Title (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat 1 An Act to amend the short title of Bombay Acts. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :- First published after having received the assent of the Governor, on 13-4-2011 and published in the Gujarat Government Gazette, Extraordinary, Part IV No. 15, dated 15-4-2011.
Gujarat Short Title (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat Short Title (Amendment) Act, 2011.
Gujarat Short Title (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires, the words "Bombay Act" mean the Act enacted by a competent legislature or other competent authority before 1st May, 1960 for the State of Bombay or, as the case may be the Province of Bombay and in force in the State of Gujarat on the Commencement of this Act.
Gujarat Short Title (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In the short title of each of the Bombay Acts specified in column 4 of the Schedule, for the word "Bombay" the word "Gujarat" shall be, and shall be deemed to have been substituted on 1st May, 1960.
Gujarat Short Title (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 4--> State(s): Gujarat Notwithstanding the amendment of short titles of Bombay Acts specified in column 4 of the Schedule by Sec. 3 the citation of those Acts by reference to the number and year respectively specified in column 2 and 3 of the Schedule against those Acts shall stand unmodified.
Gujarat Short Title (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 5--> State(s): Gujarat Unless the context otherwise requires, any reference to the short title of the Bombay Act specified in the Schedule, in any rules, regulation, bye- laws, scheme, notification or orders or in any other instrument made or issued under the said Bombay Act, shall be construed as a reference to the short title of the said Bombay Act as amended by this Act.
