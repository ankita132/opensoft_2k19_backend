Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, as in force in the State of Gujarat. In exercise of the powers conferred by section 3 of the Gujarat State Legislature (Delegation of Powers) Act, 1974, the President is pleased to enact as follows:-
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1974. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th day of December, 1973.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 32M of the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, as in force in the State of Gujarat (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in sub-section (5), for the words and figures "before the end of December, 1973", the words and figures "before the end of December, 1974" shall be substituted.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In section 32PP of the principal Act, in sub-sections (1C) and (1D), for the words, figures and letters "before 31st December, 1973", the words, figures and letters "before 31st December, 1974" shall be substituted.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 4--> State(s): Gujarat In section 32PPP of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (ii), for the words, figures and letters "before 31st December, 1973", the words, figures and letters "before 31st December, 1974" shall be substituted.
Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 5--> State(s): Gujarat (1) The Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Gujarat Amendment) Ordinance, 1973, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act. (V.V. Giri) President. (K. K. Sundaram) Secy. to the Govt. of India.
