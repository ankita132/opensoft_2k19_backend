Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may, be called the Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011. (2) It shall come into force from the 1st April, 2011.
Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Rents Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947 (Born. LVII of 1947) (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in Section 3, in sub-sec. (2), for the figures, letters and words "31st day of March, 2011" the figures, letters and words "31st day March, 2021" shall be substituted.
Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat In die principal Act, in Section 4, in sub-section (LA), the words "for a period often years from the date of the commencement of the amending Act" shall be deleted.
