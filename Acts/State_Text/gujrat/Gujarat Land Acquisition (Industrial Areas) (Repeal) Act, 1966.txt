Gujarat Land Acquisition (Industrial Areas) (Repeal) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An act to Repeal The Gujarat Land Acquisition (Industrial Areas) Act, 1961 . It is hereby enacted in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Gujarat Land Acquisition (Industrial Areas) (Repeal) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat Land Acquisition (Industrial Areas) (Repeal) Act, 1966.
Gujarat Land Acquisition (Industrial Areas) (Repeal) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat The Gujarat Land Acquisition (Industrial Areas) Act, 1961 is hereby repealed.
