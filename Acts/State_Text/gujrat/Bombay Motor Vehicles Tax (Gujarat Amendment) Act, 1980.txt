Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1980.
Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958, in the First Schedule, in Part I, in Class A, the Explanation appearing under clause VIII shall be re-numbered as Explanation 1 and after the Explanation I as so renumbered the following Explanation shall be added, namely:- "Explanation II.-It a motor vehicle is jointly owned by more persons than one, then notwithstanding anything contained in the proviso to sub-section (1) of section 24 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, such motor vehicle shall for purposes of clause I and clause VI, be deemed to be owned by a person other than an individual.".
