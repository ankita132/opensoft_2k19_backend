Bombay Land Requisition (Gujarat Extension of Duration) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act to extend the duration of the Bombay Land Requisition Act, 1948. It is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Land Requisition (Gujarat Extension of Duration) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Bombay Land Requisition (Gujarat Extension of Duration) Act, 1963.
Bombay Land Requisition (Gujarat Extension of Duration) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In section 3 of the Bombay Land Requisition Act, 1948, in sub-section (1), for the word and figures "December 1963" the word and figures "December 1966" shall be substituted.
