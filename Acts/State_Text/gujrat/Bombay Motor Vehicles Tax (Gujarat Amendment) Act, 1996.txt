Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958. It is hereby enacted in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1996. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st April, 1996.
Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958, in section 3A, in sub-section (1), in the Table, in entry 2, against clause (a), for the figures "3600" the figures "3000" shall be substituted.
