Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963 . It is hereby enacted in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat (1) This Act may be called the Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1989. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 2nd December, 1988.
Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963, in section 2, in sub-section (i), for the letters and figures "Rs. 35,00,00,00,000", the letters and figures "Rs. 45.00.00.00.000" shall be substituted.
Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Gujarat The Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Ordinance, 1988 is hereby repealed.
