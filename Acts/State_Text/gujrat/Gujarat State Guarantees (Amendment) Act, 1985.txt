Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> State(s): Gujarat An Act further to amend the Gujarat State Guarantee Act, 1963. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1--> State(s): Gujarat This Act may be called the Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1985.
Gujarat State Guarantees (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 2--> State(s): Gujarat In the Gujarat State Guarantees Act, 1963, in section 2, in sub-section (1) for the letters and figures "Rs. 25,00,00,00,000", the letters and figures "Rs, 35,00,00,00,000" shall be substituted.
