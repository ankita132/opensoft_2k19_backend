Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 20021 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 20th December, 2002.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh The clause (xix) of sub-section (1) of Section 20 of the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973 (No. 22 of 1973) shall be omitted.
