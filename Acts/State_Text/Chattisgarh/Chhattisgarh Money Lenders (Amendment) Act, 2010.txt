Chhattisgarh Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Money Lenders Act, 1934. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty first year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Chhattisgarh Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 2010.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 31st December, 2010. Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 7-1-2011.
Chhattisgarh Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh For sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Chhattisgarh Money Lenders Act, 1934 (No. 13 of 1934), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(2) It extends to the whole of Chhattisgarh except referred Scheduled Areas."
