Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Municipalities Act, 1961. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-sixth Year of Republic of India as follows:-
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2005.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 29th January, 2006. Published in C.G. Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 8-2-2006.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (1) of Section 72-B of The Chhattisgarh Municipalities Act, 1961 (No. 37 of 1961) for the words "six month" the words "as soon as possible" shall be substituted.
