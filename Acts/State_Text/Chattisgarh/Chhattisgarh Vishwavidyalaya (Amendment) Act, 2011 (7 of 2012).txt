Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Amendment) Act, 2011 (7 of 2012)_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Amendment) Act, 2011 (7 of 2012)_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Amendment) Act, 2011.1 (2) It extends to the whole State of Chhattisgarh. (3) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 18th January, 2012. Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 03-02-2012.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Amendment) Act, 2011 (7 of 2012)_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) In entries of column (4) of serial number 2 of Part-II (Revised) of the Second Schedule of the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973 (No. 22 of 1973), (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), for the words "the revenue District of Sarguja, Jashpur, Korea, Bilaspur, Korba, Janjgir-Champa and Raigarh" the words "the revenue Districts of Sarguja, Jashpur and Korea" shall be substituted. (2) After serial number 2 of Part-II (Revised) of the Second Schedule of the Principal Act, the following serial number and the entries relating thereto shall be added, namely :- (1) (2) (3) (4) "3. Bilaspur Vishwavidyalaya Bilaspur. Bilaspur Areas comprised within the limits of the revenue Districts of Bilaspur, Korba, Janjgir-Champa and Raigrah."
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Amendment) Act, 2011 (7 of 2012)_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh All the Statutes, Ordinances, Regulations in force before the commencement of the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Amendment) Act, 2011, in Sarguja Vishwavidyalaya, Sarguja shall be treated as Statutes, Ordinances, Regulations of Bilaspur Vishawavidyalya, Bilaspur.
