Chhattisgarh Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Irrigation Act, 1931. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 2012.1 (2) It extends to the whole State of Chhattisgarh. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force from 1st November, 2000. Received the assent of the Governor on the 7th January, 2013.
Chhattisgarh Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Chapter VI-A of the Chhattisgarh Irrigation Act, 1931 (No. III of 1931), relating to "Betterment Contribution", shall be omitted.
