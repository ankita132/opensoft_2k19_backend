Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act to amend the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows : -
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 17th August, 2005. Published in Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 25-8-2005.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In this Act unless context otherwise requires : - "Principal Act" means the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973 (No. 22 of 1973).
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Sub-section (xxv) of Section 4 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 4--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Sub-section (i) of Section 19 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 5--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Section 20 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 6--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Section 21 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 7--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Section 22 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 8--> State(s): Chhattisgarh For Section 23 of the Principal Act the following shall be inserted after sub-section (i) : - "(ia) Five members of the State Legislative Assembly to be nominated by State Legislative Assembly."
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 9--> State(s): Chhattisgarh For Section 47 of the Principal Act the following shall be substituted : - "The annual report of the University shall be passed by the Executive Council on or before such date as may be prescribed by the Statutes. The University shall thereafter, send a copy of the annual report to the State Government and the State Government shall, as soon as may be, cause the same to be laid on the table of the State Legislative Assembly".
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 10--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (2) of Section 48 of the Principal Act, the word "Court" shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 11--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (i) In sub-section (1) of Section 52 of the Principal Act, for the word and figures "20 to 25" the word and figures "23 to 25" shall be substituted. (ii) In Section 52 of the Principal Act, wherever the word "Court" occurs, it shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2005_Section 12--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In the para (2) of the Third Schedule of the Principal Act, Section 20, 21 and 22 and shall be omitted.
