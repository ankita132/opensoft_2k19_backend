Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam, 1976. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2014.1 (2) It shall come into force with effect from the 1st April, 2014. Received the assent of the Governor on the 20th March, 2014.
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh After clause (ff) of sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam, 1976 (No. 52 of 1976), (here-in-after referred to as the Principal Act), the following shall be inserted, namely:- "(fff) "Market Value" means the value at which the goods are generally sold in the market or where the goods are not generally sold in the market or the correct sale price thereof is not ascertainable, such value as may be notified by the State Government."
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2014_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh For sub-clause (ii) of clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 3 of the Principal Act, the following shall be substituted, namely :- "(ii) such tax, as may be notified by the State Government shall be levied from such persons or class of persons, to whom the provisions of sub-section "(1) do not apply, and thereupon the tax so notified, shall be paid by such persons or class of persons :"
