Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam, 1976 . Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows ;-
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of publication in the Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 17th April, 2002. Published in Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 23-04-2002.
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Schedule I of the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam, 1976 (No. 52 of 1976) (hereinafter referred as the Principal Act), for the entry in column (2) against Serial No. 1, the following shall be substituted "Goods specified in Schedule I of the Vanijyik Kar Adhiniyam except the goods specified in entries 4, 14 (excluding sugarcane) 15 and Atta, Maida, Suji, wheat bran specified in entry 5.".
