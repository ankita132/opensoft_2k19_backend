Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-second year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2002.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date which the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 19th January, 2002.
Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 135 of the Municipal Corporation Act, 1956 (No. 23 of 1956) for the words “Not more than ten percent”, the words “not more than twenty percent” shall be substituted.
