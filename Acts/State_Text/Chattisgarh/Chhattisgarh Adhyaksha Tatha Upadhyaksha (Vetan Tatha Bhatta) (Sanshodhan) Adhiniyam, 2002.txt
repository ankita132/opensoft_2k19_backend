Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyakasha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) Adhiniyam, 1972. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India a follows :-
Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (i) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002.1 (ii) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 19th April, 2002. Published in Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 24-04-2002
Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 4 of the Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) Adhiniyam, 1972 (No. 27 of 1972) (hereinafter referred to as Principal Act),- (1) In sub-section (1) of Section 4, after words "of a furnished", the words "office cum" shall be substituted; (2) Explanation after sub-section (1) of Section 4 shall be omitted; (3) Sub-Section (4) of Section 4 of the Principal Act shall be omitted; (4) In sub-section (5) of Section 4 of the Principal Act after the words "repairs and maintenance" the words "and furnishing" shall be added; (5) After sub-section (5) of the Principal Act, the following explanation shall be inserted, namely :- "Explanation.- For the purposes of this Section "residence" includes the staff quarters and other building appurtenant thereto and the garden thereof and "maintenance" in relation to a residence includes the payment of local rates and taxes and the provision of electricity and water.".
