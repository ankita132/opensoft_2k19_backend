Chhattisgarh Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act to amend the Chhattisgarh Money Lenders Act, 1934. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Chhattisgarh Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 2005.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 19th January, 2006. Published in C.G. Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 25-1-2006.
Chhattisgarh Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) In sub-section (1) of Section 11-C of the Principal Act,- For the figure and word "50 Rupees" the figure and word "1000 Rupees" shall be substituted. (2) In sub section (2) of Section 11-C of the Principal Act,- For the words "one year or two" the word "five" shall be substituted.
