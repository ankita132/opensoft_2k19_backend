Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001 . Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2007.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 11th August, 2007. Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 23-8-2007.
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 2 of the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001 (No. 7 of 2001) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act),- For clause (c), the following clause shall be substituted, namely :- “(c) “Medicine” means, Modem and Holistic Medicine and all its branches.”
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Act, 2007_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In the Principal Act and its Schedule where the words “Practitioner in Alternative Medicine” occur, they shall be substituted by the words “Practitioner in Modem and Holistic Medicine”.
