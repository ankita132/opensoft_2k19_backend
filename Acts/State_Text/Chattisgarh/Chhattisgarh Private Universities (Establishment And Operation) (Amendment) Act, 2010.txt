Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment And Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) Act, 2005. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-first year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment And Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2010. 1 (2) It extends to the whole State of Chhattisgarh. (3) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 6th May, 2010. Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 17-5-2010
Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment And Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh After sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) Act, 2005 (No. 13 of 2005), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely :- "Provided that after the establishment of the University any new teaching programme may be introduced with the prior approval of the State Government.".
