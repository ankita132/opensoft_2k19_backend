Chhattisgarh Vritti Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Vritti Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of publication in the Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 17th April, 2002 Published in Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 23-4-2002.
