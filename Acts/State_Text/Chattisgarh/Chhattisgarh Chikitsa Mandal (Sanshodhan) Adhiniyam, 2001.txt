Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act to amend the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001 . Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Sabha in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (i) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2001.1 (ii) It extends to the whole of Chhattisgarh. (iii) It shall come into force from such date as the State Government may, by notification appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 23rd August, 2001
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh The words "Practitioner in Alternative Medicine" shall be substituted wherever the words "Practitioner in modern medicine and surgery" appear in Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001 and its schedule.
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh The words "Practitioner in Alternative Medicine" shall be substituted wherever the words "Medicine and Surgery" appear in Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001.
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2001_Section 4--> State(s): Chhattisgarh The words "Practitioner in Alternative Medicine" shall be substituted wherever the words "Medical Diploma" appear in Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001.
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2001_Section 5--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Sub- section (c) of Section 2 of the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001 shall be substituted with the following :- "(c) ‘Medicine’ means, alternative medicine in all its branches, but does not include, Allopathy, Homeopathy, Ayurveda, Naturopathy and Unani Medicine and Veterinary Medicine and Surgery.".
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2001_Section 6--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) In clause (ii) of sub-section (1) of section 4 of the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001, the word "Five" shall be substituted with the word "Four". (2) Sub-clause (a) of clause (ii) of sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mandal Adhiniyam, 2001 shall be deleted.
