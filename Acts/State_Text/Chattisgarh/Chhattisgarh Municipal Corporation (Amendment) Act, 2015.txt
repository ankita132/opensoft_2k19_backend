Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 20151 . (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette. Published in C.G. Pajpatra (Asadharan) dated 18-8-2015.
Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh For clause (c) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956 (No. 23 of 1956), (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), the following shall be substituted, namely :- "(c) Persons not more than fifteen percent of the number of municipal wards in the town, nominate by the State Government, having special knowledge or experience-in the municipal administration : Provided that the minimum number of persons to be nominated under this clause shall be eight: Provided further that only a person residing within the municipal area and being otherwise not ineligible for election as a councillor may be nominated."
Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 308-A of the Principal Act,- (i) In sub-section (1),- (a) for the first proviso, the following shall be substituted, namely :- "Provided that in cases relating to unauthorized construction, the following compounding fees as mentioned in column (2) of the table below for the corresponding area as mentioned in column (1) of the said table, shall be charged, namely :- Table Plot area having unauthorized Construction Compounding Fees payable (1) (2) Upto 100 sq. meters. 15 times of permission fees Above 100 sq. meters but less than 200 sq. meters 20 times of permission fees Above 200 sq. meters but less than 300 sq. meters. 25 times of permission fees Above 300 sq. meters but less than 400 sq. meters. 30 times of permission fees Above 400 sq. meters but less than 500 sq. meters. 35 times of permission fees Above 500 sq. meters but less than 600 sq. meters. 40 times of permission fees Above 600 sq. meters but less than 700 sq. meters. 45 times of permission fees Above 700 sq. meters. 50 times of permission fees (b) tor the second proviso, the following shall be substituted, namely:- "Provided further that nothing contained in this section shall apply to any person, who does not have any right over the building or the land on which such construction has been made." (ii) Sub-section (2) shall stands deleted.
