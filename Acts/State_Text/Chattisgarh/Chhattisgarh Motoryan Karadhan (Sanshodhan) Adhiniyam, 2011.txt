Chhattisgarh Motoryan Karadhan (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Motoryan Karadhan (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2011.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification appoint.
