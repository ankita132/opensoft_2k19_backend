Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2005 (10 of 2005)_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-sixth Year of Republic of India as follows : -
Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2005 (10 of 2005)_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2005.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 8th August, 2005. Published in Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 25-8-2005.
Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2005 (10 of 2005)_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh For sub-section (1), (2), (3), (4) and (5) of Section 48-B of Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956 (No. 23 of 1956) the following shall be substituted, namely : - "(1) The Mohalla Committees shall be constituted within six months from the date of first meeting of the Council after the election of each Municipal Corporation. (2) The number of Mohalla Committees and determination of their territorial area, number of Members and functions, powers and the procedure for the conduct of business shall be determined in such manner as may be prescribed by the State Government.".
