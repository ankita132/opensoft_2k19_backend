Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Municipalities Act, 1961. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 20151 . (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette. Published in C.G. Pajpatra (Asadharan) dated 18-8-2015.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In clause (c) of sub-section (1) of Section 19 of the Chhattisgarh Municipalities Act, 1961 (No. 37 of 1961), (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), for the words "four" and "two", the words "five" and "three" shall be substituted respectively.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 187-A of the Principal Act,- (i) In sub-section (1),- (a) for the first proviso, the following shall be substituted, namely :- "Provided that in cases relating to unauthorized construction, the following compounding fees as mentioned in column (2) of the table below for the corresponding area as mentioned in column (1) of the said table, shall be charged, namely :- TABLE Plot area having unauthorized Construction Compounding Fees payable (1) (2) Upto 100 sq. meters. 15 times of permission fees Above 100 sq. meters but less than 200 sq. meters. 20 times of permission fees Above 200 sq. meters but less than 300 sq. meters. 25 times of permission fees Above 300 sq. meters but less than 400 sq. meters. 30 times of permission fees Above 400 sq. meters but less than 500 sq. meters. 35 times of permission fees Above 500 sq. meters but less than 600 sq. meters. 40 times of permission fees Above 600 sq. meters but less than 700 sq. meters. 45 times of permission fees Above 700 sq. meters. 50 times of permission fees (b) For third proviso, the following shall be substituted, namely :- "Provided further that nothing contained in his section shall apply to any person, who does not have any right over the building or the land on which such construction has been made." (ii) Sub-section (2) shall stand deleted.
