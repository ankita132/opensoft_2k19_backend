Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha, Neta Pratipaksha Vetan Bhatta Vidhi Sanshodhan Adiniyam, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) Adhiniyam, 1972, The Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) Adhiniyam, 1980. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows : -
Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha, Neta Pratipaksha Vetan Bhatta Vidhi Sanshodhan Adiniyam, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha, Neta Pratipaksha Vetan Bhatta Vidhi Sanshodhan Adiniyam, 2004.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 28th December, 2004 Published in Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 5-1-2005.
Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha, Neta Pratipaksha Vetan Bhatta Vidhi Sanshodhan Adiniyam, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 3 of the Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) Adhiniyam, 1972 (No. 27 of 1972),- (i) In sub-section (2) for the words "Three Thousand" the words "Five Thousand" shall be substituted, and (ii) In sub-section (3) for the words "Six Hundred" the words "Seven Hundred Sixty Seven" and for the words "Five Hundred" the words "Seven Hundred Fifty" shall be substituted.
Chhattisgarh Adhyaksha Tatha Upadhyaksha, Neta Pratipaksha Vetan Bhatta Vidhi Sanshodhan Adiniyam, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 4 of the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksha (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) Adhiniyam 1980 : - (i) In sub-section (2) for the words "Three Thousand" the words "Five Thousand" shall be substituted, and (ii) In sub-section (3) for the words "Six Hundred" the words "Seven Hundred Sixty Seven" shall be substituted.
