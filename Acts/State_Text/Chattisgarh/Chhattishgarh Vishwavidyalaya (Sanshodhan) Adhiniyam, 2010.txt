Chhattishgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Vishawavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Chhattishgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2010.1 (2) It extends to the whole State of Chhattisgarh. (3) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 8th June, 2010. Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 15-6-2010
Chhattishgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-clause (a) of Clause (xvii) of section 4 of the Chhattisgarh Vishawavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973 (No. 22 of 1973) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) for the word and figure "Part-I" the words and figure "Part-I (Revised)" shall be substituted.
Chhattishgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2010_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In the second proviso of sub-section (1) of Section 13 of the Principal Act, for the word and figure "Part-II" the words and figure "Part-II (Revised)" shall be substituted.
Chhattishgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2010_Section 4--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Principal Act, for the word "four" the word "five" shall be substituted.
Chhattishgarh Vishwavidyalaya (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2010_Section 5--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In proviso of sub-section (2-a) of Section 14 of the Principal Act, for the figure "65" the figure "70" shall be substituted.
