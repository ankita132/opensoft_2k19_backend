Chhattisgarh (Adhosanrachana Vikas Evam Paryavaran) Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh (Adhosanrachana Vikas Evam Paryavaran) Upkar Adhiniyam, 2005. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Chhattisgarh (Adhosanrachana Vikas Evam Paryavaran) Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh (Adhosanrachana Vikas Evam Paryavaran) Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 20131 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 31st July, 2013. Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated the 22nd August, 2013.
Chhattisgarh (Adhosanrachana Vikas Evam Paryavaran) Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In serial number 1 of column (2) of Schedule-I of the Chhattisgarh (Adhosanranchana Vikas Evam Paryavaran) Upkar Adhiniyam, 2005 (No. 7 of 2005), (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), for the words "On land covered under coal and iron ore mining leases", the words "On land covered under coal, iron ore, lime stone, bauxite and dolomite mining leases" shall be substituted.
Chhattisgarh (Adhosanrachana Vikas Evam Paryavaran) Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In serial number 1 of column (2) of Schedule II of the Principal Act, for the words "On land covered under coal and iron ore mining leases", the words "On land covered under coal, iron ore, lime stone, bauxite and dolomite mining leases" shall be substituted.
