Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sandhodhan) Adhiniyam, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam, 1976 . Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows : -
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sandhodhan) Adhiniyam, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (i) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sandhodhan) Adhiniyam, 2004.1 (ii) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 10th January, 2005 Published in C.G. Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 14-1-2005
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sandhodhan) Adhiniyam, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Schedule I of the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam, 1976 (No. 52 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), in column (2) against serial No. 1 after the words "bran" the words "and rice bran" shall be inserted.
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sandhodhan) Adhiniyam, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Schedule III of the Principal Act after entry No. 2 the following entry shall be inserted- "3. Rice bran-2".
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sandhodhan) Adhiniyam, 2004_Section 4--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Sandhodhan) Adhyadesh, 2004 (No. 2 of 2004) is hereby repealed.
