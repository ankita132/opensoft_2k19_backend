Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mahavidyalayon Ke Snatkottar Pathyakrmon Main Pravesh Adhiniyam, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act to regulate admission to Postgraduate Medical courses in the Medical Colleges of Chhattisgarh. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mahavidyalayon Ke Snatkottar Pathyakrmon Main Pravesh Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mahavidyalayon Ke Snatkottar Pathyakrmon Main Pravesh Adhiniyam, 2002.1 (2) It shall extend to the whole of Chhattisgarh, (3) It shall come into force in the Official Gazette of Chhattisgarh.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 24th October, 2002. Published in Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 06-11-2002
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mahavidyalayon Ke Snatkottar Pathyakrmon Main Pravesh Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh The expression "State Government" shall include, and be deemed to have always included, the State Government as it preexisted prior to 1st November, 2000 in relation to area now comprised in the State of Chhattisgarh.
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mahavidyalayon Ke Snatkottar Pathyakrmon Main Pravesh Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh The admission to Postgraduate courses in the Medical Colleges of Chhattisgarh shall be regulated by rules made by the State Government.
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mahavidyalayon Ke Snatkottar Pathyakrmon Main Pravesh Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 4--> State(s): Chhattisgarh The State Government may make rules from time to time to carry out the purposes of this Act, and may also amend such rules from time to time. (2) All the rules made under this Act shall be laid on the table of the Vidhan Sabha.
Chhattisgarh Chikitsa Mahavidyalayon Ke Snatkottar Pathyakrmon Main Pravesh Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 5--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) The Chhattisgarh Medical Snatkottar Pravesh Pareeksha Niyam, 2002, shall be deemed to be rules made by the State Government under this Act. (2) All actions taken and acts done under the Chhattisgarh Medical Snatkottar Pravesh Pareeksha Niyam, 2002 even before the commencement of this Act shall be deemed to be actions taken and acts done under this Act.
