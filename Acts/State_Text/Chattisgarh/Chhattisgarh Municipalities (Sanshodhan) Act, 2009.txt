Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Sanshodhan) Act, 2009_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act to amend the Chhattisgarh Municipalities Act, 1961. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixtieth Year of the Republic of India :-
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Sanshodhan) Act, 2009_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Sanshodhan) Act, 2009.1 (2) It extends to the whole of State of Chhattisgarh. (3)It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 9th September, 2009 Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asdharan) dated 03-10-2009
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Sanshodhan) Act, 2009_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Clause (q) of Section 35 of the Chhattisgarh Municipalities Act, 1961 (No. 37 of 1961) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Sanshodhan) Act, 2009_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Clause (ee) of sub-section (1) of Section 38 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
