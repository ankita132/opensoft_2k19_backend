Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Municipalities Act, 1961. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Adhiniyam, 2003.1 (2) It shall come into force from date of its notification in the Official Gazette. Received the assent of the Governor on the 21st April, 2003.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 322 of Chhattisgarh Municipalities Act, 1961 (No. 37 of 1961) hereinafter called Principal Act, for words "Divisional Commissioner" the words "Director Urban Planning and Development" shall be substituted.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (1) of Section 323 of the Principal Act, for words "Divisional Commissioner" the words "Director Urban Planning and Development" shall be substituted.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 4--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (1) and (2) of Section 331 of the Principal Act, for words "Divisional Commissioner" the words "Director Urban Planning and Development" shall be substituted.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 5--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (1) and (2) of Section 332 of the Principal Act, for the word "Commissioner" the words "Director Urban Planning and Development" shall be substituted.
Chhattisgarh Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 6--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (2) of Section 345 of the Principal Act, for the word "Commissioner" the words "Director Urban Planning and Development" shall be substituted.
