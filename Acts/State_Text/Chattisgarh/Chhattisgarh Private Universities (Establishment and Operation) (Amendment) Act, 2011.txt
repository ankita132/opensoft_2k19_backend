Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) Act, 2005. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2011.1 (2) It extends to the whole State of Chhattisgarh. (3) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 3rd May, 2011. Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 5-5-2011.
Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh After clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 7 of the Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) Act, 2005 (No. 13 of 2005) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely : - "Provided that nontransferable lease (not less than 30 years), given for the purpose, by State Government or its Agency shall be admissible."
Chhattisgarh Private Universities (Establishment and Operation) (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In proviso to sub-section (2) of Section 9 of the Principal Act. for the figure and word "6 months" the figure and word "2 years" shall be substituted.
