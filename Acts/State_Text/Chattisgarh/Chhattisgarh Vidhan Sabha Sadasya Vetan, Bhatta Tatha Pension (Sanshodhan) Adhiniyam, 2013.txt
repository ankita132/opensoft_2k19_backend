Chhattisgarh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension, Adhiniyam, 1972. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the official gazette2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 26th April, 2013. Published in the "Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan)", dated the 6th May, 2013.
Chhattisgarh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Section 6-C of the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension, Adhiniyam, 1972 (No. 7 of 1973), for the words 'Two Thousand Five Hundred" the words "Ten Thousand" shall be substituted.
