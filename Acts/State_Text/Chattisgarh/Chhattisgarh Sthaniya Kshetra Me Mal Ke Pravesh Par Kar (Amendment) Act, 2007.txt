Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Act, 1976. Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Amendment) Act, 2007.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 17th August, 2007 Published in the Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 29-8-2007
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In Schedule II of the Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam 1976 (No. 52 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act),- For the entry (a) in column number (2) of serial number 22, the following entry shall be substituted :- “(a) Pan Masala containing Tobacco”.
Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Chhattisgarh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar (Amendment) Ordinance, 2007 (No. 1 of 2007) is hereby repealed.
