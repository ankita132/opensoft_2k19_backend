Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksh (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Chhattisgarh An Act further to amend the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksh (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) Adhiniyam, 1980 . Be it enacted by the Chhattisgarh Legislature in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksh (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Chhattisgarh (1) This Act may be called the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksh (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002.1 (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Gazette.2 Received the assent of the Governor on the 19th April, 2002. Published in Chhattisgarh Rajpatra (Asadharan) dated 24-04-2002
Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksh (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksh (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) Adhiniyam, 1980 (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) after the words "of a furnished" the words "office cum" shall be added.
Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksh (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 3--> State(s): Chhattisgarh Sub-section (4) of Section 5 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Chhattisgarh Vidhan Mandal Neta Pratipaksh (Vetan Tatha Bhatta) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2002_Section 4--> State(s): Chhattisgarh In sub-section (5) of Section 5 of the Principal Act after the words "repairs and maintenance" the words "and furnishing" and before the word "residence" wherever it occurs including in explanation of this section the words "office cum" shall be added.
