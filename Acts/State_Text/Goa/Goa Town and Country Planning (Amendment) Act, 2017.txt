Goa Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Goa An Act further to amend the Goa, Daman and Diu Town and Country Planning Act, 1974 (Act No. 21 of 1975). Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of Goa in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Goa Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Goa (1) This Act may be called the Goa Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Goa Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Goa In section 16A of the Goa, Daman and Diu Town and Country Planning Act, 1974 (Act No. 21 of 1975) (hereinafter referred to as the "principal Act"), in sub-section (2), for the expression "fine which may extend to Rs. 1.00 lakh", the expression "simple imprisonment which may extend to one year, or with fine of Rs. 10.00 lakh, or with both" shall be substituted.
Goa Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Goa In section 17B of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the expression "Rs. 1.00 lakh", the expression "Rs. 10.00 lakhs" shall be substituted.
Goa Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 4--> State(s): Goa In section 49 of the principal Act, in sub-section (6),- (i) for the expression "within a planning area", the expression "within a planning area or from the Town and Country Planning Department within a area other than a planning area" shall be substituted; (ii) for the expression "in respect of the planning area", the expression "in respect of the planning area or from the Chief Town Planner (Planning) or such officer as may be authorised by him by notification in the Official Gazette, in respect of such other area" shall be substituted. (Sudhir Mahajan) Secretary to the Government of Goa Law Department (Legal Affairs). Secretariat, Porvorim, Goa. Dated: 6-2-2018.
