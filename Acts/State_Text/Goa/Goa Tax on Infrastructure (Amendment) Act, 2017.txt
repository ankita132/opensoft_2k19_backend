Goa Tax on Infrastructure (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Goa An Act further to amend the Goa Tax on Infrastructure Act, 2009 (Goa Act 20 of 2009). Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of Goa in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Goa Tax on Infrastructure (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Goa (1) This Act may be called the Goa Tax on Infrastructure (Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Goa Tax on Infrastructure (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Goa In section 2 of the Goa Tax on Infrastructure Act, 2009 (Goa Act 20 of 2009) in clause (k), after the expression "home for spastic/retarded children", the expression "or housing for locals under the Government Scheme" shall be inserted. (Sudhir Mahajan) Secretary to the Government of Goa Law Department (Legal Affairs). Secretariat, Porvorim, Goa. Dated: 6-2-2018.
