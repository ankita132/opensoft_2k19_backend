Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, and to validate the levy, assessment, reassessment and collection of tax on sales of monoblock pumping sets BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana (1) This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982. (2) The following provisions of this Act shall be deemed to have come into force on the dates specified against them and the remaining provisions thereof shall come into force at once: -Section 3 1-1-1969 Section 6 9-4-1979 Section 7 1-4-1965.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana After clause (d) of second proviso to sub-section (2) of section 6 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following clause shall be inserted, namely: -"(e) who deals in foreign liquor (Indian made foreign liquor and foreign liquor), the liability to pay tax shall commence from the date on which he deals in such goods.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (2) of section 15 of the principal Act, the following words shall be inserted at the end, namely: -" and thereafter from 1st January, 1969 to the 16th November, 1976, at the rate of six paise in a rupee; and from 17th November, 1976 to the 31st August, 1981, at the rate of seven paise in a rupee.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana Tax in case of goods specified in Schedule C shall be leviable and payable on the sale or purchase thereof at the stage and subject to the conditions indicated in the said Schedule.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana After sub-section (2) of section 27 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely: -"(3) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, the taxable turnover of any dealer for any period on or after the first day of April, 1982, shall not include his turnover during that period in respect of any sale or purchase of foreign liquor (Indian made foreign liquor and foreign liquor) at any stage other than that specified in Schedule C; provided he furnishes a certificate or declaration in such form as may be prescribed.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana In section 28 of the principal Act, -(i) in sub-section (4), for the words "within three years after the expiry of such period,", the words "within five years after the expiry of such period, proceed to" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (5), for the words "within three years after the expiry of such period, after giving the dealer a reasonable opportunity of being heard,", the words within five years after the expiry of such period, after giving the dealer a reasonable opportunity of being heard, proceed to" shall be substituted; and (iii) sub-section (5A) shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 7--> State(s): Haryana For section 28A of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely: -" 28A. Dispensing with second notice, etc - Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained in this Act or any other law or judgement or order of any court or any authority, in respect of cases relating to assessment for the period prior to 1st April, 1979, -( i) no second notice shall be required to be issued, -(a) under sub-section (4) or (5) of section 28; (b) under sub-section (4) and (5) of section 11 of the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948; and (ii) it shall be necessary, to intimate the basis for arriving at best of judgement assessment or to take any other step for proceeding to assess to the best of judgement within the period of five years as specified in the provisions mentioned in sub-clause(a) and (b) above.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 8--> State(s): Haryana For the existing proviso to sub-section (1) of section 36 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely: -" Provided that the books, documents and accounts of a period of more than five years prior to the year in which assessment is made shall not be so required except in case where some proceedings under the Act are pending.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 9--> State(s): Haryana Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained in any judgement, decree or order of any court or other authority, any, levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of any amount by way of tax or penalty made or purporting to have been made in respect of monoblock pumping sets, for the period commencing on the 1st January, 1969, and ending with the 31st August, 1981, and any action taken or thing done or purporting to have been taken or done in relation to such levy, assessment, reassessment or collection shall be deemed to be as valid and effective as if such levy, assessment, assessment or collection had been made or action taken or things done under the principal Act, and accordingly, -(a) all acts, proceedings or things done or action taken by the State Government or by any officer of the State Government or by any authority, in collection of such tax or penalty shall, for all purposes be deemed to be, and to have always been, done or taken in accordance with law; (b) no suits or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or before any authority for tenderer refund of such tax or penalty so collected; and (c) no court or authority shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such tax or penalty so collected.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1982_Section 10--> State(s): Haryana The Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1982 (Haryana Ordinance No. 2 of 1982), is hereby repealed. R.K.GUPTA, Joint Secretary to Government, Haryana,
