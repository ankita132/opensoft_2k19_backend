Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 and validate the imposition of certain taxes. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation). ACT, 1986
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In section 2 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the principal Act), - (a) In sub-clause (ii) of clause (j), the words and figures "entered into on or after the18th day of April, 1984" shall be inserted at the end; and (b) In sub-clause (ii) of clause (1), the words and figures" entered into on or after the 18 th day of April, 1984" shall be inserted at the end;and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 18 th April, 1984.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1986_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana For entry 27 of Schedule B of the Principal Act, the following entry shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 5 th May, 1973 namely : "27 Fertilizers excluding oil-cakes, crushed bones, bone meals, bone powder, bone grists and bone sinews."
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1986_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana Notwithstanding any judgement, decree or order of any court or other authority to be contrary, any levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of any amount by way of tax or penalty made or purporting to have been made in respect of crushed bones, bone meals, bone powder, bone grists and bone sinews for the period commencing on the 5 th May, 1973 and ending with the commencement of the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1986 and any action taken or thing done or purporting to have been taken or done in relation to such levy, assessment or collection shall be deemed to be as valid and effective as if such levy, assessment , reassessment or collection had been made or action taken or things done under the principal Act, and accordingly. a) all acts, proceedings or things done or action taken by the State Government or by any officer of the State Government or by any authority, in connection with the levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of such a tax or penalty shall, for all purposes be deemed to be ,and to have always been, done or taken in accordance with law : b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or before any authority for the refund of any such tax or penalty so collected ; and c) no court or authority shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such tax or penalty so collected. SHIV CHARAN DAS BAJAJ Secretary to Government , Haryana
