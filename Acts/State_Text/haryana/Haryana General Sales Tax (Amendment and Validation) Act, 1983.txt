Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 and to validate the levy, assessment and collection etc. of tax in certain cases. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In clause (g) of section 2 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 hereinafter called the principal Act), the words "or vehicle or any road transport when plying in collaboration with rail transport when plying in collaboration with rail transport and under the control of the Railways" shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In section 9 of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (I) ,-(i) for clause (b)the following clause shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted for the period commencing from the 27 th day of May,1971and ending with the 8th day of April, 1979, namely :-" (b) purchase goods, other than those specified in Schedule B, from any source in the State and uses them in the State in the manufacture of any other goods and either disposes of the manufactured goods in any manner otherwise than by way of sale in the State or despatches the manufactured goods to a place outside the State in any manner otherwise than by way of sale in the course of inter-state trade or commerce or in the course of export outside the territory of India within the meaning of sub-section (I) of section 5 of the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956; or"; (ii) after clause (b), the following clause shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 9th day of April, 1979,namely :- "(bb) Purchase goods, other than those specified in Schedule B except milk, from any source in the State and uses them in the State in the manufactured of any other goods and either disposes of the manufactured goods in any manner otherwise than by way of sale in the State or despatches the manufactured goods to a place outside the State in any manner otherwise than byway of sale in the course of inter-State trade or commerce or in the course of export outside the territory of India within the meaning of sub-section (I) of section 5 of the Central Sales Tax Act,1956; or"; (iii) the following proviso shall be added, namely :- " provided that no tax shall be leviable under this section on scientific goods and guar gum, manufactured in the State and sold by him in the course of export outside the territory of India within the meaning of sub-section (3) of section 5 of the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956"; and (b) in sub-section (3), the words "other than Railway premises" shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana Existing section 24 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (I) thereof -(a) in sub-section (I) so renumbered -(i) to clause (a),the following proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided that a dealer registered under this Act, shall also be entitled to purchase barley, without payment of sales tax on the authority of his' certificate of registration, on furnishing to the selling registered dealer, a declaration referred to above, for sale by him in the course of export outside the territory of India within the meaning of section 5 of the Central Sales Tax Act,1956" ; and (ii) to clause (b), the following proviso shall be added, namely :- " Provided that a dealer registered under this Act, shall also be entitled to purchase, without payment of sales tax, on the authority of his certificate of registration, goods mentioned in clause (b) above, on furnishing to the selling registered dealer a declaration referred to above, for use by him in the manufacture, in the State, of scientific goods and guar gum for the purpose of sale by him in the course of export outside the territory of India within the meaning of sub-section (3) of section5 of the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956";and (b) after sub-section (I) so renumbered, the following sub-section shall be inserted and deemed to have been inserted with effect from the first day of April, 1976, namely :-"(2)Notwithstanding anything contained in Form S.T. 15 or the certificate of registration issued under this Act or the rules made thereunder, no dealer shall be entitled to claim the right envisaged in sub-section (I) so renumbered for the period from the first day of April, 1976 to the third day of September, "1979, in contravention of the provisions of sub-section (I) so renumbered".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana In section 33 of the principal Act, for the words "one year", the words "two years" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana In section 37 of the principal Act, - (i) in sub-sections (1), (2) and (3), the words "other than Railway premises" shall be omitted; (ii) in sub-sections (14) and(15), the words "a rail head or" shall be omitted ; and (iii) sub-section (16) shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 7--> State(s): Haryana In clause (ii) of the Explanation to section 38 of the principal Act, for the words "other than a rail head or a post office", the words "or of a Railway out-agency, city booking office or city booking agency, when run by a private person under a contract with the "Railways but excluding a rail head or a post office" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 8--> State(s): Haryana Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained in any judgement, decree or order of any court or other authority ,- (i) the notifications issued and deemed to have been issued under section 15 read with section 9 of the principal Act ; and (ii) any levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of any amount by way of tax or penalty made or purporting to have been made under the provisions of the principal Act for the period on or after the twenty-seventh day of May,1971 and any action taken or thing done or purporting to have been taken or done in relation to such levy, assessment, reassessment or collection ; It shall be deemed to be valid and effective as if the said notifications were issued and such levy, assessment, reassessment or collection had been made or action taken or things done under the principal Act and accordingly :- a) all acts, proceedings or things done or action taken by the State Government or by any officer of the State Government or by any authority, in connection with the issue of notifications, levy, assessment , reassessment or collection of such tax or penalty shall, for all purposes be deemed to be and to have always been, done or taken in accordance with law : b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or before any authority for the refund of any such tax or penalty so collected ; and c) no court or authority shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such tax or penalty so collected.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1983_Section 9--> State(s): Haryana The Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation ) Ordinance ,1983 (Haryana Ordinance No.1 of 1983), is hereby repeated. SHIV CHARAN DAS BAJAJ Secretary to Government , Haryana
