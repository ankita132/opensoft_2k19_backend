Haryana Cooperative Sugar Mills (Constitution and Tenure of Committees) Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to provide for the constitution of first committees and extension of tenure of committees already constituted under the Punjab Co-operative Societies Act, 1961, for the Co-operative Sugar Mills in which the State Government has a financial interest. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Twenty Sugar Mills (Constitution and Tenure of Committees) Act, 1971.
Haryana Cooperative Sugar Mills (Constitution and Tenure of Committees) Act, 1971_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana Cooperative Sugar Mills (Constitution and Tenure of Committees) Act, 1971.1 Received the assent of the Governor of Haryana on the 11th November, 1971 and was first published in Haryana Government Gazette (Extraordinary) of the 11th November, 1971.
Haryana Cooperative Sugar Mills (Constitution and Tenure of Committees) Act, 1971_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained in the Punjab Co-operative Societies Act, 1961, or the rules made there under or the bye laws made by any Co-operative Sugar Mills in which the State Government has a substantial financial interest :- (a) the State Government may, by notification, constitute, by nomination, the first committee of any Co-operative Sugar Mill, consisting of such number of members not exceeding eleven, out of whom not less than one- third shall be shareholders; (b) the tenure of the committees of such Co-operative Sugar Mills constituted under the said Act, or under this Act, shall be four years from the date of constitution thereof.
Haryana Cooperative Sugar Mills (Constitution and Tenure of Committees) Act, 1971_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana The other provisions of the Punjab Co-operatives Societies Act, 1961 and the rules made thereunder shall, mutatis mutandis, apply to the Co-operative Sugar Mills.
