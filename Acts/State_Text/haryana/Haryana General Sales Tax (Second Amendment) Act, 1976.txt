Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana BE it enacted by the legislature of the State of Haryana in the Twenty-seventh year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana (1) This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976. (2) Sections 2, 3 and 5 of this Act shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th day of May, 1973, and the remaining provisions thereof shall come into force at once.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana (After Section 16 of Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), the following section shall be inserted namely: -" 16A.Tax on goods specified in Schedule C-Tax in case of goods specified in Schedule C shall be leviable and payable on the purchase thereof at the stage indicated in the said schedule."
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In Section 24 of the Principal Act, for the words "without payment of tax", words "without payment of sales tax" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (4) of Section 37 of the principal Act, the words "the owner of goods shall produce the said copy before the Assessing Authority at the time of assessment." Shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana For Schedule C to the principal Act, the following Schedule shall be substituted, namely: -" Schedule C - (See Section 16 A) Serial Description of goods Stage of levy of tax No. 1. Resin (Crude pine gum) Last purchase within the State by a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act. 2. Paddy Last purchase within the State by a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act."
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana (1) The Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Haryana Ordinance No. 1 of 1976), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act as if this Act had come into force on tenderer 5th day of May, 1973. PART II HARYANA GOVERNMENT EXCISE AND TAXATION DEPARTMENT The 23rd February, 1976 Pronugated by the Governor of Haryana in the Twenty-seventh year of the Republic of India. Whereas the Legislature of the State of Haryana is not in session and the Governor of Haryana is satisfied that circumstances exist which render it necessary for him to take immediate action; Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of article 213 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Haryana thereby promulgates the following ordinance: - 1.Short title and commencement: - (1) This ordinance may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1976. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th day of May, 1973. 2. Insertion of section 16A in Haryana Act 20 of 1973. After Section 16 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following section shall be inserted, namely: -" 16A.Tax on goods specified in Schedule C-Tax in case of goods specified in Schedule C shall be leviable and payable on the purchase thereof at the stage indicated in the said schedule." 3. Amendment of Section 24 of Haryana Act 20 of 1973, -In section 24 of the principal Act, for the words "without payment of tax", the words "without payment of sales tax" shall be substituted.4.Substitution of Schedule C to Haryana Act 20 of 1973 For Schedule C to the principal Act, the following schedule shall be substituted, namely: - "Schedule C (See Section 16A) Serial No. Description of goods Stage of levy of tax 1. Resin (Crude pine gum) Last purchase within the State by a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act. 2. Paddy Last purchase within the State by a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act.
