Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973.BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Twenty - Eight year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (1) of section 16 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the Principal Act), for the words "two", the words "fifteen" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana (1) The Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1977 (Haryana Ordinance No. 10 of 1977), is hereby repealed. (2)Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done taken under the Principal Act, as amended by this Act, as if this Act had come into force on the 1st day of September, 1977.
