Haryana General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-seventh year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1986.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana After proviso to sub-section (1) of section 9 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, the following further proviso shall be added, namely: -" Provided further that no tax shall be leviable under this section where the goods are taken outside the State for job work or processing by a dealer and such goods after job work or processing are brought back by the dealer in the State, subject to the dealer furnishing a declaration in such form and manner as may be prescribed.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana The Haryana General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Ordinance, 1986 (Haryana Ordinance No. 1 of 1986), is hereby repealed. SHIV CHARAN DASS BAJAJ, Secretary to Government, Haryana
