Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1996.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana After clause (bb), in section 7 of Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the principal Act), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely: -"(bc) in relation to any dealer who extracts or processes or deals in minerals including crushed, cut, broken into pieces and pulverised minerals. Nil (bd) in relation to any dealer who dealer in lottery tickets Nil".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In section 25 of principal Act, in sub-section (2A), for the words "five lakhs", the words "one lakh" shall be substituted. B.L.GUPTA, Secretary to Government, Haryana,
