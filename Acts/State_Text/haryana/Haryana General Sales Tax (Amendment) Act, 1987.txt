Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 . BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-eight Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1987.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In section 2 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the principal Act), -(a) in clause (j), the existing note shall be renumbered as note 1 thereof and after note 1 as so renumbered, the following note shall be added, namely: -" Note 2. - Sub-clause (ii), and sub-clause (iv) so far as it relates to the goods, namely, shuttering material (used in construction of buildings), tents, kanats, chholdari, crockery, utensils, furniture, and all other goods dealt with by the tent dealer as also other allied dealer for decoration and lighting purpose, electricity meters and water meters, shall come into force with effect from the 1 st day of April, 1987;" (b) in clause (1), the existing note shall be renumbered as note 1 thereof and after note 1 as so renumbered, the following note shall be added, namely: -" Note 2. - Sub-clause (ii), and sub-clause (iv) so far as it relates to the goods, namely, shuttering material (used in construction of buildings), tents, kanats, chholdari, crockery, utensils, furniture, and all other goods dealt with by the tent dealer as also other allied dealer for decoration and lighting purpose, electricity meters and water meters, shall come into force with effect from the 1 st day of April, 1987;";
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana After clause (iv) in the proviso to sub-section (1) of section 15 of the principal Act, the following clauses shall be inserted, namely: - "(v) in the case of goods involved in the execution of a works contract entered into on or after the 18 th day of April,1984 and goods, namely, shuttering material (used in construction of buildings), tents, kanats, chholdari, crockery, utensils, furniture, and all other goods dealt with by the tent dealer as also other allied dealers for decoration and lighting purposes, electricity meters and water meters, in which right to use has been transferred, where the dealer has not charged tax during the period commencing from the 18 th day of April, 1984 and ending on the 31 st day of March, 1987, he shall not be liable to pay tax. (vi) in the case of crushed bones, bone meals, bone powder, bone grists and bone sinews, where the dealer has not charged tax during the period commencing from the 5 th day of May, 1973 and ending on the 31 st day of March, 1986, he shall be liable to pay tax. (vii) In the case of sale of goods packed in jute bags, the value of such bags, whether charged separately or not, shall be charged at the rate at which such goods are taxable.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana To entry 27 of Schedule B to the principal Act, the following note shall be inserted, namely: -" Note. - Entry as substituted by Haryana Act No. 7 of 1986, shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 1 st of April, 1986".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana For sub-section (5) of section 39 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely: - "(5) No appeal shall be entertained unless it is filed within sixty days from the date of the order appealed against and the appellate authority is satisfied that the amount of tax assessed, and the interest, if any, recoverable from the dealer, has been paid and he has furnished a personal surety and security as may be prescribed for payment of the penalty, if any, imposed: Provided that in the case of an appeal against any which has to be communicated by the appropriate authority to the assessee, the period of sixty days shall commence from the date of receipt of the copy of the order by the assessee and in case of an appeal against any other made under this Act, the time spent in obtaining the certified copy of order shall be excluded in computing the period of sixty days.". SHIV CHARAN DAS BAJAJ, Secretary to Government, Haryana,
