Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana After clause (e) of the second proviso to sub-section (2) of section 6 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the principal Act), the following clause shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 1st day January, 1988, namely: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana After 13-A of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted namely: -" 13-B. Power to exempt certain class of industries - The State Government may, if satisfied that it is necessary or expedient so to do in the interest of industrial development of the State, exempt such class of industries from the payment of sales tax, for such period and subject to such conditions as may be prescribed."
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana Section 14 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) thereof and after sub-section (1) as so renumbered, the following sub-section shall be inserted, and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 5th day of May, 1973, namely: -"(2) Notwithstanding any judgement, decree, or order of any court or other authority to the contrary, the burden of proof that the purchased of goods from within the State from my source without payment of tax is not liable to tax under section 9 or section 24, shall be discharged by the person making the purchase by proving that the goods so purchased or the goods manufactured there from have actually been sold either: (a) within the State; (b) in the course of inter-State trade or commerce: or (c) in the course of export out of territory of India : Provided that if he fails to so prove, he shall be liable to pay tax under section 9 or section 24 on his purchase turnover calculated on pro-rata basis according to the following formula: -Purchases from X(Transfer + consignment Sales within the State sale of Schedule B goods manufactured by the dealer) Gross turnover.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana To sub-section (2) of section 36 of the principal Act, the following provisos shall be added, namely: -" Provided that when the dealer contravenes the provisions of clause (a) or clause (d), he shall be liable to pay a penalty to be imposed by an officer referred to in sub-section (1), not exceeding rupees five hundred but not less than rupees two thousand: Provided further that no penalty shall be imposed unless the dealer concerned has been given a reasonable opportunity of being heard."
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana After clause (f) of sub-section (2) of Section 64 of the principal Act, the following clause shall be inserted, namely: -"(ff) class of industries, period of exemption and conditions of such exemption under section 13-B"
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 7--> State(s): Haryana In Schedule A to the principal Act, for entry 17, the following entry shall be substituted, namely: -" 17. Cosmetics, perfumery and toilet goods, including shaving brush, shaving brush, shaving cream and shaving blades but not including tooth paste, tooth powder and tooth brush, soap, kum-kum, dhoop and aggarbattis".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 8--> State(s): Haryana In Schedule B to the principal Act,-(a) in entry 15, the words "on which additional excise duty in lieu of sales tax is levied" shall be omitted and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the 1st day of January, 1988' (b) notwithstanding any judgement, decree, or order of any court or other authority to the contrary, below entry 34 as it exceeded prior to the 1st day of January, 1988, the following explanation shall be added and shall be deemed to have been added with effect from the 5th day of May, 1973, namely: - "Explanation.- Fodder(dry or green) shall not include rice bran." SURINDER SARUP, Secretary to Government, Haryana
