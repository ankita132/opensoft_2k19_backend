Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, and to validation levy and collection of tax on certain goods and the proceedings connected therewith. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-Second year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (5) of section 25 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter referred to as theprincipal Act), for the existing proviso, the following provisos shall be substituted, namely: -" Provided that where the amount of tax not paid as required under sub-section (3) does not exceed five hundred rupees, the interest payable thereon shall not exceed the amount of the tax not so paid: Provided further that for the purposes of calculation of interest, a period of fifteen days or more shall be deemed to be one month and the amount of fifty rupees or more shall be deemed to be one hundred rupees and a period of less than fifteen days and an amount of less than rupees shall be ignored.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In section 37 of the principal Act, -(a) in sub-section (5), for the words "for an amount not exceeding one thousand rupees or twenty rupees or twenty percentum of the goods, whichever is greater", the words "for an amount not less than ten per centum and not more than twenty-five percentum of the value of goods" shall be substituted; and (b) in sub-section (6), -(i) after the words "impose on the owner of the goods,", the words "and in case the owner is not forthcoming or his identity is not disclosed by the person in-charge of the goods or the person in-charge of the goods carrier, in which goods are being carried, on the person in-charge of the goods or the goods carrier or the driver" shall be inserted; and (ii) for the existing proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely: -" Provided further that no penalty shall be imposed unless the owner of the goods or his representative or person in-charge of the goods or the goods carrier or the driver has been given a reasonable opportunity of bring heard.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana To sub-section (5) of section 39 of the principal Act, the following further proviso shall be added, namely: -" Provided further than in the case of an appeal against any order, which has to be communicated by the appropriate authority to the assessee, the period of sixty days shall commence from the date of receipt of the copy of the order by the assessee and in the case of an appeal against any other made under this Act, the time spent in obtaining the certified copy of order shall be excluded in computing the period of sixty days.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana To sub-section (1) of section 40 of the principal Act, the following provisos shall be added, namely :-" Provided that no order, shall be so revised after the expiry of a period of eight years from the date of the order: Provided further that aforesaid limitation of period shall not apply where the order in a similar case is revised as a result of the decision of the Tribunal or any court of law.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana To sub-section (1) of section 42 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be added, namely: -" Provided that for the purposes of calculating the period of sixty days the period spent in obtaining the copy of the order shall be excluded.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 7--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (3) of section 43 of the principal Act, for the existing proviso, the following provisos shall be substituted, namely: -" Provided that where the amount not so refunded, does not exceed five hundred rupees, the interest payable thereon shall not exceed the amount not so refunded; Provided further that for the purpose of calculation of interest, a period of fifteen days or more shall be deemed to be one month and amount of fifty rupees or more shall be deemed to be one hundred rupees and a period of less than fifteen days an amount of less than fifty rupees shall be ignored.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 8--> State(s): Haryana In section 54 of the principal Act, for the words, "require any person, including a banking company, post office or any officer thereof to furnish any information or statement useful for, or relevant to any proceedings under this Act" the words "require any dealer or person including a banking company, post office, or any officer thereof to furnish any information or statistics useful for or relevant to any proceedings under or for the administration of this Act." Shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 9--> State(s): Haryana For sub-section (2) of section 57 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely: -"(2) In this section an 'Income-tax Practitioner' means any person who before the 10th day of May, 1953, appeared before any assessing or other sales tax authorities is connection with any proceedings under this Act or under the Punjab General Sales Tax, 1948, or the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1941, on behalf of any assessee otherwise than in the capacity of an employee or relative of that assessee, or a retired gazetted officer of the Haryana Excise and Taxation Department who has worked as an assessing authority or an appellate authority or a revisional authority under the aforesaid Acts, for a minimum period or a revisional authority under the aforesaid Acts, for a minimum period of five years in one or more than one of the aforesaid capacities after a period of two years has passed since the date of his retirement, or who passed any accountancy examination recognised in this behalf, by the Cental Board of Revenue, or holds a degree in Commerce, law, Economics or Banking including auditing conferred by any Indian University, English and Welsh Universities.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 10--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (1) of section 59 of the principal Act, for the second proviso, the following provisos shall be substituted, namely: - "Provided further that where the amount of tax or penalty, not paid or paid less, does not exceed five hundred rupees, the interest payable thereon shall not exceed the amount of tax or penalty not so paid or paid less subject to the minimum of ten rupees: Provided further that for the purposes of calculation of interest, a period of fifteen days or more shall be deemed to be one month and an amount of fifty rupees or more shall be deemed to be one hundred rupees and a period of less than fifteen days and an amount of less than fifty rupees shall be ignored.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 11--> State(s): Haryana In entry 14 of Schedule B to the principal Act, after the words "woollen durrees and cotton floor durrees", the words "and dryer felts whether made from cotton, wool or man-made fibre" shall be deemed to have been added for the period commencing on the 26th day of April, 1971, and ending with the 6th day of September, 1978.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1981_Section 12--> State(s): Haryana Notwithstanding any judgement, decree or order of any court or other authority to the contrary, any levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of any amount by way of tax or penalty made or purporting to have been made in respect of dyer felts, whether made from cotton, wool or man-made fibre, for the period commencing on the 26th day of April, 1971, and ending with the 6th day of September, 1978, and any action taken or thing done or purporting to have been taken or done in relation to such levy, assessment, reassessment or collection shall be deemed to be as valid and effective as if such levy, assessment, reassessment or collection had been made or action taken or things done under the principal Act, and accordingly-(a) all acts, proceedings or things done or action taken by the State Government or by any officer of the State Government or by any authority, in connection with the levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of such a tax or penalty shall, for all purposes by deemed to be, and to have always been, done or taken in accordance with law; (b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or before any authority for the refund of any such tax or penalty so collected; and (c) no court or authority shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such tax or penalty is collected.
