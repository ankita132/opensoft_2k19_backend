Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973.BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment0 Act, 1978.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana For clause (a) of subsection (1) of section 15 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), the following clause shall be substituted namely: - "(a) twenty paise in a rupee in the case of liquor (foreign liquor and Indian made foreign liquor specified at serial number 25 of Schedule A and ten paise in a rupee in the case of other goods specified therein; and:.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (1) of section 16 of the principal Act, for the words "fifteen", the word "two" shall be substituted with effect from the 1st day of April, 1978.
