Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Forty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1992.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana For section 15A of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, the following section shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 27th day of May, 1971, namely: -"
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 15A--> State(s): Haryana (i) the tax leviable under this Act or the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, on the sale of goods by a dealer manufactured by him, shall be reduced by the amount of tax paid in the State on the sale or purchase of goods, other than paddy, Cotton and oil seeds, used in their manufacture; and (ii) when no tax is leviable on the sale of manufactured goods except those specified in Schedule B, subject to the conditions and expectations specified therein, or when the tax leviable on the sale of manufactured goods is less than the paid on the State in the sale or purchase of goods, other than paddy, cotton and oil seeds, used in their manufacture, the full amount of tax paid or the excess amount of tax paid over the tax leviable on sale,as the case may be, shall be refundable if the manufactured goods are sold in the State or in the course of inter-State trade or commerce or in the course of export out of the territory of India: Provided that in case the manufactured goods have been sold before the 1st day of January, 1988, the tax paid on goods, leviable to tax at the first stage of sale under section 18, used in their manufacture, shall not be refunded.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana The Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment)Ordinance, 1992 (Haryana Ordinance No. 1 of 1992), is hereby repealed. T.P.GARG, Secretary to Government, Haryana,
