Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Forty-eighth year of Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana For clause (ii), of section 15A of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, following clause shall be substituted, namely: -"(ii) the tax paid in the State on the sale or purchase of goods used in manufacture, other than the tax paid on the last purchase of paddy, cotton and oil seeds, shall be refundable if and only if the manufactured goods are other than those specified in Schedule B, subject to the conditions and restrictions specified therein and are sold to a registered dealer in the State against prescribed declaration and are leviable to tax at last stage of purchase or sale or are sold in the course of export out of the territory of India within the meaning of section 5 of the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956."
