Evacuee Interest (Separation) Second Supplementary Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to supplement the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 by applying in the [Principal Territories]1 the amendments made in that Act by the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960. Be it enacted by the Legislature or the State of Punjab in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows Substituted for "State of Punjab" vide Haryana Adoption of Laws Order 1968.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Second Supplementary Act, 1960_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana (1) This Act may be called the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Second Supplementary Act, 1960.1 (2) It extends to the whole of the [Principal Territories]2 (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the date appointed by the Central Government under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960. Received the assent to the Governor of Punjab on the 28th November, 1960, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette Extraordinary dated the 28th November, 1960. Substituted for "State of Punjab" vide Haryana Adoption of Laws Order 1968.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Second Supplementary Act, 1960_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana The amendments made to the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951, by the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960, shall, so far they relate to any matter enumerated in List II of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution, be as valid in the [Principal Territories]1 as if the provisions contained therein had been enacted by the Legislature of the State. Substituted for "State of Punjab" vide Haryana Adoption of Laws Order 1968.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Second Supplementary Act, 1960_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana (1) The Evacuee Interest (Separation) Second Supplementary Ordinance, 1960 (Punjab Ordinance No. 5 of 1960), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Second Supplementary Ordinance, 1960, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act as if this Act had commenced on the 29th September, 1960.
