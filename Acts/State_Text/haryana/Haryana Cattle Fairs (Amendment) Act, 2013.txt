Haryana Cattle Fairs (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act further to amend the Haryana Cattle Fairs Act, 1970. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows : -
Haryana Cattle Fairs (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana Cattle Fairs (Amendment) Act, 2013. Amendment of section 2 of Haryana Act 30 of 1970.
Haryana Cattle Fairs (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In clause (b) of section 2 of the Haryana Cattle Fairs Act, 1970, the word and sign "elephant," shall be omitted. (Raj Rahul Garg), Secretary to Government Haryana, Law and Legislative Department.
