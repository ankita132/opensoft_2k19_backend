East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Haryana Validation Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to validate certain schemes made under the East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Act, 1948. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Twenty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Haryana Validation Act, 1971_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Haryana Validation Act, 1971.1 Received the assent of the President of India on the 27th July, 1971.
East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Haryana Validation Act, 1971_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any court or other authority to the contrary, where, in any scheme made at any time before the commencement of this Act, land has been assigned or reserved for a common purpose as defined in clause (bb) of Section 2 of the East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Act, 1948, in accordance with the executive instructions issued by the State Government from time to time, such scheme shall be, and shall always be deemed to have been, valid and no such scheme shall be questioned merely on the ground that the scale for such assignment or reservation had not been laid down by rules made under the East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Act, 1948.
