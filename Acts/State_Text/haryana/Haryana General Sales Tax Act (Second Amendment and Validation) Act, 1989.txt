Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Fortieth Year of the republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989.
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In the second proviso to sub-section (2) of section 6 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the principal Act) after clause (f), the following clause shall be inserted, namely, -"(g) who is a contractor doing the work of construction, fitting, improvement or repair of any building, road, wall, bride, embankment, dam or other immovable property and has not charged tax has not made use of the authority of his registration certificate under this Act or the Central Sales Tax Act 1956, during the period from first day of April, 1987 to 31 st day of March, 1989, shall not be liable to pay tax under this Act during aforesaid period on the goods involved in the execution of works contract.".
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In clause (f) of section 7 of the principal Act, for the figures and word "1,00,000 rupees", the figures and word "2,00,000 rupees" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana In section 13B of the principal Act, the word "sales" shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana In section 28C of the principal Act, for the words "three lakh rupees", the words "five lakh rupees" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana For the first proviso to sub-section (2) of section 36, of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely: -" Provided that when the dealer contravenced the provisions of clause (a) or clause (d), he shall be liable to pay a penalty of rupees five hundred to be imposed by an officer referred to in sub-section (1):",
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 7--> State(s): Haryana For sub-section (4) of section 37 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely: -"(4) The owner of person incharge of the goods of goods carrier, entering or leaving the limits of the State, shall furnish a copy of goods receipt, documents as referred to in sub-section (2), or a declaration containing such particulars, in such form obtained from such authority, in the manner as may be prescribed, of the goods carried by him or in such carrier, as the case may be, before the officer-in-charge of the check-post or barrier and shall produce the copy of the said goods receipt, documents or declaration duly verified and returned to him by the officer-in-charge of the check-post or barrier before any other Officer as mentioned in sub-section (20.".
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 8--> State(s): Haryana Proviso to sub-section (1) of section 39 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 9--> State(s): Haryana In section 42 of the principal Act, -(a) in sub-section (1), for the word and letter "section 39", the words and letter "sections 39 and 41" shall be substituted; and (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "thirty days", the words "ninety days" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 10--> State(s): Haryana In Schedule B to the principal Act, -(a) in entry 14,word and sign "blankets," shall be omitted; and (b) in entry 39, for the word "excluding", the word "including" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax Act (Second Amendment and Validation) Act, 1989_Section 11--> State(s): Haryana Notwithstanding any judgement, decree or order of any court of Tribunal or other authority to the contrary or the fact that a contractor has not charged or has not charged or has not made use of the authority of his registration certificate under this Act or the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, in respect of the goods involved in execution of works contract during the period from the 1 st day of April, 1987, to the 31 st day of March, 1989, any levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of any amount by way of tax or penalty made or purported to have been made and any action taken or done during the aforesaid period in relation to such levy, assessment, reassessment or collection under the provisions to the principal Act, shall be deemed to be valid and effective as if such levy, assessment, reassessment or collection had been made or action taken or things done under the principal Act and shall not be called in question in any Court or Tribunal or other authority and accordingly, -(a) all acts, proceedings, or things done or action taken by the State Government or by any officer of the State Government or by any authority in connection with the levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of such tax or penalty shall for all purposes be deemed to be, and to have always been done or taken in accordance with law; (b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or before any authority for the refund of any such tax or penalty so collected; and (c) no court or authority shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such tax or penalty so collected.
