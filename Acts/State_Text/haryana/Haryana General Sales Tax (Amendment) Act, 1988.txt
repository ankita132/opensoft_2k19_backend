Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1988.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In clause (d) of section 7 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the principal Act),-(i) the words "halwai shop, bakeri," shall be omitted and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the 1st day of January, 1988; and (ii) for the figures and word "2,00,000 rupees", the figures and word "1,00,000 rupees" shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 1st day of January, 1988.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana Clause (vii) of the proviso to sub-section (1) of section 15 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana In the third proviso to sub-section (4) of section 37 of the principal Act, for the words "for any place inside the State", the words "from a place inside the State to any other place inside the State" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana For the proviso to sub-section (1) of section 39 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely: - " Provided that appeal shall lie to the Tribunal only where the caseinvolves question of law, or when the subject-matter or appeal involves a sum exceeding five thousand rupees."
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana In Schedule B to the principal Act,-(i) in entry 24-A, the figures "1987-88" shall be omitted ; (ii) for entry 27, the following entry shall be substituted,namely: -" 27. (a) Fertilizers excluding oil cakes and Department-oiled cakes; and (b) Bones, crushed bones, bone meals, bone powder, bone grists and bone sinews. Note.- Sub-entry shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 5th day of May, 1973."; (iii) after entry 30, the following entry shall be inserted and shall deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 1st day of January, 1988, namely: -" 30A. Articles ordinarily prepared When sold by halwais by halwais exclusively."; (iv) after entry 39, the following entry shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 1st day of January, 1988, namely: -" 40. Bakery goods prepared without When sold, otherwise than in using power at any stage. containers and packers by bakers dealing exclusively in such goods."; and (v) after entry 44, the following entry shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 1st day of January, 1988, namely: -" 45. Indian food preparations ordinarily When sold by persons running prepared by tandoorwalas, lohwalas tandoors, doors, lohs and And dhabawalas. dhabas exclusively".
