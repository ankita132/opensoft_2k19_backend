Haryana Canal and Drainage (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act further to amend the Haryana Canal and Drainage Act, 1974. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Sixty- fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Haryana Canal and Drainage (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana Canal and Drainage (Amendment) Act, 2013.
Haryana Canal and Drainage (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In section 2 of the Haryana Canal and Drainage Act, 1974 (hereinafter called the principal Act),- (i) after clause (9), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(9A) 'sewage' means effluent from any sewerage system and includes sullage from open drainage;"; and (ii) after clause (12), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(12A) 'trade effluent' includes any liquid, gaseous or solid substance, which is discharged from any premises used for carrying out any industrial operation or process or treatment and disposal system, other than domestic sewage;".
Haryana Canal and Drainage (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In clause (b) of section 5 of the principal Act, the word "unlined" shall be omitted.
Haryana Canal and Drainage (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana In section 58 of the principal Act,- (i) after clause (j), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(k) discharges any sewage or trade effluent into a Canal.": (ii) for the existing para after clause (k). the following para shall be substituted, namely:- "shall, in respect of offences under clauses (a), (b), (c), (g) and (k) above, be liable on conviction to a fine not exceeding five thousand rupees or imprisonment not exceeding six months or both and in case of continuing offence/contravention, with an additional fine which may extend to five hundred rupees for every subsequent day. In respect of other offences, the offender shaft be liable on conviction to a fine not exceeding one thousand rupees or imprisonment not exceeding one month, or both.". (Raj Rahul Garg), Secretary to Government Haryana, Law and Legislative Department.
