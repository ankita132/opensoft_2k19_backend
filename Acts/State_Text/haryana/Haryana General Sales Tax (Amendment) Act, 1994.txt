Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Fortyfifth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana To the second proviso to sub-section (I) of section 6 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, after clause (g), the following clause shall be added and shall be deemed to have been added with effect from the Ist day of April, 1987, namely :- "(h) who transfer the right to use tents, kanats, chholdari, crockery, utensils, furniture and all other goods for decoration and lighting purpose, and has not charged tax or has not made use of the authority of his registration certificate under this Act, or the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, during the period from Ist day of April, 1987 to 31 st day of March, 1989 and opts for the payment of lump sum as may be prescribed, in lieu of sales tax ; shall not be liable to pay tax under this Act during the aforesaid period and his liability to pay tax under this Act on the transfer of right to use any goods for cash, deferred payment or other valuable consideration shall commence from Ist day of April, 1989."
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana The Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance 1993 (Haryana Ordinance No. 1 of 1993), is hereby repealed. I.P.GARG Secretary to Government, Haryana,
