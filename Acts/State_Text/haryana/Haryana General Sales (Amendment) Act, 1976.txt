Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Twenty-seventh Year of Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976.
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In section 2 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), -(i) to clause(c), the following proviso shall be added, namely: -" Provided that a person or a member of his family who sells within the State inclusively the agricultural produce grown by himself on any land in which he has an interest whether as owner, unsufructuary mortgages, tenant or otherwise, shall not be deemed to be a dealer."; (ii) in clause (e), the words "or in the course of export out of the territory of India" shall be omitted; and (iii) after clause (m), the following clause shall be inserted, namely: -"(mm) 'silk fabrics' mean all varieties of fabrics manufactured either wholly or partly from silk and includes embroidery in pieces, in strips or in ; but does not include any fabrics on which duty of excise is leviable under the Additional Duties of Excise (Goods of special Importance) Act, 1957 (Parliament Act 58 of 1957);".
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In sub-clause (ii) of clause (a) of sub-section (1) of section 9 of the principal Act, the words "or in the course of export out of territory of India" shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana After sub-section (6) of section 19 of principal Act, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely: -
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana Clause (iii) of section 24 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (1) of section 27 of the principal Act, -(i) sub-clause (iii) of clause (a) shall be omitted; (ii) sub-clause (iii) of clause (b) shall be omitted; and (iii) sub-clause (ii) of clause (c) shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 7--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (6) of section 36 of principal Act, -(i) for the words "a penalty not exceeding two thousand rupees or twenty per cent" shall be substituted; and (ii) the words "whichever is greater" shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 8--> State(s): Haryana For sub-section (6) of section 37 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely: - "(6) The officer detaining the goods shall record the statement, if any, given by the owner of the goods or his representative or the driver or other person in-charge of the goods carrier and shall require him to produce proper and genuine documents as referred to in sub-section (2) or sub-section (4), as the case may be. Is, after the enquiry, such officer finds that there has been an attempt to evade the tax due under this Act, he shall, by order, impose on the owner of the goods penalty of not less than ten per cent and not more than twenty-five per-cent of the value of the goods, and in case he finds otherwise, he shall order the release of the goods: Provided that no penalty shall be imposed unless the owner of the goods has been given a reasonable opportunity of being heard.".
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 9--> State(s): Haryana For section 44 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have always been substituted, namely: -" 44.	Power to withhold refund-(1) where an order giving rise to a refund is the subject-matter of an appeal or further proceedings or where any other proceedings under this Act are pending, and the assessing authority is of the opinion that the grant of the refund is likely to adversely affect the recovery, the assessing authority may withhold the refund and refer the case to the Commissioner for order. The orders passed by the Commissioner shall be final. (2) The period during which the refund remains so withheld shall be excluded for the purpose of calculation of interest under section 43.".
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 10--> State(s): Haryana In section 50 the principal Act, for the words and figures "leviable under section 9 of this Act in respect of such goods" the words "that would have been payable under this Act, if such goods had not been purchased on the strength of the registration certificate" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 11--> State(s): Haryana After section 50 of the principal Act the following section shall be inserted, namely: -" 59A. Sugar cane"; and (c) serial number 25 and the entry thereagainst shall be omitted.
