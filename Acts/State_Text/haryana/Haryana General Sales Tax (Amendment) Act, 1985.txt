Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1985.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In section 7 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, (hereinafter called the principal Act), -(a) in clause (c), for the word and figure "25,000 rupees", the word and figure "1,00,000 rupees" shall be substituted; and (b) in clause (d), the words "Tandoor, Loh, Dhaba," shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana After entry 44 of Schedule B of the principal Act, the following entry shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 18 th of April, 1984, namely: - "45. Indian food preparations ordinarily prepared by tandoorwalas, lohwalas and dhabawalas. When sold by persons running tandoors, lohs and dhabas exclusively.". SHIV CHARAN DAS BAJAJ, Secretary to Government, Haryana,
