Haryana General Sales Tax (Second amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty fourth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax ( Second amendment ) Act,1983
Haryana General Sales Tax (Second amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (I) of section 15 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 - (i) in clause (a) , for the words " ten paise" , the words " twelve paise" shall be substituted ; and (ii) in clause (b) for the words " seven paise" , the words " eight paise" shall be substituted. SHIV CHARAN DAS BAJAJ, Secretary to Government of Haryana
