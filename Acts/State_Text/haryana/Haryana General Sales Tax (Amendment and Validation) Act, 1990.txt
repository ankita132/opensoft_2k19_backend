Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana (1) This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the principal Act), after the words, brackets, letter and figures "clause(e) of section 2 27-5-1971", the following words, brackets, letters and figures shall be inserted, namely: -" Clause (p) of section 2 27-5-1971 Section 6 27-5-1971".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In section 2 of the principal Act, in clause (p), -(i) in Explanation (2), the words and figures "but the purchase value of the goods liable to tax under section 9 or section 24 shall be included" shall be omitted and shall be omitted and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the 27 th day of May, 1971; (ii) in Explanation (3), for the word "tax", the words "tax under this Act" shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 5 th day of May, 1973; (iii) after Explanation (5), the following shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 31 st day of March, 1983, namely: - "(6) The purchase of barley or of goods used in the manufacture of guar gum, scientific goods, utensils and metal handicrafts shall not form part of the turnover of a dealer for the period he is entitled to purchase the goods on the authority of his certificate of registration without payment of sales tax under section 24; provided these are exclusively for the specified purposes.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana In section 6 of the principal Act, -(i) for sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 27 th day of May, 1971, namely: -"(1) Every dealer whose gross turnover during the year immediately preceding the coming into force of the provisions of this section exceeded the taxable quantum shall be liable to pay tax on all sales and purchases effected after the coming into force of the provisions of this section: Provided that this sub-section shall not apply to a dealer who dealts exclusively in goods specified in Schedule B."; (ii) in sub-section (2), for the words, brackets, figure and sign "Every dealer to whom sub-section (1) does not apply shall, subject to other provisions of this Act be liable to pay tax under this Act on the expiry of thirty days after the date on which his gross turnover during any year first exceeds the taxable quantum:", the words, brackets, figure and sign "Every dealer, to whom sub-section (1) does not apply shall be liable to pay tax on all sales and purchases effected on the expiry of thirty days after the date on which his gross turnover during any year first exceeds the taxable quantum:" shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been sub-substituted with effect from the 27 th day of May, 1971; and (iii) Explanation to sub-sections (1) and (2) shall be omitted and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the 27 th day of May, 1971.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana After section 15 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 27 th day of May, 1971, namely: -" 15A. Adjustment and refund of tax - (1) The amount of tax paid or payable by a dealer under this Act on purchase of any Goods, except those specified in Schedule C or Schedule D, shall be adjustable, in the manner and subject to such conditions as may prescribed, from the tax payable in the State under this Act or the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, on the sale of goods manufactured therefrom. (2) when no tax is payable in the State under this Act or the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, by a dealer manufacturing any goods in the State on the sale of such goods (except those specified in Schedule B), made from within the State, the full amount of tax under this Act on the purchase of goods except those specified in Schedule C or Schedule D which are use in the manner and subject to such goods, shall be refundable, in the manner and subject to such conditions as may be prescribed, to the registered dealer making the payment: Provided that no refund shall become due on transactions made before the 1st day of January, 1988, except on sales made to registered dealers.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana For section 17 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 5th day of May, 1973, namely: -" 17. Tax on declared goods - Tax on declared goods shall be leviable and payable at the stage of sale or purchase, as the case may be, and under the circumstances specified against such goods in Schedule D: Provided that where the goods have not been subjected to tax at any of the stages of sale or purchase in Schedule D, the tax shall be levied on and paid by a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act at the state of last purchase of such goods by him: Provided further that the tax under this section shall be levied, charged and paid after proving deduction admissible under section 27 of this Act.".

Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section 8--> State(s): Haryana To sub-section (4) of section 37 of the principal Act, the following provisos shall be added and shall be deemed to have been added with effect from the 1 st day of October, 1989, namely: -" Provided that where the owner or person incharge of the goods or the driver or other person incharge of the goods carrier bound for any place out side the State passes through the State, such owner or person incharge of the goods or the driver or other person incharge of such carrier shall in the prescribed manner furnish, in duplicate, to the officer incharge of the Check post or barrier of his entry into the State, a declaration in the prescribed form and obtain from him a copy thereof duly verified. The owner or person incharge of the goods carrier or the goods carrier shall deliver within twenty-four hours the said copy to the officer incharge of the check post or barrier at the point of his exit from the State, failing which he shall be liable to pay a penalty, to be imposed by the officer incharge of the check post or barrier of the entry, not exceeding two hundred two thousand rupees or twenty per centum of the value of the goods, whichever is greater: Provided further that no that no penalty shall e imposed unless the person concerned has been given a reasonable opportunity of being heard: Provided further that where the owner or person incharge of the goods or the driver or other person incharge of the goods or carrier bound from A place inside the State to any other place inside the State has to pass through another State, such owner or person or the driver or other person shall furnish, in duplicate, to the officer incharge of the check post or barrier of his exit from the State, a declaration in the prescribed form and obtain from him a copy thereof duly received and shall deliver the same to the officer incharge of the check post or barrier of his entry into the State, within four hours of his exit from the previous barrier or check post in the State, falling which he shall be liable to pay a penalty to be imposed by the officer incharge of the check post or barrier of his exit, not exceeding two thousand rupees or twenty is greater, unless he explains the time taken in excess to the satisfaction of the officer incharge of the exit barrier or check post.".

Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990_Section 14--> State(s): Haryana Notwithstanding any judgement, decree or order of any court or tribunal or other authority to the contrary, any levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of any amount by way of tax made or purporting to have been made and any action taken or things done or purporting to have been or done before the commencement of the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990, in relation to such levy, assessment, re-assessment or collection made under the provisions of section 9 valid and effective as if such levy, assessment, re-assessment or collection has been made or action taken or things done under the provisions of clause (p) of section 2, section 6, section 15A, section 17, section 27 and Schedule D appended to this Act and as amended by the provisions of the Haryana General Sales Tax Amendment and Validation) Act, 1990 and shall not be called in question in any court or tribunal or other authority and accordingly-(i) all acts, proceedings or things done or action taken by the State Government or by any officer of the State Government or by any authority, in connection with the levy, assessment, reassessment or collection of such a tax shall, for all purposes be deemed to be, and to have always been done or taken in accordance with law; (ii) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or before any authority for the refund or any such tax so collected; and (iii) no court or authority shall enforce a decree or order directing the refund of any such tax so collected.
