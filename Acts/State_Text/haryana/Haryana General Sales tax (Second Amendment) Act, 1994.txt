Haryana General Sales tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Forty fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Haryana General Sales tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1994.
