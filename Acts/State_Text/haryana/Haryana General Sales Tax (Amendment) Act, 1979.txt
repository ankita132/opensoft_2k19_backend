Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 , BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India, as follows: -
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In section 7 of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act)m-(i) in clause(c), for the "figure and word "10,000 rupees", the figure and word "25,000 rupees" shall be substituted; and (ii) in clause(d), for the figure and word "25,000 rupees", the figure and word "40,000 rupees" shall be substituted; and (iii) in clause (f) , for the figure and word "40,000 rupees", the figure and word "1,00,000 rupees" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In section 9 of the principal Act, for sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely: -"(1) Where a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act, -(a) purchases goods, other than those specified in Schedule B from any source in the State and uses them in the State in the manufacture of goods specified in Schedule B; or(b) purchases goods, other than those specified in Schedule Bexcept milk, from any source in the State and uses them in the State in the manufacture of any other goods and disposes of the manufactured goods in any manner other wise than by way of sale whether within the State or in the course of inter-State trade or commerce or in the course of export out of the territory of India within the meaning of sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956; or (c) purchases goods, other than those specified in Schedule B,from any source in the State and exports them, in the circumstances in which no tax is payable under any other provision of this Act, there shall be levied, subject to the provisions of section 17, a tax on the purchase of such goods at such rate as may be notified under section 15".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana in section 13 of the principal Act, -(a) in sub-section (1), -(i) for the words "cottage industries", the words "cottage industries or rural tiny industries" shall be substituted;and (ii) for the words "co-operative societies", the words "co-operative societies, rural tiny industries units" shall be substituted;(a) in sub-section (2), for the words "such societies" occurring twice, the words "such societies, industries units" shall be substituted; and "Explanation. - For the purposes of this section, "rural tiny industrial unit" means an industrial unit set up in a rural area, after coming into force of the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979, whose capital investment on machinery and equipment does not exceed one lakh rupees.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana In sub-sections (4) and (5) of section 28 of the principal Act, -(a) for the words "five", the word "three" shall be substituted; and (b) the words "proceed to" shall be omitted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana After entry 33 of Schedule A to the principal Act, the following entry shall be inserted, namely: -" 34 Cement.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 7--> State(s): Haryana In Schedule B to the Principal Act, -(a) in entry 2, for the words "condensed and dried milk", the words and figure "milk liable to tax under section 9 and condensed and dried milk" shall be substituted; and (b) entry 30A shall be omitted. K.D.JAUHAR, Under Secretary to Government, Haryana
