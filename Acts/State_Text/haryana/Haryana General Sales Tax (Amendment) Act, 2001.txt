Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana An Act further to amend the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Fifty-second year of the Republic of India as follows :--
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana This Act may be called the Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana In Section 13 B of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 (hereinafter called the principal Act), after the words "for such period", the words and sign "either prospectively or retrospectively," shall be inserted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Haryana In clause (a) of Sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the principal Act, for the word "twenty", the words and sign "twenty-five" shall be substituted.
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 4--> State(s): Haryana In section 64 of the principal Act, after Sub-section (2), the following Sub-section shall be inserted, namely :-- "(2A) The power to make rules under Sub-sections (1) and (2) with respect to clauses (ff) and (oo) of Sub-section (2) shall include the power to give retrospective effect to such rules i.e. from the date on which policy for incentives to industry is announced by the State and for this purpose rules 28A, 28B and 28C of the Haryana General Sales Tax Rules, 1975, shall have retrospective effect i.e. with effect from 1 st April, 1988, 1 st August, 1997 and 15 th November, 1999, respectively, but such retrospective operation shall not prejudicially affect the interests of any person to whom such rules may be applicable."
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 5--> State(s): Haryana In Schedule B of the principal Act, -- (i) serial number 24-A and entry thereagainst shall be omitted; and (ii) against serial number 50, for the existing entry, the following entry shall be substituted, namely :-- "All goods except Indian made foreign liquor and foreign liquor sold to the serving military personnel and ex-servicemen by the Canteen Stores Department direct or through the authorised Canteen Contractors or through Unit run canteens.".
Haryana General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 6--> State(s): Haryana In Schedule C of the principal Act, under column 2 and 3, against serial number 2, for the existing entries, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-2 "Indian made foreign liquor and foreign liquor - (i) when sold by L-1B or L-1B1 licence, granted licence under the Punjab Excise Act, 1914 (1 of 1914), as applicable to the State of Haryana. (ii) when sold to L-9 lincensees, granted licence under the Punjab Excise Act, 1914 (1 of 1914), as applicable to the State of Haryana, if not subjected to tax under entry (i) above (iii) when sold circumstances not covered under entries (i) and (ii) above At the stage of sale by L-1B or L-1B1 licensees in the State ; At the stage of sale to L-9 licensee in the State ; At the stage of sale made for the first time in the State of Haryana by an excise licensee. Note :-- If an L-4/L-5 or L-12C licensee under the Punjab Excise Act, 1914 (1 of 1914), purchases liquor on which sales tax has already been paid in the State, then such licensee shall not be liable to pay tax on the sale of such liquor by him.". L.N. MITTAL, Secretary to Government, Haryana,
